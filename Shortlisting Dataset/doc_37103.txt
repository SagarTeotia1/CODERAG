DROI Sakharov 2009 - results of the vote on the 3 finalists On Tuesday, 6 October 2009 at a joint AFET / DEVE meeting the following three nominees were selected as finalists for the Sakharov Prize 2009 (in alphabetical order): Dr Izzeldin ABUELAISH (Palestinian doctor), Mr Dawit ISAAK (Eritrean journalist) and Ms Lyudmila Alexeyeva, Mr Oleg Orlov and Mr Sergei Kovalev on behalf of MEMORIAL and all other human rights defenders in Russia (Organisation promoting fundamental rights in post-Soviet states) . 20091006CAN61941 EP Sakharov webpage
