Answer given by Mr Dimas on behalf of the Commission (6 June 2007) Infringement file in question concerns a failure of Ireland to provide an implementation report in accordance with Article 9 of Directive 2000/53/EC of the Parliament and of the Council of 18.9.2000 on end-of-life vehicles by the date specified OJ L 269, 21.10.2000. (i.e. 21 April 2006). Following a Commission letter of formal notice addressed to Ireland, an implementation report, dated 11 December 2006, was communicated to the Commission on 13 December 2006. This communication will allow the Commission to close the infringement file.
