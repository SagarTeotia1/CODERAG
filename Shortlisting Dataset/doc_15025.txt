WRITTEN QUESTION E-5170/08 by Georgios Georgiou (IND/DEM) to the Commission (30 September 2008) Subject: Drought in Greece Greece is facing a severe drought problem, despite the heavy rain that has fallen in the country in the past years. Water reserves for the Greek capital (and its five million inhabitants) are at their lowest for six years, falling below the level recorded in 2002. What projects (dams, desalination plants, etc.) has the EU co-financed in Greece in the past six years, and what was their total cost? Did the Greek authorities submit co-financing applications for such projects in 2008?
