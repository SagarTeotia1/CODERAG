Answer given by Ms Ferrero-Waldner on behalf of the Commission (4 February 2010) Taiwan is a full member of the WTO, under the official name of ‘Separate Customs Territory of Taiwan, Penghu, Kinmen and Matsu (Chinese Taipei)’ and as such able to participate at all levels and in all activities of the WTO. Over the past 19 years the Commission has developed an excellent bilateral, trade-related relationship with Taiwan, guided by a spirit of mutual interest and cooperation. The Commission has been cooperating with Taiwan on several issues also in the WTO, notably related to the efforts for an early conclusion of the negotiations on the Doha Development Agenda (DDA).
