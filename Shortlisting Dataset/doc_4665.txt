Question for written answer E-007556/2011 to the Commission Rule 117 Jim Higgins (PPE) (1 August 2011) Subject: Harmonisation of drug driving laws Could the Commission please outline what measures (if any) it intends to adopt in order to combat drug driving across the European Union? Is there any intention to harmonise the laws in this area? Does it intend to allocate funding to research into more reliable drug detection methods?
