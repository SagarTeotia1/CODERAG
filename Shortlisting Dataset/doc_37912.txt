The Commission also publishes a monthly monitoring report on the EU employment situation and social outlook and presented in July 2010 a toolkit http://ec.europa.eu/social/main.jsp?catId=103&langId=en on good practice in socially responsible restructuring for the use of stakeholders. In addition, as announced in the ‘Industrial Policy’ Flagship Initiative, the Commission will launch a consultation of the European social partners on a European framework for Restructuring in the course of 2011 Communication from the Commission ‘An Integrated Industrial Policy for the Globalisation Era — Putting Competitiveness and Sustainability at Centre Stage’. COM(2010)614 final. . The Commission would point out that workers likely to be affected by restructuring may qualify for support from the European Social Fund and, under certain conditions, from the European Globalisation Adjustment Fund (EGF). To date, no request has been received from the Portuguese authorities for support from the EGF for the workers made redundant by the Delphi factory in Guarda. The Commission would invite the Honourable Member to contact the EGF Contact Person in Portugal for more information on whether an application is being planned http://ec.europa.eu/social/main.jsp?catId=581&langId=pt .
