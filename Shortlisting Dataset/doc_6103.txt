EXPLANATORY STATEMENT Introduction This proposal concerns the protocol which was initialled 30 May 2001 between the republic of Guinea-Bissau and the European Union establishing the fishing possibilities and the corresponding financial compensation for a 5 year period from 16 June 2001 to 15 June 2006. On a provisional basis fishing activities continue while the European Union, pursuant to the text of the protocol, commits itself to pay the first annual instalment before 15 January 2002. The agreement with Guinea-Bissau is one of the oldest the EU has concluded with a third country with the first protocol dating back to 1980.
