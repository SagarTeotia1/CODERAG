ORAL QUESTION WITH DEBATE O-0050/03 pursuant to Rule 42 of the Rules of Procedure by Caroline Jackson , on behalf of the Committee on the Environment, Public Health and Consumer Policy to the Council Subject: Interim progress report on the EU's contribution to the WSSD Plan of Implementation Could the Council give a detailed progress report on its commitments made at the Johannesburg summit last September, and specifically in the field of water and energy? Will the Council please report back on the 22nd session of the UNEP Governing Council/Global Ministerial Environment Forum in Nairobi in February 2003, with specific reference to issues related to global environmental governance? How will the Council make sure, that ‘sustainable development becomes a major and regular aspect of forthcoming Spring Councils’, as demanded by the European Parliament in its Spring Council resolution on 12.2.2003?
