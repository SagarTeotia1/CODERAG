PROCEDURAL PAGE By letter of 26 February 2004 the Council requested Parliament's assent, under Article 300(3), second subparagraph of the EC Treaty, on the proposal for a Council decision on the position of the European Community on the draft Regulation of the United Nations Economic Commission for Europe concerning the adoption of uniform technical prescriptions relating to the protection of motor vehicles against unauthorised use ( COM(2003) 632 – 5048/2004 – 2003/0248(AVC)). At the sitting of 8 March 2004 the President of Parliament announced that he had referred the proposal to the Committee on Industry, External Trade, Research and Energy as the committee responsible and the Committee on Regional Policy, Transport and Tourism for its opinion (C5‑0105/2004). At its meeting of 5 April 2004, the Committee on Industry, External Trade, Research and Energy decided, on a proposal from the chairman, to apply the simplified procedure under Rule 158(1), and the chairman was thus appointed rapporteur. The following were present for the decision on the chairman’s proposal: Gordon J. Adam, Danielle Auroi, María del Pilar Ayuso González, Luis Berenguer Fuster, Ward Beysen, Felipe Camisón Asensio, Gérard Caudron, Marie-Hélène Descamps, Marie-Françoise Duthu, Francesco Fiori, Colette Flesch, Méropi Kaldi, Helmut Kuhne, Peter Liese, Rolf Linkohr, Eryl Margaret McNally, Peter Michael Mombaur, Angelika Niebler, Reino Paasilinna, John Purvis, Godelieve Quisthoudt-Rowohl, Bernhard Rapkay, Mechtild Rothe, Christian Foldberg Rovsing, Paul Rübig, Esko Olavi Seppänen, Jaime Valdivielso de Cué, Alejo Vidal-Quadras Roca, Olga Zrihen Zaari. The Committee on Regional Policy, Transport and Tourism decided on 25 November 2003 not to deliver and opinion. The recommendation was tabled on 6 April 2004.
