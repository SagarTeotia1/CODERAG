Joint answer given by Mr Dimas on behalf of the Commission Written questions : E-1208/06 , E-1209/06 , E-1211/06 , E-1212/06 , E-1213/06 , E-1215/06 , E-1216/06 , E-1210/06 , E-1214/06 (5 May 2006) In response to the abovementioned questions, the Commission can inform the Honourable Member that the municipalities of Ancona, Carrara, Livorno, Massa, Pistoia, Prato and Siena did not submit any project for co-financing under the LIFE III programme from 2004. This includes the 2006 selection exercise. The municipality of Firenze submitted one project (ref. LIFE06 ENV/IT/000333) for co-financing under the LIFE III programme in 2005, for which evaluation is underway and should be finalised by July 2006. It did not submit any project in the other years. The municipality of Pisa submitted one project (ref. LIFE06 ENV/IT/000259) for co-financing under the LIFE III programme in 2005, for which evaluation is underway and should be finalised by July 2006. It did not submit any project in the other years. During the 2003 exercise, the municipality of Prato submitted one project (ref. LIFE03 ENV/IT/000333), which was not financed, however.
