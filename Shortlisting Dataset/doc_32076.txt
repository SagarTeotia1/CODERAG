WRITTEN QUESTION E-2952/07 by Robert Kilroy-Silk (NI) to the Commission (14 June 2007) Subject: Threats to Christians in Pakistan 1. What representation has the Commission made to the Government of Pakistan about the Apostasy Bill? 2. What pressure has the Commission exerted against the Government of Pakistan to provide effective protection for the Christians in Charsadda in the North-West Frontier Province who are being threatened by Islamic extremists that they will be attacked if they do not convert to Islam?
