March 2001 ( A5-0109/2001 ). At the sitting of 4 April 2001 Parliament adopted a resolution postponing the discharge decision in respect of implementation of the budget of the sixth, seventh and eighth European Development Funds for the 1999 financial year. By letter of 9 July 2001 the Commission transmitted the audit report on the management, monitoring and control arrangements governing the use of counterpart funds and budgetary support in development aid. At its meeting of 10 July 2001 the Committee on Budgetary Control confirmed the appointment of Heide Rühle as rapporteur. At its meetings of 10 July 2001, 10 September 2001 and 10 October 2001 the committee examined the draft report. At the last of these meetings it adopted: 1. the proposal for a decision on the discharge in respect of the implementation of the budget of the sixth, seventh and eighth European Development Funds for the 1999 financial year unanimously. 2. the proposal for a decision closing the accounts of the sixth, seventh and eighth European Development Funds for the 1999 financial year unanimously; 3. the motion for a resolution containing the comments which form part of the decision on the discharge in respect of implementation of the budget of the sixth, seventh and eighth European Development Funds for the 1999 financial year unanimously. The following were present for the vote: Diemut R. Theato, chairman; Heide Rühle, rapporteur; Mogens N.J. Camre (for Isabelle Caullery), Paulo Casaca (for Michiel van Hulten), Christos Folias (for Raffaele Costa), Helmut Kuhne, John Joseph McCartin (for Christopher Heaton-Harris), Eluned Morgan, Jan Mulder (for Antonio Di Pietro), José Javier Pomés Ruiz, Francisca Sauquillo Pérez del Arco (for Freddy Blak), Michel Ange Scarbonchi and Gabriele Stauner. The explanatory statement is attached. The second report was tabled on 11 October 2001. The deadline for tabling amendments will be indicated in the draft agenda for the relevant part-session.
