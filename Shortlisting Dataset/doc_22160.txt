WRITTEN QUESTION E-1242/07 by Robert Kilroy-Silk (NI) to the Commission (13 March 2007) Subject: Fighting terrorism Further to the Commission’s answer to my Question E‑0151/07 on the failure of countries like Ireland and Germany to meet the 2 August 2005 deadline for implementing Council Framework Decision 2003/577/JHA OJ L 196, 2.8.2003, p. 45. of 22 July 2003, on, inter alia , freezing property or evidence relating to terrorism, will the Commission now (a) list those Member States that have not implemented it, (b) seek specific information from the defaulting Member States on their reasons for not implementing the framework Decision and (c) seek information on when they expect to implement it?
