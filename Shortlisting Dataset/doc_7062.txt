FEMM Social integration of women belonging to ethnic minority groups Following the public hearing on 7 April on the same topic, the Rapporteur presented her draft report to the Committee on 3 May. The report aims to evaluate social integration policies in the EU for women belonging to ethnic minority groups and to highlight where this works and where there are problems to help identify solution. The text is divided into three parts according to the following key aspects: role of integration policies in the EU, role of women in ethnic minorities, and gender equality policy. 20100507CAN74301
