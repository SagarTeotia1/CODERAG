Amendments by Parliament Amendment 1 Recital 9 (9) This Decision respects the fundamental rights and observes the principles recognised in particular by the Charter of Fundamental Rights of the European Union as general principles of Community law, (9) This Decision respects all the fundamental rights and observes all the principles recognised in particular by the Charter of Fundamental Rights of the European Union as general principles of Community law, Justification The European Parliament stresses in particular that all fundamental rights and principles recognised by the Charter of Fundamental Rights of the European Union must be reflected as general principles of Community law in the Research Fund as elsewhere. Proposal for a Council decision laying down multiannual financial guidelines for managing the assets of the "ECSC in liquidation" and, on completion of the liquidation, the "Assets of the Coal and Steel Research Fund" ( COM(2000) 520 - COM(2001) 121 - C5-0166/2001 - 2001/0363(CNS)) Text proposed by the Commission OJ C 180, 26.06.2001. Amendments by Parliament Amendment 2 Recital 2a (new) (2a) In decision-making in this area, the European Parliament must be placed on an equal footing with the Council. Amendment 3 Recital 5 (5) This Decision respects the fundamental rights and observes the principles recognised in particular by the Charter of Fundamental Rights of the European Union as general principles of Community law, (5) This Decision respects all the fundamental rights and observes all the principles recognised in particular by the Charter of Fundamental Rights of the European Union as general principles of Community law, Justification The European Parliament stresses in particular that all fundamental rights and principles recognised by the Charter of Fundamental Rights of the European Union must be reflected as general principles of Community law in the Research Fund as elsewhere. Amendment 4 Article 2a (new) (2a) By 24 July 2004 the Commission shall submit a proposal enabling the Coal and Steel Research Fund to be topped up with money from public and private sources and permitting other research topics to be covered. Amendment 5 Article 2b (new) (2b) The Commission shall set up a group of experts, with the participation of the European Parliament, to draw up proposals for a European Research Foundation.
