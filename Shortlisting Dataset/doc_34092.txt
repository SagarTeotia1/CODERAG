ORAL QUESTION H-0696/02 for Question Time at the part-session in October 2002 pursuant to Rule 43 of the Rules of Procedure by Gerard Collins to the Commission Subject: Strategic EU-Africa partnership One of the positive results of the World Summit held in Johannesburg was the signing of a strategic EU-Africa partnership on water and sanitation. Will the Commission state how it intends to ensure that the objectives of this partnership are met and will it outline the steps that have already been taken in this regard?
