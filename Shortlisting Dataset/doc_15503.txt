Second, it sets out the main changes in the DB relative to the preliminary draft budget (PDB) for 2007 prepared by the European Commission. Third, it reports on the conciliation meeting held between the Council and European Parliament in Brussels on 14 July 2006. I - Main elements of the Council's draft budget 2.
