WRITTEN QUESTION E-6367/07 by Francisco José Millán Mon (PPE‑DE) to the Commission (18 December 2007) Subject: Position on remarks by Cuban deputy foreign minister According to media reports, Cuba's Deputy Minister for Foreign Affairs, Mr Eumelio Caballero, said in Havana on 28 November 2007 that the EU does not have an independent policy on Cuba, thanks to US pressure, and called on the Union to remove the measures implemented against Cuba in 2003 in order to pave the way for a ‘respectful dialogue between the parties’. Can the Commission state its position on these remarks?
