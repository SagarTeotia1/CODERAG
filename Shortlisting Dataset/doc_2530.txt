Question for written answer E-011248/2011 to the Commission Rule 117 Niki Tzavela (EFD) (2 December 2011) Subject: Drugs According to articles in the Greek press, increasing numbers of drugs are appearing on the European market; this is the warning given in a report by the European Monitoring Centre for Drugs and Drug Addiction, published in Lisbon. There were 41 new substances in 2010 and, to date, a further 39 have been identified. According to the report, about 14.5 million Europeans have tried cocaine during their lifetime, with first place held by the United Kingdom, followed by Denmark, Spain, Italy and Cyprus. What action has the Commission taken against the scourge of drugs, and has this action produced any results according to the statistics at its disposal?
