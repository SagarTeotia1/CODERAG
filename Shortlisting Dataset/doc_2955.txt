WRITTEN QUESTION E-6743/09 by Franz Obermayr (NI) to the Commission (13 January 2010) Subject: EU publicity spending for Sweden The new ‘Treffpunkt Europa’, in a modern building with 2 500 m² of floorspace in Vienna (Wipplingerstraße 35, 1010 Wien), has been designed as a meeting place for those interested in the EU to share experiences. Following its grand opening on 16 October 2009 by European Parliament President Jerzy Buzek, European Commission President José Manuel Barroso and high-ranking Austrian government representatives, a great many conferences and events have been arranged. 1. How much money does the EU grant to Sweden for publicity campaigns (posters, brochures, PR, advertising, etc.)? 2. How much money was spent on advertising materials and subsidies for EU public-relations activities in Sweden for 2009? 3. How large is Sweden's 2010 budget for advertising materials and subsidies for EU public-relations activities?
