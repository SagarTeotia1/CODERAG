Reply (4 October 2006) In reply to the questions put by the Honourable Member of Parliament, the Council would like to recall that the agenda for the Ecofin Council of 11 July 2006 See doc. 11288/06: Provisional agenda for 2741st meeting of the Council of the European Union (Economic and Financial Affairs). was established in accordance with the provisions of Article 8 of the Council's Rules of Procedure, which were recently amended following the decision of the European Council to further open up the work of the Council See the text as set out in document 10926/06: ‘Council Decision adopting the Council's Rules of Procedure’. On 27 June 2006, the Permanent Representatives Committee decided, pursuant to Article 8(3) of the Council's present Rules of Procedure, that the opening up of Council deliberations and the organisation of public debates in the Council as provided for in the amended version of Article 8 will be applied as from the 1st of July 2006 and until the entry into force of the amended Rules of Procedure. . Thus, the Ecofin Council held a public debate on the Presidency's work programme and deliberated in public on a proposal for a Council decision concerning EIB external lending mandates, in accordance with the provisions laid down in Article 8, paragraphs 2 and 4, of the amended version of the Council's Rules of Procedure. Certain other agenda items with a bearing on economic and monetary issues and on international relations were addressed in the closed part of the session. This is in full accordance with the enhanced transparency rules of the Council.
