WRITTEN QUESTION E-1202/06 by Roberta Angelilli (UEN) to the Commission (23 March 2006) Subject: Municipality of Livorno: update regarding the situation of the funds relating to the action plan on promoting safer use of the Internet With reference to the earlier Written Question E‑1081/03 OJ C 58 E, 6.3.2004, p. 39 . in which the Commission was asked whether the Municipality of Livorno had submitted projects and secured funding under the action plan on promoting safer use of the Internet, the Commission replied on 15 May 2003 that the Municipality of Livorno had not submitted funding proposals under the 1999‑2002 action plan. Would the Commission say whether, for the period from 2003 to the present, the Municipality of Livorno: 1. has submitted projects for the action plan on promoting safer use of the Internet? 2. has secured funding for those projects? 3. has put that funding to any use?
