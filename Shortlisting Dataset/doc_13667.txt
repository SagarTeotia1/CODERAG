WRITTEN QUESTION E-0390/06 by Hélène Goudin (IND/DEM) to the Commission (7 February 2006) Subject: Postal services directive Further to my previous oral question to the Commission concerning the postal services directive (H‑1133/05 Oral answer of 17.1.2006. ), will the Commission also say which consultancy firms it has engaged to assess the deregulation of the postal market in the EU, what conclusions each consultancy firm has drawn, and what specific changes to the existing postal directive the Commission wishes to make?
