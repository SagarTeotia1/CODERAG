Answer given by Mr Frattini on behalf of the Commission (7 November 2006) The Frontex Project Managing team of HERA consists of two officers based in Warsaw, performing missions to the Canary Islands during the time frame of the operation performing management tasks. One Member State expert acting on behalf of Frontex is based at the Tenerife Operational Centre. His main tasks are involvement in local management of HERA II Operation and reporting to the Project Manager and Frontex Operational Coordination Centre.
