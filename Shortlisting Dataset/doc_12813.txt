Answer given by Mr Piebalgs on behalf of the Commission (30 March 2007) The Commission has not received such requests. In addition to the Interconnector Greece-Italy (IGI), which is a project of European interest (with the highest priority), the guidelines for trans-European energy networks Guidelines for Trans-European energy networks, Decision No 1364/2006/EC of Parliament and of the Council of 6 September 2006 laying down guidelines for trans-European energy networks and repealing Decision 96/391/EC and Decision No 1229/2003/EC, OJ L 262, 22.9.2006. mention three projects of common interest for Greece: — No 6.8: a high pressure branch to Corinth — No 6.9: a high pressure branch to North West Greece — No 6.14: a high pressure branch to Patra Therefore, the three abovementioned projects are eligible for funding, although not with the highest priority reserved for the projects of European interest.
