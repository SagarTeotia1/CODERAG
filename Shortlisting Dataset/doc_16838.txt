AFET Enlargement strategy report considered in AFET on 2 April On Wednesday, 2 April 2008 Mr Brok will submit to the Committee on Foreign Affairs his first draft for a resolution on the Union's enlargement strategy. The document, which will be debated in the presence of two academic experts, calls for a more differentiated range of contractual relations to be established between the Union and its neighbouring countries.
