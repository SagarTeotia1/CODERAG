WRITTEN QUESTION E-4757/06 by Dimitrios Papadimoulis (GUE/NGL) to the Commission (7 November 2006) Subject: Community finance from the European Social Fund (ESF) for vocational training centres in Greece Articles are constantly appearing in the Greek press which consider that the manner in which finance is provided from the European Social Fund for combating unemployment, particularly through the vocational training centres, is not transparent and an ineffective use of public money. Will the Commission investigate this matter in order to safeguard its authority in the face of such accusations and avoid any possible waste of Community finance? What measures will it take to bring transparency to the operation of, method of funding and the allocation of programmes to the vocational training centres?
