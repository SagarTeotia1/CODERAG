WRITTEN QUESTION E-4914/06 by Proinsias De Rossa (PSE) to the Commission (15 November 2006) Subject: FP7 and research and training networks Could the Commission outline how it is envisaged that the seventh framework programme of R & D will allow the continuation of EU financial support for research and training networks, particularly the flexible support given under FP5 and FP6 to a positive mix of both ‘early-stage researchers’ and ‘experienced researchers’?
