Their reports claim that the idea came from Italy, in response to the enormous harm suffered by the European textile industry as a result of imitations made in developing countries, but that it has now swelled to embrace the general problem of the labelling of all products manufactured within the Union. - Is there any truth in these reports? - Is the Commission going to put forward some proposal in this field, embodying this or other ideas? - If the reports are correct, what does the Commission think are the advantages and disadvantages of establishing a Made in EU label, to the detriment of nationally-based labels?
