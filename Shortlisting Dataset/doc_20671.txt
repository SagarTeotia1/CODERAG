Urges that the negotiations between the Israelis and the Palestinians should move forward in accordance with the deadlines laid down in the Sharm El-Cheikh agreements and urges the two parties to refrain from any unilateral actions which might call those agreements into question; 6. Points out, finally, that any definitive peace agreement must be guaranteed not only by the parties involved, but also by the international community, if necessary by means of a presence on the ground; 7. Instructs its President to forward this resolution to the Council, the Commission, the Israeli Government and the Knesset, the Palestinian Authority and the Palestinian Legislative Council and the Syrian Government and Parliament.
