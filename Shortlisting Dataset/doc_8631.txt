Commission services carefully assess emerging threats and risks in this area. In 2011, a study on the application of Directive 2005/60 EC has been published http://ec.europa.eu/internal_market/company/docs/financial-crime/20110124_study_amld_en.pdf . Commission services also actively participate in the assessment and revision of the international standards in the anti-money laundering (AML) area, particularly those set up by the Financial Action Task Force on money laundering (FATF). This assessment and revision process is ongoing and depending on the outcome of these discussions and taking into account other developments in this area (e.g. change of money laundering threats or schemes), Commission services will consider proposals for possible amendments to the current legal framework.
