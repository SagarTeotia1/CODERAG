WRITTEN QUESTION P-3946/05 by Eluned Morgan (PSE) to the Commission (21 October 2005) Subject: Bird flu What is the Commission doing to encourage Member States to stockpile medicines to counteract the effects of bird flu, in the event that it becomes transferable between humans? Does the Commission have its own contingency plan to deal with an outbreak? If not, could the Commission outline when it will produce some EU guidelines?
