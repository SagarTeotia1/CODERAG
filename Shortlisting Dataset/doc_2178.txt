2004/C 65 E/064 WRITTEN QUESTION E-1176/03 by María Sornosa Martínez (PSE) to the Commission (1 April 2003) Subject: Community funding for Spain Can the Commission forward, to the author of this question, a full list of all hydrological infrastructure schemes for which funding has been requested by the Spanish central government under the Structural Funds or the Cohesion Fund (for 2000-2006), in the context of major projects, whether planned on an individual basis or included in measures under the operative programmes?
