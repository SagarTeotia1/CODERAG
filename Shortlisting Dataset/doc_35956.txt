The High Representative fully agrees with the Honourable Member’s view that structural poverty is the basis for many social and economic problems in Latin America. For this reason one of the main areas of EU dialogue, action and cooperation with the EU‑LAC countries is the struggle for social cohesion. Indicatively, 40 % of the national and regional EU cooperation strategies with Latin America between 2007 and 2013 is dedicated to social cohesion.
