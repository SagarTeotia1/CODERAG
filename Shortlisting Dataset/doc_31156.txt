WRITTEN QUESTION E-5474/07 by Cristiana Muscardini (UEN) to the Commission (8 November 2007) Subject: Diabetes Day 14 November will be World Diabetes Day, the theme of which this year will be diabetes in children and adolescents. 1. What does the European Union plan to do to improve the situation of children suffering from diabetes, particularly in the educational context? 2. Are there action programmes geared to the protection of children and to making treatment homogeneous in all the EU Member States? 3. What contribution does the European Union make to the European Diabetes Federation and the International Society for Pediatric and Adolescent Diabetes? 4. Does the seventh framework programme include funding for research into diabetes mellitus?
