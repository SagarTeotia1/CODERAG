Furthermore, the Republic of Cyprus has made it compulsory, under these regulations, for coastal fishermen to renew their professional licence every year. Given the attempt by the European Union to restrict intensive fishing, will the Commission say whether the above measure setting a minimum catch limit for coastal fishing adopted by the Republic of Cyprus is contrary to EU efforts, and also whether it is reasonable and necessary, on the basis of these criteria, to require coastal fishermen to renew their professional licences every year?
