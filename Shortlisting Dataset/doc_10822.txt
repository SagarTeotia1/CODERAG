Question for written answer E-009768/2011 to the Commission Rule 117 Marian Harkin (ALDE) (27 October 2011) Subject: Cabotage rules Can the Commission clarify whether cabotage rules (Article 8 and 9 of Regulation (EC) No 1072/2009) apply to haulage activities within the UK if: 1. the goods carried are the property of the undertaking or have been sold, bought, let out on hire or hired, produced, extracted, processed or repaired by the undertaking; 2. the purpose of the journey is to carry the goods to or from the undertaking or to move them, either inside or outside the undertaking for its own requirements; 3. motor vehicles used for such carriage are driven by personnel employed by or put at the disposal of the undertaking under contractual obligation; 4. the vehicles carrying the goods are owned by the undertaking, or have been bought by it on deferred terms, and 5. such carriage is no more than ancillary to the overall activities of the undertaking?
