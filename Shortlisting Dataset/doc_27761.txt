Answer given by Ms Hübner on behalf of the Commission (16 July 2007) 1. The European Regional Development Fund allocation for the Regional Operational Programme for Galicia for the period 2000‑06 was EUR 2,438.7 million. This information was included in the Commission's reply to the Honourable Member's previous Written Question E‑0337/07 . 2. The European Regional Development Fund allocation for the Regional Operational Programme for Galicia for the period 2007‑13 is EUR 2,191 million. This information was included in the Commission's reply to the Honourable Member's previous Written Question E‑1599/07 . All figures are in current prices. In both programming periods Galicia also receives European Regional Development Fund contributions from Spanish pluri-regional programmes and Community initiatives, for which the Commission does not have the breakdown by region.
