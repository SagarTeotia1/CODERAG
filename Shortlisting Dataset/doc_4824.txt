9999/C Q999 E/176 WRITTEN QUESTION E-3840/03 by Proinsias De Rossa (PSE) to the Commission (04 December 2003) Subject: Race Directive (2000/43/EC) Has the Commission been in contact with the Irish Government over its failure to implement the equal treatment between persons without racial and ethnic discrimination in employment and the social sector - Directive 2000/43/EC OJ L 180, 19.7.2000, p. 22 . , which should have been implemented in Ireland by 19 July 2003? What has been the Government's response? What action is the Commission taking or proposing to take to ensure that all people living in Ireland enjoy the full protection of this legislation?
