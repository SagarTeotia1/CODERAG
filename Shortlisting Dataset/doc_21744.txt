WRITTEN QUESTION E-2674/06 by Francisca Pleguezuelos Aguilar (PSE) to the Commission (21 June 2006) Subject: Data on the implementation of the Structural Funds in Granada, Andalucia (Spain) Now we are at the end of the current Financial Perspective and the 2005 budget has been executed, can the Commission supply figures for the execution of Structural Fund payment and commitment appropriations for the water supply project ‘Abastecimiento de agua potable en el área metropolitana de Granada’ in Granada (Andalucia, Spain) during the current programming period (2000‑05)?
