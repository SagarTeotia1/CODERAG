Kingdom: an order to control BZP was laid before Parliament on 19 October 2009. BZP is made part of a proposal on a generic approach to piperazines. Subject to Parliamentary agreement, BZP will be controlled under the Misuse of Drugs Act 1971 as a Class C drug on 23 December 2009. Conclusion Of the EU Member States, 24 countries have made BZP subject to control measures and criminal provisions. In three Member States decisions are pending.
