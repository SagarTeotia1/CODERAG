Strongly regrets that, despite its competence on Budgetary Control, the Committee only became aware of the allegations relating to the said arrangements from media reports rather than, as should have been expected, from the administration or from the Bureau of the European Parliament, both of which had been fully aware of the allegations for several weeks; 13.
