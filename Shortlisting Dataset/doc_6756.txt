WRITTEN QUESTION E-1201/08 by Proinsias De Rossa (PSE) to the Commission (5 March 2008) Subject: Ireland's national strategy for biodegradable waste In its report (COM(2006)406 final) of 19 July 2006 on implementation of Community Waste legislation, the Commission said (p. 9, English version) that Ireland had not submitted its national strategy for reducing biodegradable waste going to landfill? Could the Commission indicate whether Ireland has now submitted this strategy? What action has it taken or is planning to take in this regard?
