P5_TA(2001)0556 EIB: Community guarantee against lending losses * A5-0350/2001 Proposal for a Council decision granting a Community guarantee to the European Investment Bank against losses under a special lending action for selected environmental projects in the Baltic Sea basin of Russia under the Northern Dimension ( COM(2001) 297 - C5-0307/2001 - 2001/0121(CNS) ) The proposal was approved. European Parliament legislative resolution on the proposal for a Council decision granting a Community guarantee to the European Investment Bank against losses under a special lending action for selected environmental projects in the Baltic Sea basin of Russia under the Northern Dimension ( COM(2001) 297 - C5-0307/2001 - 2001/0121(CNS) ) (Consultation procedure) The European Parliament, - having regard to the Commission proposal to the Council ( COM(2001) 297 OJ C 240 E, 28.8.2001, p. 295. ), - having been consulted by the Council pursuant to Article 308 of the EC Treaty ( C5-0307/2001 ), - having regard to Rule 67 of its Rules of Procedure, - having regard to the report of the Committee on Budgets and the opinion of the Committee on the Environment, Public Health and Consumer Policy ( A5-0350/2001 ), 1. Approves the Commission proposal; 2. Calls on the Council to notify Parliament should it intend to depart from the text approved by Parliament; 3. Asks to be consulted again if the Council intends to amend the Commission proposal substantially; 4. Instructs its President to forward its position to the Council and Commission.
