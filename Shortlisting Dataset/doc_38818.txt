The Commission acknowledged receipt of all NP opinions it had received. The Commission prepares replies to these NP opinions, which put forward substantial remarks, according to its internal procedure. All opinions coming from NP are duly considered by the Commission, which is obliged by its internal procedure to provide a thorough response. NP opinions clearly contribute to the process of policy formulation inside the Commission. Moreover, Commission responses will be sent to Parliament and the Council, which gives these institutions a chance to include NP observations in their internal deliberations. Commission responses sent to both these institutions will be accompanied by the NP opinions, which they refer to. Thereby, the Honourable Member can become easily acquainted with comments expressed in these opinions.
