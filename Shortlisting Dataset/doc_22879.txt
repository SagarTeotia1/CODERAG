Question for written answer E-009735/2011 to the Commission Rule 117 Marina Yannakoudakis (ECR) (27 October 2011) Subject: Registration of right-hand drive and left-hand drive vehicles in the EU Can the Commission please comment on whether right-hand drive and left-hand drive vehicles are permitted to be registered in any European Union Member State? For example, are there restrictions and regulations in place in Spain that would prevent a British left-hand drive car being successfully registered there?
