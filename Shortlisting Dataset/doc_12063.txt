FEMM Act concerning the election of the Members of the European Parliament - 30 November The FEMM amendments to the draft report of the Committee on Constitutional Affairs (AFCO), "Proposal for a modification of the Act concerning the election of the Members of the European Parliament", promote the idea of a proportionate representation of gender in the elections at national and European level. The amendments also propose that a minimum of 40% representation of either gender would be an eligibility criterion of any EU-wide list for a future "additional constituency" from the whole territory of the Union. 20101123CAN98689 AFCO draft report
