Question for written answer E-005723/2011 to the Commission Rule 117 Kartika Tamara Liotard (GUE/NGL) (15 June 2011) Subject: Practical measures to combat gender stereotypes 1. Has the Commission — and more specifically DG MOVE — in the context of the ‘Roadmap for equality between women and men 2006‑10’ drawn up a specific action plan to combat gender stereotypes? 2. If so, what practical measures were taken in this field by DG MOVE, and what concrete results were attained as a result? 3. If not, why were the objectives not attained and/or why were too few practical measures or none at all taken? 4. Has the Commission — and more specifically DG MOVE — in the context of the ‘Strategy for equality between women and men 2010‑15’, drawn up a specific follow-up plan to combat gender stereotypes? 5. If so, what practical measures are being taken in this field by DG MOVE, and within what time frame are the intended objectives to be attained?
