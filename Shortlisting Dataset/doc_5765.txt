IMCO Consumer Rights Directive: exchange of views with Commissioner Meglena Kuneva Commissioner Meglena Kuneva will present the new Commission proposal of a Directive on Consumer Rights to IMCO Members on 5 November 2008, from 15.00 to 16.00, room PHS 3 C 050. This Directive seeks to strengthen consumer protection and to simplify 4 existing EU consumer rights directives into one single instrument. The aim of the Directive is to boost consumer confidence while reducing administrative burdens. Further information on the European Commission Consumer Affairs website 20081021CAN40137
