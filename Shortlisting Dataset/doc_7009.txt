ECON Public Hearing on Strengthening Economic Governance in the EU Members of ECON held a Public Hearing on Strengthening Economic Governance in the EU on Thursday, 13 January 2011. Topics discussed included the reform of the Stability and Growth Pact, the surveillance of macroeconomic imbalances, the permanent crisis mechanism for public debt as well as the idea of issuing common bonds in the euro area (Eurobonds). The hearing featured Mario Monti, President of Bocconi University and former Commissioner, as keynote speaker, as well as a panel of experts including Paul de Grauwe, Professor of International Economics at KU Leuven, Paolo Manasse, Professor of Macroeconomics and International Economic Policy at the University of Bologna and Gustav Horn, Professor and Director at the Hans BÃ¶ckler Foundation. 20110106CAN11319 Programme and presentations of experts
