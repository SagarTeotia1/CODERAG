To avoid confusing consumers, MEPs say that the names of these products should be stated on the label, and the proposed margins of tolerance for stating fat content (+/- 0.2%) should be deleted. Standardisation for preserved milk (amendment of Directive 2001/114) Finally, Members recommend approving without amendment the Commission proposal to bring authorised protein content for milk powder and condensed milk - currently between 31% and 37% - into line with the international (Codex) standard of 34%. The aim of this is to put European exporters on an equal footing with other exporters.
