24.2.2012 B7-0121/2012 MOTION FOR A RESOLUTION pursuant to Rule 120 of the Rules of Procedure on an awareness campaign to combat gambling Sergio Paolo Francesco Silvestris PE483.163v01-00 B7‑0121/2012 Motion for a European Parliament resolution on an awareness campaign to combat gambling The European Parliament , – having regard to Rule 120 of its Rules of Procedure, A. whereas gambling is a phenomenon that has been spreading in Europe, especially in recent years; B. whereas gambling has devastating effects on gamblers, as it can break up entire families and provide a breeding ground for usury; C. whereas pathological gambling is a disorder in which people cannot control their urge to gamble continuously and whereas this constitutes a ‘social disease’ that is hard to fight; 1. Calls on the Commission to organise an appropriate awareness campaign in which the messages conveyed should be such as to help citizens understand the risks and disorders associated with gambling and pathological gambling.
