Additionally, it will step up efforts to better integrate biodiversity concerns into other policies, especially in view of delivering on the EU 2020 Strategy regarding the goals of achieving sustainable growth and resource efficiency and combating climate change. Indeed, healthy and resilient ecosystems are crucial to combating and adapting to climate change. At the occasion of the reforms of the Common Agriculture Policy, Common Fisheries Policy and the Cohesion policy, the Commission will seek to ensure the effective contribution of these policies to the achievement of the 2020 target.
