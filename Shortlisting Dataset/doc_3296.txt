LEGISLATIVE PROPOSAL Proposal for a Council regulation setting the limits to the Community financing of work programmes drawn up by approved operators' organisations in the olive sector provided for in Regulation (EC) No 1638/98 and derogating from Regulation No 136/66/EEC ( COM(2002) 343 – C5‑0353/2002 – 2002/0134(CNS)) The proposal is approved. DRAFT LEGISLATIVE RESOLUTION European Parliament legislative resolution on the proposal for a Council regulation setting the limits to the Community financing of work programmes drawn up by approved operators' organisations in the olive sector provided for in Regulation (EC) No 1638/98 and derogating from Regulation No 136/66/EEC ( COM(2002) 343 – C5‑0353/2002 – 2002/0134(CNS)) (Consultation procedure) The European Parliament , – having regard to the Commission proposal to the Council ( COM(2002) 343 OJ C not yet published. ), – having been consulted by the Council pursuant to Article 37 of the EC Treaty ( C5‑0353/2002 ), – having regard to Rule 67 of its Rules of Procedure, – having regard to the report of the Committee on Agriculture and Rural Development ( A5‑0292/2002 ), 1. Approves the Commission proposal; 2. Calls on the Council to notify Parliament should it intend to depart from the text approved by Parliament; 3. Asks to be consulted again should the Council intend to amend the Commission proposal substantially; 4. Instructs its President to forward its position to the Council and Commission.
