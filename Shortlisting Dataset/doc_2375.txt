Reaffirms its rejection of any challenges to its budgetary powers, such as that proposed by the ECOFIN Council; 11. Considers that the aims and tasks of the European Central Bank should be revised in order to make them subject to democratic control and to take due account of the goals of sustainable development and employment; 12. Calls for any amendment of the Treaty to be ratified by the national parliaments and rejects the 'nihil obstat' procedure, which would mean tacit authorisation by the national parliaments within a simplified procedure for revision of the Treaty; 13. Instructs its President to forward this resolution to the Council, the Commission and the governments and parliaments of the present and future Member States of the European Union.
