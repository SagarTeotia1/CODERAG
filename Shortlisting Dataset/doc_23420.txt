2004/C 84 E/112 WRITTEN QUESTION E-3717/03 by Roberta Angelilli (UEN) to the Commission (10 December 2003) Subject: Update concerning the funds for the SAVE II programme for the municipality of Massa With reference to question E-0966/03 OJ C 268 E, 7.11.2003, p. 169 . , in which the Commission was asked whether the municipality of Massa (Italy) had submitted projects and obtained funds under the SAVE II programme, the Commission replied on 25 April 2003 to the effect that no proposal had been submitted. Can the Commission state whether, since then and up to the present, it has received any SAVE II projects from that municipality?
