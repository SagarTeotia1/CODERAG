WRITTEN QUESTION E-1613/09 by Marios Matsakis (ALDE) to the Commission (13 March 2009) Subject: Freedom of the press in Turkey It is apparent that freedom of the press in Turkey is under attack, not least by the Turkish Prime Minister Mr Erdogan. Is the Commission concerned about this state of affairs? How can the Commission help to protect freedom of the press and freedom of speech in this future EU Member State?
