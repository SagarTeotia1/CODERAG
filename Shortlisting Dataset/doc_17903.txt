LIBE Extraordinary LIBE meeting in Strasbourg 14.2. In an extraordinary LIBE meeting on 14 February in Strasbourg, a vote was held on the own-iniative report on EU strategy on Roma inclusion (2010/2276(INI), rapporteur Lívia JÁRÓKA (EPP, HU)) and it was adopted with 50 votes in favour, 1 against. In an orientation vote on the draft report on Sexual abuse and sexual exploitation of children and child pornography (2010/0064(COD), rapporteur Roberta ANGELILLI (EPP, IT)), the text was approved, in view of the negotiations with the Council, with 40 votes in favour, 5 abstentions. 20110209CAN13342
