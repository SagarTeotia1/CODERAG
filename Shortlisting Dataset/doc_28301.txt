He has already served 20 months of his sentence. Under Belgian law, he would normally be entitled to a release on parole. However the administration of the Ministry of Justice will not consider this release on parole because he does not have a valid residence permit. Mr Amarantides' situation is contrary to the principle of equal treatment of all citizens in the European Union.
