Question for oral answer O-000166/2011 to the Commission Rule 115 Klaus-Heiner Lehne on behalf of the Committee on Legal Affairs Subject: The Hague Conference - ratification of conventions In view of its previous statements promoting the work of the Hague Conference, what measures has the Commission taken to date, and what further actions are envisaged, to encourage Member States to ratify the pre-2000 Hague Conventions. What is the Commission doing to encourage non-member countries to accede to the Hague Conventions, given that this is in the interest of EU citizens? Will it be using the resources of the European External Action Service (EEAS) to that end? Will EEAS staff be trained and equipped so as to able to assist citizens and central authorities with the practical workings of the Hague Conventions, particularly the child abduction convention, on the ground? Does the Commission envisage providing supplementary financial support to sustain current and future projects of the Hague Conference, in particular with regard to international surrogacy?
