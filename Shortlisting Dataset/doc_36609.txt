WRITTEN QUESTION E-6103/07 by Paulo Casaca (PSE) to the Commission (7 December 2007) Subject: Federation of Islamic Organisations in Europe Has the Commission any knowledge of the Federation of Islamic Organisations in Europe (FIOE)? Is this organisation granted any EU direct or indirect support, namely through the Structural Funds? Does this organisation perform any consultative tasks for the Commission? Can the Commission provide with confidence credentials of the leaders of this organisation, namely Sheikh Yusuf Al-Qardawi or Ibrahim Zayad?
