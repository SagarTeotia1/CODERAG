ORAL QUESTION H-0020/03 for Question Time at the part-session in February 2003 pursuant to Rule 43 of the Rules of Procedure by Ole Krarup to the Commission Subject: Commission's contribution to information campaigns in the applicant countries What practical activities is the Commission planning to carry out in the individual applicant countries, before the forthcoming referenda on membership, to inform their populations about the significance of EU membership? How will the Commission ensure that current and prospective campaigns are organised and conducted in such a way as to highlight both the advantages and disadvantages of the country's membership of the EU? Will the Commission provide detailed information concerning the nature of the activities and the budgets for the campaigns? To what extent are or will the Commission's activities be coordinated with the individual governments' corresponding activities?
