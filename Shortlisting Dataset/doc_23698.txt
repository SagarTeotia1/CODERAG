Conference of Presidents will hold its now traditional meeting with incoming Council Presidency, this time in Ljubljana. The political group leaders and President Pöttering will have meetings with the Slovenian Prime Minister, Janez Jansa, and his government. President Pöttering will also meet the Speaker of the Slovenian National Assembly, France Cukjati, and the group leaders will take part in a Youth Forum at the National Assembly. There will be a press conference with President Pöttering and Prime Minister Jansa at 17.40 on Wednesday.
