ORAL QUESTION H-0704/05 for Question Time at the part-session in September II 2005 pursuant to Rule 109 of the Rules of Procedure by Jim Higgins to the Commission Subject: Dairy farming Can the Commission indicate whether the Irish Minister for Agriculture has held discussions with the Commission regarding the problems affecting Irish dairy farmers and beef producers? If so, when did the most recent discussions take place. Finally, would the Commission indicate whether it is prepared to consider including representatives of producers on its Dairy Management Committee?
