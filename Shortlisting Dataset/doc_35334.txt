Question for written answer E-010775/2010 to the Commission Rule 117 Pat the Cope Gallagher (ALDE) (3 January 2011) Subject: Imprisonment of minors in Palestine It is estimated that over 300 Palestinian children are arrested and imprisoned by the Israeli authorities each month. Furthermore, allegations of ill treatment against these detainee minors are commonplace. Can the Commission confirm whether it intends to pursue this serious issue with the Israeli authorities?
