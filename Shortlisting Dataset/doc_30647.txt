It is, on the other hand, unclear that the introduction of an outright prohibition of such forms would be effective or proportionate. Nevertheless, it should be noted that the Commission does not have any powers under the Treaties to enforce the directive in individual cases, even if such legislative changes were made. It is for national authorities to enforce the legislation transposing the directive in their jurisdictions. Ultimately where businesses have been misled they should be aware that there is protection under the directive and national criminal and civil law.
