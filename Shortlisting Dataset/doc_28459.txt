Indeed, there are social costs linked to this problem which are not only related to costs of treatment but to the effects this has on individual players and their families. However, the issue of gambling addiction will be one of the issues covered by the broad consultation that was launched on 24 March 2011. Through this consultation the Commission seeks to accrue clearer information and data on a number of complex issues, including the policy objectives of Member States in this respect.
