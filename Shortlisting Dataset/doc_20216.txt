REGI Presentation of the external study at the next REGI Committee meeting The external study on "Regional development aspects of the impact of tourism in coastal regions" was presented to the members of the REGI Committee at its meeting on 26 June. The study examines the role that Structural Funds play in supporting tourism intervention in coastal regions based on the results of the programming period 2000-2006 and addresses specific recommendations to the European Commission and the coastal Member States and regions. 20080626CAN32834
