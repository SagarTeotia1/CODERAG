PECH FPA Protocols with Seychelles and São Tomé coming up - Micronesia vote on hold On 12 April, the PECH Committee Members will consider draft reports relating to the Protocols for the Fisheries Partnership Agreements with São Tomé and Principe and the Seychelles, as proposed by the rapporteurs, Luis Manuel CAPOULAS SANTOS (S&D, PT) and Alain CADEC (EPP, FR). The Committee vote on the new FPA Protocol with Micronesia, scheduled for the same date, has been put on hold, awaiting a formal approval of the protocol by the Micronesian Parliament, which postponed its vote until after the elections scheduled for May. 20110411CAN17517 OEIL dossier NLE/2010/335 (Seychelles) OEIL dossier NLE/2010/355 (São Tomé) OEIL dossier NLE/2010/290 (Micronesia)
