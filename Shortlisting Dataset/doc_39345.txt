In March 2002, the Commission organised, at La Toja (Spain), in cooperation with the Spanish Presidency, the first seminar bringing together officials of the Ministries of Sport and colleagues from the border police and customs. However, although most of the Member States are aware of the seriousness of the problem, it did not prove possible to adopt conclusions following this meeting.
