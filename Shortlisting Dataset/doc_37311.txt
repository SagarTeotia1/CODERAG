PECH Technical measures in the Baltic Sea On 29.09.2010 the PECH Committee will hold an exchange of views on a new proposal concerning technical measures in the Baltic Sea, for which Marek Józef Gróbrarczyk (ECR, PL) is rapporteur. The purpose of the proposed regulation is to move the provisions regarding technical measures out of the regulatory framework establishing annual fishing opportunities. 20100916CAN82510 OEIL dossier COD/2010/175
