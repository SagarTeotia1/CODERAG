CONTENTS PROCEDURAL PAGE DRAFT EUROPEAN PARLIAMENT LEGISLATIVE RESOLUTION PROCEDURAL PAGE By letter of 27 November 2003 the Commission submitted to Parliament, pursuant to Articles 251(2) and 80(2) of the EC Treaty, the proposal for a European Parliament and Council decision concerning the activities of certain third countries in the field of cargo shipping (codified version) ( COM(2003) 732 – 2003/0285(COD)). At the sitting of 3 December 2003 the President of Parliament announced that he had referred the proposal to the Committee on Legal Affairs and the Internal Market as the committee responsible and the Committee on Regional Policy, Transport and Tourism for its opinion (C5‑0578/2003). The committee appointed Sir Neil MacCormick rapporteur at its meeting of 22 January 2004. At is meeting of 19 February 2004 the Committee on Legal Affairs and the Internal Market decided, on a proposal from the chairman, to apply the simplified procedure under Rule 158(1), and the chairman was thus appointed rapporteur. The following were present for the decision on the chairman's proposal : Ioannis Koukiadis and Bill Miller (vice-chairmen), Janelly Fourtou, Marie-Françoise Garaud, José María Gil-Robles Gil-Delgado, Kurt Lechner, Klaus-Heiner Lehne, Sir Neil MacCormick, Manuel Medina Ortega, Anne-Marie Schaffner, Marianne L.P. Thyssen and Ian Twinn (for Rainer Wieland). The Committee on Regional Policy, Transport and Tourism decided on 21 January 2004 not to deliver and opinion. The report was tabled on 23 February 2004 .
