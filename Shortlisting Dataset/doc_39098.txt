WRITTEN QUESTION E-5455/09 by Lidia Joanna Geringer de Oedenberg (S&D) to the Commission (5 November 2009) Subject: State of implementation of Directive 2007/65/EC on the coordination of certain provisions laid down by law, regulation or administrative action in Member States concerning the pursuit of television broadcasting activities On 10 April 2008 Parliament adopted my Written Declaration No 99/2007 on the subtitling of all public service television programmes in the EU. Further to Commissioner Reding's reply of 3 September 2008, what stage has been reached in the implementation of Directive 2007/65/EC on broadcasting services OJ L 332, 18.12.2007, p. 27 . , which entered into force on 19 December 2007 and which must be transposed by the end of December 2009? To date, how many Member States have notified their transposing measures to the Commission? How many Member States have implemented Article 3 of the directive, requiring Member States to encourage media service providers to ensure that their services are gradually made accessible to people with a visual or hearing disability? What practical means (sign language, subtitling or audio description) have Member States used to implement Article 3 of the directive?
