Question for written answer E-6694/2010 to the Commission Rule 117 Seán Kelly (PPE) (19 August 2010) Subject: Google Adwords and trade marks Further to the judgment of the European Court of Justice on joined cases C‑236/08, C‑237/08 and C‑238/08, and taking into consideration the extension of Google's Adwords policy to allow third parties to purchase Adwords which match existing trade marks in continental Europe, does the Commission foresee the need to review Directives 2000/31/EC OJ L 178, 17.7.2000, p. 1. and 2008/95/EC OJ L 299, 8.11.2008, p. 25. ?
