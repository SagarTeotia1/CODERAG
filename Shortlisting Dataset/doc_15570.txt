recalls that hosting an agency requires serious efforts by the host country to facilitate the establishment and functioning thereof; calls upon the Dutch authorities to assist Eurojust in establishing its premises in close vicinity to the new Europol building and to provide Eurojust with substantial financial support for doing so; (4) Restates its repeatedly made demand that Europol be placed on a Community footing in order both to increase its effectiveness and to step up the democratic and judicial oversight to which it must be subject;
