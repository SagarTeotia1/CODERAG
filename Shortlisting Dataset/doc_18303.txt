Approval of the minutes of the previous sitting The minutes of the previous sitting were approved. 3 Agenda The President proposed, in view of recent events in Tibet and with the agreement of the political groups, the following amendment to the afternoon's agenda: - the insertion, as the second item on the agenda, after the debate on the results of the European Council (Brussels, 13/14 March 2008) , of Council and Commission statements on the situation in Tibet. The sitting would be extended to take account of this. The House agreed to the proposal. The debate on the statements on the situation in Tibet would be wound up with the tabling of motions for resolutions. The vote would take place on Thursday 10.04.2008 at 10.30.
