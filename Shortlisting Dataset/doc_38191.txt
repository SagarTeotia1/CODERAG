Answer given by Mr Borg on behalf of the Commission (15 September 2008) To date 10 Member States have submitted a report to the Commission, as required under Council Regulation (EC) No 1185/2003 Council Regulation (EC) No 1185/2003 of 26 June 2003 on the removal of fins of sharks on board vessels, OJ L 167, 4.7.2003. . Bulgaria, Estonia, Ireland, Lithuania and Slovenia submitted their reports for the reference year 2007 before the deadline (1 May 2008). Italy, Germany, Greece, Finland and the United Kingdom submitted their reports after the deadline. The Commission will shortly send a letter to those Member States who have not yet submitted their reports, urging them to comply, as soon as possible, with their obligations in this field.
