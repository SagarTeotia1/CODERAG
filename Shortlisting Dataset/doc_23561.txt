Answer given by Mrs Ferrero-Waldner on behalf of the Commission (6 June 2005) As per the Official Journal L 139 dated 6 June 2003, the list of groups and entities drawn up by the Council Common Position 2003/402/CFSP (5 June 2003) in application of specific measures to combat terrorism comprises, inter alia , ‘Mujahedin-e Khalq Organisation (MEK or MKO) (minus the “National Council of Resistance of Iran” (NCRI) (a.k.a. The National Liberation Army of Iran NLA, the militant wing of the MEK), the People's Mujahidin of Iran (PMOI), the Muslim Iranian Student's Society)’. In addition to the answer already provided to Written Question P‑0400/05 relating to the Hamid Pourmand case, the Commission provided further elements in answering Question E‑1044/05 . The Commission kindly refers the Honourable Member to these two previous answers. Regarding the Honourable Member's specific question, it goes without saying that the Commission remains very vigilant as to the development of the situation in Iran, and it continues to carefully calibrate the various instruments at its disposal, including the trade and cooperation agreement (TCA) negotiations.
