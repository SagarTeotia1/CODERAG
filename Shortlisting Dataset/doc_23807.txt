Calls on the Commission and the Member States to pay particular attention to women at risk of being excluded from the benefits of the Information Society, such as older women, unemployed and low-income women, migrant women, women belonging to ethnic minorities and women with disabilities, in order to prevent the emergence of a two-speed society and to give them the opportunity to participate in easily accessible ITC courses in their home localities; 4.
