AFCO Changes in the Rules adopted 03/11 The draft report by David MARTIN (S&D, GB) on changes in the EP Rules of Procedure required by the Lisbon Treaty was adopted at the Committee on Tuesday 03/11 with 19 votes for, 2 against and 1 abstention. The Parliament is voting on the report in the November plenary in Strasbourg. 20091103CAN63550 report D. Martin (A7-0043/2009)
