Temporary agents to be recruited must possess the same educational/training qualifications and professional experience as the equivalent official. They are also subject to the obligations of impartiality, integrity and loyalty which apply to officials. The Commission does not consider, therefore, that the limited use of temporary agents lowers the calibre of the European civil service and there is no objective past or present evidence to suggest otherwise.
