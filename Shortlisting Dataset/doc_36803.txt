WRITTEN QUESTION E-5034/09 by Morten Messerschmidt (EFD) to the Commission (15 October 2009) Subject: Number of rules in force in the EU For each of the last five years, what is the latest figure for the total number of regulations, directives, decisions, international agreements, recommendations, communications, Green Papers, White Papers, resolutions of the European Council and other institutions, unpublished decisions and rules, etc. plus statements by Member States and institutions on regulations, directives, agreements, decisions etc.? How many of these are new? Will the Commission also give information for each of the last five years on the total number of CEN, CENELEC und ETSI standards, other standards, international standards from the Codex Alimentarius, etc. that are also used in EU Member States and other countries, and any other rules that are either binding on, or benchmarks for, EU citizens? How many of these are new?
