Question for written answer E-7110/2010 to the Commission Rule 117 Claudio Morganti (EFD) (14 September 2010) Subject: EU funding for the Marche region Would the Commission state how much EU funding has been allocated and/or paid to the Marche regional authorities or residents of the region since 2000 and in the current year and what amounts are to be allocated and/or paid in future years? In particular, would the Commission list the names of the recipients and the projects funded (by category)? To what extent and for which projects were the funds allocated and/or paid to the Marche regional authorities or residents of the region drawn from the European Globalisation Adjustment Fund, the Cohesion Fund, the European Regional Development Fund and the European Social Fund?
