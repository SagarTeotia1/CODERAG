WRITTEN QUESTION E-1612/09 by Dimitrios Papadimoulis (GUE/NGL) to the Commission (13 March 2009) Subject: Special quality control adviser With a delay of some two and a half years the contract of the special quality control adviser (ESPEL) charged with carrying out spot checks to verify the quality of co-funded EU projects executed in Greece has finally been signed. Will the Commission state: in the intervening period which body or organisation carried out quality controls on EU co-funded projects in Greece? Can it provide detailed information on quality problems identified by controls to date? Which projects do they concern and which amounts are involved?
