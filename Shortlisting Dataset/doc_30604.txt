WRITTEN QUESTION E-3971/05 by Baroness Sarah Ludford (ALDE) to the Commission (27 October 2005) Subject: Western Balkans' involvement in EU funding programmes What progress has been made in the implementation of the commitment made at the Thessaloniki summit in 2003 to open EU funding programmes to the Western Balkan countries? In particular, what progress has been made in opening up the Leonardo and Socrates education programmes to these countries?
