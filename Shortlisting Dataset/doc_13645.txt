WRITTEN QUESTION E-0215/10 by Joe Higgins (GUE/NGL) to the Commission (2 February 2010) Subject: WEEE Directive in Ireland Does the current practice by electrical producers in Ireland of imposing vEMC or PRF fees ‘in addition’ to retail prices when supplying a new product of similar type violate the explicit ‘at least free of charge’ provisions in Article 5(2b) of the EC Directive (2002/96/EC OJ L 37, 13.2.2003, p. 24. ) for the return of household WEEE?
