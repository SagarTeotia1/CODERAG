VIII B of the budget, once the legal basis has been adopted; Budget of the Joint Supervisory Data Protection Bodies 46. Is of the opinion that the budget of the Joint Supervisory Data Protection Bodies, set up by the Convention on the Establishment of a European Police Office (Europol Convention), the Convention on the Use of Information Technology for Customs Purposes and the Convention implementing the Schengen Agreement on the gradual abolition of checks at the common borders (Schengen Convention), should, for reasons of transparency, become Section VIII C of the general budget (the budget of the Ombudsman would become Section VIII A); 47. Instructs its President to forward this resolution to the institutions and bodies concerned.
