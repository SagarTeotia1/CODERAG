WRITTEN QUESTION E-4686/08 by Georgios Papastamkos (PPE‑DE) to the Commission (3 September 2008) Subject: Digitisation of the EU's cultural heritage According to the Commission's Communication of 11 August 2008 (COM(2008)0513) on progress on the digitisation and online accessibility of cultural material and digital preservation across the EU, ‘Between 2003 and 2007, Greece supported 180 different digitisation projects from museums, libraries and archives with total funding of EUR 100 million. Under the Greek national digital strategy for 2008‑13 a similar amount is earmarked for digitisation’. 1. What measures have been taken to promote archaeological sites and finds in the regions of Central Macedonia, Western Macedonia and Eastern Macedonia-Thrace? 2. Have measures of this kind in Macedonia received support from the European structural funds and other Community programmes? 3. Does the Commission have any comparative data on the use of resources for digitisation in the Member States of the EU?
