Question for written answer E-006660/2011 to the Commission Rule 117 Nikolaos Salavrakos (EFD) (13 July 2011) Subject: Tourism for persons with disabilities In the EU there are approximately 133 million tourists who are disabled, and the potential revenue from tourism is estimated at EUR 80 billion. According to 2007 Eurobarometer statistics, 91 % of disabled respondents said they would spend more if the physical obstacles were reduced. In view of the above, will the Commission say: 1. What financial and operational measures does it intend to take to persuade European companies to intervene to improve accessibility for the disabled? 2. Does it provide special funds for companies that undertake such actions in favour of the disabled?
