WRITTEN QUESTION P-1132/06 by Marie Panayotopoulos-Cassiotou (PPE‑DE) to the Commission (15 March 2006) Subject: Education and training programmes and European identity Under the plan for the new series of programmes (Culture 2010, Lifelong learning 2007–13, Education and Training 2010 and Youth in Action 2007‑13), what specific actions and measures to simplify procedures and reduce bureaucracy does the Commission intend to take to exploit the new opportunities for education and training, which aim to develop young people’s intellectual and scientific abilities and help them to acquire skills and professional qualifications and in parallel to strengthen their feeling of belonging to Europe, so that they can contribute to the economic, social and political life of the EU?
