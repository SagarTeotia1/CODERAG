WRITTEN QUESTION E-2868/10 by Diogo Feio (PPE) to the Commission (29 April 2010) Subject: Alleged violation by Portugal of Regulation (EC) No 1698/2005 and of National Decree No 229-B/2008 (Portugal) of 6 March 2008 — permanent grazing, biodiversity-related permanent grazing and fodder crops In response to my Written Question E‑0742/10 on the alleged violation by the Portuguese State of Regulation (EC) No 1698/2005 OJ L 277, 21.10.2005, p. 1. and of National Decree 229-B/2008 (Portugal) of 6 March 2008, M. Dacian Cioloş, Commissioner, stated that the Commission had requested information from the Member State in question on the points raised and that I would be informed of the Commission's conclusions, for which I am extremely grateful. — When does the Commission expect to receive a response from the Portuguese State? — Has the Commission taken steps itself to investigate the situation described in my question? Is the Commission prepared to take such steps should it not receive a timely response to its request for information?
