Question for written answer E-002242/2012 to the Commission Rule 117 David Casa (PPE) (28 February 2012) Subject: Early school leaving According to Androulla Vassiliou, EU Commissioner for Education, Culture, Multilingualism, and Youth, 14 % of pupils in the EU leave school right after the completion of compulsory education. What can the Commission report on meeting the its set target to bring down early school leaving to 10 % by 2020? If current trends prevail, what actions will the Commission take to speed up efforts to keep students in school by 2020?
