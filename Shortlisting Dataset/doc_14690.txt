WRITTEN QUESTION E-3011/05 by Proinsias De Rossa (PSE) to the Commission (8 August 2005) Subject: Draft directive on access to justice in environmental matters In addressing the lower house of the Irish Parliament in November 2004, the Irish Minister for the Environment expressed doubt that the Commission's proposal for a directive on access to justice in environmental matters ( COM(2003)0624 ) would be adopted by Council, which has been discussing this matter since October 2003. What is the Commission's assessment of the likelihood of this proposal being adopted by Council in the immediate future? Does it believe a Commission initiative is warranted to secure adoption of this proposal?
