ORAL QUESTION H-0456/07 for Question Time at the part-session in July 2007 pursuant to Rule 109 of the Rules of Procedure by Glenis Willmott to the Council Subject: Environmental and human violations in the cotton industry Cotton is the world's most valuable non-food agricultural commodity, yet it has also been labelled the world's 'dirtiest' crop, accounting for more insecticide release than any other crop and with a high reliance on child labour, including the use of forced child labour. Is the Council aware of the levels of human rights and environmental violations in the cotton industry?
