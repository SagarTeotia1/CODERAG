WRITTEN QUESTION E-2698/07 by Robert Kilroy-Silk (NI) to the Commission (24 May 2007) Subject: EU officials and diplomatic immunity Further to my Question E‑1120/07 will the Commission: — give details of any cases in the last 20 years when it has refused to lift the diplomatic immunity of an official, agent or member of staff when asked to do so by a Member State; — give details of the case when it lifted the diplomatic immunity of a member of its staff posted to a third country at the request of the judicial authorities of a Member State?
