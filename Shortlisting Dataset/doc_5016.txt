JURI Inter-Parliamentary Committee Meeting - Workshop on Civil Justice on 30.11.10 "How to facilitate the life of European families and citizens?" Removing the legal and administrative barriers that citizens face when they start a family life in a Member State other than their own was at the centre of the Workshop on Civil Justice which took place in the European Parliament on 30.11.10 . It was opened by Diana Wallis on behalf of the President of Parliament, Melchior Wathelet, Belgian Secretary of State for the Budget, Migration and Asylum Policy, Family Policy and Federal Cultural Institutions, representing the presidency and Vice-President Viviane Reding, Commissioner for Justice.
