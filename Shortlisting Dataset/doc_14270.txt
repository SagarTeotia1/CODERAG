WRITTEN QUESTION E-0360/09 by Roberta Angelilli (UEN) to the Commission (30 January 2009) Subject: Information on the utilisation of funding from the Intelligent Energy — Europe programme by the municipality of Pesaro (Italy) The EU's financial framework for 2007‑2013 includes a considerable number of programmes whose purpose is to support Community policies in a range of thematic areas including R & TD, training programmes, cultural promotion, the environment, transport, energy, health and consumer protection. In particular, the municipality of Pesaro (Marche, Italy) is in need of EU funds to promote energy efficiency, rational resource use and the use of renewable energy sources. Can the Commission state: 1. whether the municipality of Pesaro has submitted any projects under the Intelligent Energy — Europe programme; 2. whether the municipality of Pesaro has obtained any funding, and, if not, why such funding has not been granted; 3. if funding has been granted, whether the funds concerned were utilised within the stipulated deadline and, if not, whether the Commission has asked for the sums concerned to be returned?
