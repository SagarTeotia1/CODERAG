WRITTEN QUESTION E-6419/08 by Ilda Figueiredo (GUE/NGL) to the Commission (28 November 2008) Subject: Community funding to support Sakihi — Portugal The multinational Sakihi — Portugal, which is Indian in origin, has a factory in the municipality of Maia, in the district of Porto, that produces foundry products for the car industry and employs around 420 people. Can the Commission say whether Community funds were granted to this multinational for setting up in Portugal, and specifically for the factory in Maia?
