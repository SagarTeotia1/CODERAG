Answer given by Mr Tajani on behalf of the Commission (30 June 2008) The Commission has opened proceedings on the public support provided by the Scottish Executive for the provision of ferry services OJ C 126, 23.5.2008 . , following numerous and repeated complaints by competitors and Members of the Parliament. The Commission can confirm that it will conclude this investigation as speedily as possible, after taking into account comments made by the United Kingdom government and interested third parties.
