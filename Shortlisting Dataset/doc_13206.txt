ORAL QUESTION H-0465/08 for Question Time at the part-session in July 2008 pursuant to Rule 109 of the Rules of Procedure by David Martin to the Council Subject: Palestinian unity government Former US President Jimmy Carter recently criticised the Middle East Quartet's continued embargo on Gaza. In the view of the Council, can the continued embargo help resolve Palestine's internal difficulties peacefully? Would the Council consider the formation of a unity government involving Hamas as an acceptable step towards a solution?
