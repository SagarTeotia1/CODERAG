Wednesday Following the Conference of Presidents decision of 15 January 2004, Council and Commission statements on the Irish Presidency programme and the European Constitution would be included from 15.00 to 16.00, instead of the Commission communication. The debate would include Council and Commission statements, speeches by Mr Elmar BROK (EPP-ED, D) and Mr Klaus HÄNSCH (PES, D) (Parliament's representatives at the IGC), and interventions from representatives of the political groups and from other members. This debate will lead to a draft resolution, on which there will be a vote on Thursday. There would be a Council statement on the Statute for Members, without debate at 16.00.
