WRITTEN QUESTION E-1765/09 by Georgios Georgiou (IND/DEM) to the Council (19 March 2009) Subject: Deception involving Turkish 50 kurus and one lira coins Turkey is continuing to circulate 50 kurus coins which have the same appearance and dimensions as one euro coins, and the same applies to one lira coins which are identical to two euro coins. Why is this state of affairs tolerated, since it causes confusion and harms the interests of citizens? Why are no sanctions imposed on Turkey which has pledged to withdraw the above coins?
