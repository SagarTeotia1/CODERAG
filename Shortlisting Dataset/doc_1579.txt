9999/C Q999 E/193 WRITTEN QUESTION E-3859/03 by Raffaele Costa (PPE-DE) to the Commission (04 December 2003) Subject: Funding of small Tacis Programme projects Having regard to the fact that the percentage share of funding allocated to small projects under the Tacis programme increased by 15% in 2002, could the Commission state how many small projects received funding, and what amounts of money were involved?
