WRITTEN QUESTION P-4444/06 by John Bowis (PPE‑DE) to the Commission (12 October 2006) Subject: Combating a ‘flu pandemic’ What is the Commission's response to research indicating that statins could play a pivotal part in combating a flu pandemic? Is it aware that this conclusion has been reached by Professor Peter Dunnill of University College London, Professor David Felson of the University of Virginia and Susan Chu, the Editor of FLUWIKI.com, who show that the damping down of the cytokine storm by statins could counter virulent forms of pandemic flu?
