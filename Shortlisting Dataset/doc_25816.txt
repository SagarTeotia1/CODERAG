It may be added that the Gas Directive does not oblige Member States to ensure that all households are supplied with natural gas supply. Therefore, Member State may decide whether or not households will be connected to the natural gas network. With regard to measures taken to provide alternative fuels to oil to the small industrial users, commercial and households, the Greek Government offers incentives to this category of customers to develop renewable energy and co-generation. For more information on those measures, the Honourable Member could contact the Greek Ministry of Development.
