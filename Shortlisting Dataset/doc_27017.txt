Biofuels Parliament underlines the need for the Conference of Parties (COP 10) to adopt a recommendation on biofuels, given the extreme importance of assessing the direct and indirect impacts of biofuels on biodiversity and of establishing biofuel certification and sustainability. "Destroying natural habitats and continued logging of rain forests to make way for biofuel crops are definitely not a solution", warn MEPs. About Parliament's COP-10 delegation The delegation, in Nagoya from 25 to 27 October, is headed by EP Environment Committee Chair Jo Leinen (S&D, DE).
