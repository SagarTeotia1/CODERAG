Question for written answer E-008629/2011 to the Commission Rule 117 Rareş-Lucian Niculescu (PPE) (29 September 2011) Subject: Suspension of gas transit services by Ukraine The Prime Minister of Ukraine, Mikola Azarov, warned the European Union last week that Ukraine might suspend gas transit services and stated that the EU, Ukraine and Russia should take a joint decisions on Ukraine’s supply system. Can the Commission state what forms of dialogue it intends to launch with Ukraine in order to settle any potential problems and what alternative solutions it has in mind?
