2004/C 78 E/239 WRITTEN QUESTION E-3187/03 by Geoffrey Van Orden (PPE-DE) to the Commission (27 October 2003) Subject: Free movement of bankrupt persons in the EU A person who has been declared bankrupt in the UK is free to live anywhere they wish in the UK. Is a person who has been declared bankrupt in the UK free to live anywhere they wish in the EU in the same way that a citizen not declared bankrupt would be able to?
