MOTION FOR A RESOLUTION EXPLANATORY STATEMENT PROCEDURAL PAGE On 15 June 2000 the Commission forwarded to Parliament, pursuant to Article 276 of EC Treaty, the balance sheets and administrative accounts of the sixth, seventh and eighth European Development Funds for the 1999 financial year ( COM(2000) 357 – 2000/2164(DEC)). At the sitting of 8 September 2000 the President of Parliament announced that she had forwarded these documents to the Committee on Budgetary Control as the committee responsible and to the Committee on Development and Cooperation for its opinion ( C5-0257/2000 ) On 14 November 2000, the Court of Auditors forwarded to Parliament its annual report on the activities of the sixth, seventh and eighth European Development Fund together with the replies from the institutions. At the sitting of 11 December 2000 the President of Parliament announced that she had forwarded these documents to the Committee on Budgetary Control as the committee responsible and to the Committee on Development and Cooperation for its opinion ( C5-0618/2000 ). On 14 March 2001 the Council forwarded to Parliament the recommendations concerning the discharge to be given in respect of the European Development Funds for the 1999 financial year (6536/2001 – C5-0122/2001 , 6537/2001 – C5-0123/2001 , 6538/2001 – C5-0124/2001 ). At the sitting of 2 April 2001 the President announced that she had forwarded these documents to the Committee on Budgetary Control as the committee responsible and to the Committee on Development and Cooperation for its opinion ( C5-0122/2001 , C5-0123/2001 , C5-0124/2001 ). At its meeting of 6 November 2000 the Committee on Budgetary Control had appointed Heide Rühle rapporteur. At its meetings of 6 February and 22 March 2001, the committee examined the balance sheets and administrative accounts for the 1999 financial year, the Council's recommendation, the draft report and the opinion of the Committee on Development and Cooperation. At the last meeting it adopted the motion for a resolution unanimously. The following were present for the vote: Diemut R. Theato, chairman; Herbert Bösch and Freddy Blak, vice-chairmen; Heide Rühle, rapporteur; Mogens N.J. Camre, Paolo Casaca (for Anne Ferreira), Rijk van Dam, Michiel van Hulten, José Javier Pomés Ruiz, Bart Staes and Gabriele Stauner. The explanatory statement was presented orally in plenary sitting. The opinion of the Committee on Development and Cooperation was attached. The report was tabled on 23 March 2001 ( A5-0109/2001 ). At the sitting of 4 April 2001 Parliament adopted a resolution postponing the discharge decision in respect of implementation of the budget of the sixth, seventh and eighth European Development Funds for the 1999 financial year.
