WRITTEN QUESTION E-2812/10 by Liam Aylward (ALDE) to the Commission (23 April 2010) Subject: Humanitarian situation in Gaza Following the visit of the High Representative of the Union for Foreign Affairs and Security Policy, Catherine Ashton, to Israel and Gaza, can the Commission provide an assessment of how EU aid has been distributed and used in the region? Can the Commission also provide an update on the current political and humanitarian situation in the region?
