ORAL QUESTION H-0868/07 for Question Time at the part-session in November 2007 pursuant to Rule 109 of the Rules of Procedure by Stavros Arnaoutakis to the Commission Subject: Framework Programme on Solidarity and Management of Migration Flows What stage has been reached regarding the implementation of the new funds set up under the Framework Programme on Solidarity and Management of Migration Flows (European Fund for the Integration of Third Country Nationals, European Refugee Fund, External Borders Fund and European Return Fund)? Have the fund guidelines and allocations to each country been decided? Will regional and local bodies and non-governmental organisations be involved in the planning and implementation of fund actions, and to what extent?
