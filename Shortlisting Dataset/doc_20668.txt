Question for written answer E-001570/2011 to the Commission Rule 117 James Elles (ECR) (18 February 2011) Subject: Rights of private keepers under European Directive 92/65/EEC In the context of Directive 92/65/EEC, laying down animal health requirements governing trade in and imports into the Community of animals, could the Commission answer the following question: When and how was Directive 92/65/EEC implemented in the UK? How are the rights of private keepers who wish to import primates into the EU from third countries defined in relation to this Directive?
