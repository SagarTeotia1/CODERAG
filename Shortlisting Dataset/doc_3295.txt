CONTENTS PROCEDURAL PAGE LEGISLATIVE PROPOSAL DRAFT LEGISLATIVE RESOLUTION PROCEDURAL PAGE By letter of 17 July 2002 the Council consulted Parliament, pursuant to Article 37 of the EC Treaty, on the proposal for a Council regulation setting the limits to the Community financing of work programmes drawn up by approved operators' organisations in the olive sector provided for in Regulation (EC) No 1638/98 and derogating from Regulation No 136/66/EEC ( COM(2002) 343 - 2002/0134 (CNS)). At the sitting of 2 September 2002 the President of Parliament announced that he had referred this proposal to the Committee on Agriculture and Rural Development as the committee responsible ( C5-0353/2002 ). At its meeting of 11 July 2002 the Committee on Agriculture and Rural Development decided to apply the simplified procedure under Rule 158(1), thus appointing its chairman rapporteur. It considered the Commission proposal and draft report at its meeting of 11 September 2002. At the latter meeting it adopted the draft legislative resolution unanimously.
