Question for written answer E-006003/2011 to the Commission Rule 117 Marina Yannakoudakis (ECR) (20 June 2011) Subject: Refugee camps of Tindouf in south-west Algeria What efforts are being undertaken by the Commission to attempt to protect and uphold the fundamental human rights of the population in the refugee camps of Tindouf in south-west Algeria, where oppression, exploitation and subjugation prevail and where basic freedoms such as the right to freedom of expression and movement are being denied?
