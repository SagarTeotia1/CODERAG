Question for written answer E-000070/2011 to the Commission Rule 117 Sajjad Karim (ECR) and Malcolm Harbour (ECR) (19 January 2011) Subject: Promoting innovation through copyright policy On 4 November, the British Government announced its plan to review the Intellectual Property Framework to improve its support for innovation and economic growth in the online sector. The review document makes specific reference to ‘fair use’ exceptions. In light of such a political initiative in a leading Member State, what actions does the European Commission plan, to follow up on the 2008 Green Paper on Copyright in the Knowledge Economy, in order to balance enforcement measures with flexible copyright exceptions?
