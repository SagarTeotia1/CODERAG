Question for Question Time H-000221/2011 to the Council Part-session: July 2011 Rule 116 Bernd Posselt (PPE) Subject: Review of the Dayton Accords What is the Council's view of the possibilities for a review of the Dayton Accords, with the aim of achieving a viable federation of all three ethnic groups in Bosnia-Herzegovina? Do the Accords themselves not stipulate that, in the light of experience, such a review must take place?
