Question for written answer E-7883/2010 to the Commission Rule 117 Silvana Koch-Mehrin (ALDE) (27 September 2010) Subject: Commission funding of local radio and television networks In its resolution of 7 September 2010 on ‘journalism and new media — creating a public sphere in Europe’, Parliament notes, in paragraph 33, ‘the Commission’s work with, and funding of, local radio and TV networks […]’ What local radio networks does the Commission fund, in what countries? What local TV networks does the Commission fund, in what countries? Why does the Commission fund local radio and TV networks? What form does the Commission funding for each of these local radio and TV networks take? What is the annual outlay on funding for each of these local radio and TV networks, and since when has the funding been in place? What proportion of these local radio and TV networks’ overall financing does the Commission funding constitute in each case? What conditions or criteria do local radio and TV networks have to meet to be eligible for Commission funding? How does the Commission ensure that such conditions are met? How does the Commission ensure that the funding is put to proper use? What form does the Commission’s work with local radio and TV networks take?
