As concerns the European Initiative for Democracy and Human Rights (EIDHR), appropriations are increased by some 8 million to a total of EUR 130, 7 million. This initiative maintains separate budget lines but the titles of these now reflect the Commission's proposal to integrate human rights into the four main instruments. The EP is requesting that a proposal for a separate legal base is presented, which would necessitate adjustments to the titles of these lines. 74.
