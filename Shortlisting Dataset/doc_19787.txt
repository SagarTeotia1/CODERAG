P5_TA(2001)0469 Instrument for structural policies for pre-accession * (procedure without debate) A5-0295/2001 Proposal for a Council regulation amending Regulation (EC) No 1267/1999 establishing an Instrument for Structural Policies for Pre-Accession ( COM(2001) 110 - C5-0152/2001 - 2001/0058(CNS) ) The proposal was amended as follows: Text proposed by the Commission OJ C 180 E, 26.6.2001, p. 197. Amendments by Parliament Amendment 1 RECITAL 8 (8) The provisions of Regulation (EC) No 1267/1999 should moreover be adapted to take account of Council Decision 1999/468/EC of 28 June 1999 laying down procedures for the exercise of implementing powers conferred on the Commission. (8) The provisions of Regulation (EC) No 1267/1999 should moreover be adapted to take account of Council Decision 1999/468/EC of 28 June 1999 laying down procedures for the exercise of implementing powers conferred on the Commission , which requires the Commission to inform the European Parliament on a regular basis of committee proceedings, to transmit to it documents related to activities of committees, and to inform it whenever the Commission transmits to the Council measures or proposals for measures to be taken. Amendment 2 ARTICLE 1, PARAGRAPH 1 A (new) Article 7, paragraph 1 (Regulation (EC) No 1267/1999) 1a. Article 7 (1) is replaced by the following: “1. The Commission shall adopt decisions on the measures to be financed under ISPA and on the participation of third countries in invitations to tender and contracts in accordance with the procedure laid down in Article 14.” Amendment 3 ARTICLE 1, PARAGRAPH 2 A (new) Article 9, paragraph 1, point (c) (Regulation (EC) No 1267/1999) 2a. Article 9(1)(c) is replaced by the following: “(c) to prevent irregularities and take action against them; to notify the Commission immediately when serious irregularities are suspected; and to inform it regularly of all relevant administrative or criminal investigations and allow officials of the European Anti-Fraud Office (OLAF) and the European Court of Auditors, where they so request, to have access to the inquiry reports in question;” Amendment 4 ARTICLE 1, PARAGRAPH 2 B (new) Article 9, paragraph 1, point (da) (new) (Regulation (EC) No 1267/1999) 2b. The following point (da) is added to paragraph 1 of Article 9: “(da) to make, not later than 1 January 2002, corruption of officials called upon to administer Community funds, and conflicts of interest involving those officials, punishable offences;” Amendment 5 ARTICLE 1, PARAGRAPH 2 C (new) Article 9, paragraph 5a (new) (Regulation (EC) No 1267/1999) 2c. The following paragraph 5a is added to Article 9: “5a. Not later than 30 March 2002 the Commission shall submit a progress report to the European Parliament and the Council on compliance with the requirements imposed on beneficiary countries under paragraph 1.” Amendment 6 ARTICLE 1, PARAGRAPH 3 A (new) Annex V, point 9 (Regulation (EC) No 1267/1999) 3a.
