1999/C 348 E/055 WRITTEN QUESTION P-0258/99 by Joan Colom i Naval (PSE) to the Commission (5 February 1999) Subject: Alleged ESF fraud in Catalonia The High Court in Catalonia has agreed to hear a lawsuit concerning claims of ongoing corruption stemming from alleged irregularities in the processing of assistance from the European Social Fund in Catalonia. To what extent is the Commission aware of this situation? Have its services, in particular UCLAF, taken any measures to deal with the alleged fraud?
