2002/C 205 E/017 WRITTEN QUESTION P-2980/01 by Emmanouil Bakopoulos (GUE/NGL) to the Commission (19 October 2001) Subject: Harmonisation of Regulation (EC) 925/1999 At the recent meeting of the ICAO in Montreal, Canada (25 September-5 October 2001), an agreement was reached on noise and pollution caused by civil aircraft. What measures will the Commission take to bring Regulation (EC) 925/1999 OJ L 115, 4.5.1999, p. 1 . into line with this agreement? Can the Commission also say whether this ICAO decision allays the public's concern over pollution and noise caused by aircraft?
