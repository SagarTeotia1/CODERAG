Moreover, in the strategy for the effective implementation of the Charter of Fundamental Rights by the European Union COM(2010)573 final. the Commission has explained that it would ensure that the EU’s approach to fundamental rights in legislation would be exemplary. As one means to achieve this, the Commission will assess the impacts which its legislative proposals may have on fundamental rights, such as the right to non-discrimination, in its impact assessments. In this context, the Commission published on 6 May 2011 an Operational Guidance documents Operational guidance on taking account of fundamental rights in Commission impact assessments. Commission Staff Working Paper, SEC2011 (567). to its departments on how to assess impact on fundamental rights in practice and how to ensure that EU legislation respects the Charter of Fundamental Rights. It provides that when interpreting the meaning and scope of the rights of persons with disabilities in the Charter, the UNCPRD should be taken into account.
