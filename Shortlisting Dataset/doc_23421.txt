TRAN Efficient, sustainable and multimodal transport The Commission proposes a revision of the TEN-T guidelines in order to tackle the current obstacles to the realisation of an efficient, sustainable and multimodal Trans-European Transport Network. The two co-Rapporteurs, Georgios Koumoutsakos (EPP, GR) and Ismail Ertug (S&D, DE), will present their first impressions on the proposal in the TRAN Committee on 27.02.12.
