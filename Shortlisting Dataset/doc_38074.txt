Question for written answer E-006303/2011 to the Commission Rule 117 Georgios Papanikolaou (PPE) (30 June 2011) Subject: Attainment of a 12.5 % target for adult lifelong learning The European Union has set a target for average participation in lifelong learning programmes for adults of 12.5 % for 2010, up from 9.5 % in 2008. I ask the Commission: — To what extent has this target been achieved and, according to the Commission, to what is any deviation due? — Which Member States show the best performance in the percentage of adults participating in lifelong learning programmes and what is the situation in Greece?
