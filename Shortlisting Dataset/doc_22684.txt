WRITTEN QUESTION E-1912/07 by Proinsias De Rossa (PSE) to the Commission (11 April 2007) Subject: Hunting of birds in Malta Could the Commission indicate whether it has received the response from the Maltese authorities to the letter of formal notice (first warning) sent to Malta in July 2006 concerning possible breaches of the Wild Birds Directive (79/409/EEC OJ L 103, 25.4.1979, p. 1. ) by Malta which was due by November 2006? If so, what was the nature of that response? What action has the Commission taken, or is it planning to take in this matter? Can it confirm that it has opened a second complaint against the Maltese authorities concerning problems regarding the enforcement of illegal hunting rules? What is the current status of this second complaint?
