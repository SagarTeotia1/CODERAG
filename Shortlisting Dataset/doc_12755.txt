Under the draft, the ECB's structure, tasks and powers would, however, remain the same as in the EC Treaty. In particular, it would retain its legal personality and full independence in relation to both the EU institutions and the Member States (Articles 107 and 108 of the Treaty on the Functioning of the Union).
