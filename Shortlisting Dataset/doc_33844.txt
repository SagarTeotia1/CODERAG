Answer given by High Representative/Vice-President Ashton on behalf of the Commission (31 August 2010) The Commission is aware of the difficult domestic situation in Iran, including as regards fundamental freedoms and the right to independent education. The European Union continues to raise its voice in defence of human rights in Iran. Several statements on Iran and its deteriorating Human Rights situation have been issued by High Representative/Vice President Ashton in the last months. Alongside these statements, several diplomatic meetings on human rights issues both in Tehran and in Brussels have taken place at EU request.
