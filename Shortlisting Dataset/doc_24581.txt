Question for written answer E-001947/2011 to the Commission Rule 117 Liam Aylward (ALDE) (8 March 2011) Subject: Web accessibility for the visually impaired One in six people in the EU (around 80 million) have a disability. Only around 5% of public websites comply with web accessibility standards for those who are blind or whose eyesight is partially impaired. What steps is the Commission taking to ensure that the disability strategy is applied, either through the proposed European Accessibility Act or by other means? What action can be taken to increase the number of public websites with acceptable accessibility standards?
