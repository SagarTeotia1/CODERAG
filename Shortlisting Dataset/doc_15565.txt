The culture and education committee is responsible for budget lines which are spread over different policy areas of the PDB: 09 Information society and media; 15 Education and culture; 16 Communication; 26 Administration. The MFF includes these areas within three different headings: Heading 1a ("Competitiveness for growth and employment") and 3b ("Citizenship") cover operational expenditure, Heading 5 covers administration expenditure. 5. Policy area 9 - Information society and Media: The 2007 PDB proposes an overall increase for this area of merely 1.9% compared to 2006 (commitments). Within this area the Culture and education committee is responsible for different budget lines under 09 01 (staff and management) and 09 06 (i2010 - Audiovisual policy and MEDIA programme). The PDB proposes commitments of 74.862 m for operational expenditure of the MEDIA programme ( 84.810 in 2006 ). 6. Policy area 15 - Education and Culture: The 2007 PDB proposes an overall increase for this area of 22.19% compared to 2006 (commitments). The new programme Lifelong learning combines activities related to education and to vocational training with four different target groups (secondary education; third level education; vocational training; adult education) and horizontal activities such as language learning and ICT, Jean Monnet Action on European integration. The PDB proposes commitments of 820.9 million for 2007 (629.976 in 2006) for operational expenditure for Lifelong Learning (Line 15 02 22), and 55.6 (from 38.111 in 2006) for the programme Erasmus Mundus (Line 15 02 02 05). The PDB proposes a budget of 47.8m in commitments for the Culture 2007 programme (15 04 44). The completion of previous programmes in the field of culture and language in 2006 had an amount of 38.086m on the line.
