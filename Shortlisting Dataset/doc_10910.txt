ORAL QUESTION H-0959/06 for Question Time at the part-session in December 2006 pursuant to Rule 109 of the Rules of Procedure by Manuel Medina Ortega to the Council Subject: Immigration strategies Given the scale of world-wide migratory movement, and the demographic and ageing problems of the countries of the European Union, what strategies does the Council intend to pursue in order to regulate immigration into Europe in the immediate future?
