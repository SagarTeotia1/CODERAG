Answer given by Mr Tajani on behalf of the Commission (7 December 2011) The Commission would like to inform the Honourable Member that it has indeed received a complaint on the alleged infringement of EC law, notably provisions of Directive 98/34/EC laying down a procedure for the provision of information in the field of technical standards and regulations as amended by Directive 98/48/EC (‘Transparency Directive’) and Directive 2000/31/EC on certain legal aspects of information society services, in particular electronic commerce, in the internal market (‘E-Commerce Directive’), in relation to the provision of premium rate services in Ireland. The Commission is currently examining the factual and legal background of the complaint.
