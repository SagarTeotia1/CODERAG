Jamila Madeira SUGGESTIONS The Committee on Employment and Social Affairs calls on the Committee on Budgetary Control, as the committee responsible, to incorporate the following suggestions in its motion for a resolution: 1. Expresses its satisfaction at the proper implementation of the 2006 budget; 2. Notes that the European Centre for the Development of Vocational Training (Cedefop) has been endeavouring to strengthen and develop its internal supervision system, enhancing the effectiveness of its role and operational activities; 3. Notes that steps have been taken to address and resolve the reservations initially expressed by the Court of Auditors about an individual decision of the Appeals Committee concerning the payment of compensation to an official for non-pecuniary damage; 4. Believes that Cedefop plays an important role in disseminating information at local, regional, national, and Community level, and in relation to civil society; and urges it to become more effective still in fulfilling its purpose; again applauds the work carried out by Cedefop, which is performing the tasks entrusted to it, unstintingly and to invaluable effect; 5. Considers, in the light of the foregoing, that discharge can be given for implementation of the budget of the European Centre for the Development of Vocational Training for the financial year 2006.
