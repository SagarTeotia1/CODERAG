WRITTEN QUESTION E-1463/07 by Robert Kilroy-Silk (NI) to the Commission (21 March 2007) Subject: Con trick of Open Skies deal Will the Commission comment on the statement by the Chairman of British Airways, Martin Broughton, that the draft Open Skies agreement demonstrates that the Commission has fallen for a ‘con trick’ by ‘greedy Americans’, and that it gives too much away to American airlines with nothing in return?
