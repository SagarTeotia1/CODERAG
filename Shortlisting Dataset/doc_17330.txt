Question for written answer E-4035/2010 to the Commission Rule 117 Claude Moraes (S&D) (7 June 2010) Subject: The EU strategy for sport and the London 2012 Olympics Given the strengthened role of the Commission in the area of sport under the Lisbon Treaty, how is the Commission planning on integrating the London 2012 Olympics into its overall strategy for sport over the next two years? In particular, does the Commission agree that the 2012 Olympics offer a good opportunity to highlight the ways in which sport can make a positive contribution to other areas of EU competence, including health, social inclusion and equal opportunities, volunteering and the fight against racism? How does the Commission plan on ensuring that the opportunities presented by the 2012 Olympics in this regard are maximised?
