WRITTEN QUESTION E-1693/07 by Christopher Heaton-Harris (PPE‑DE) to the Commission (30 March 2007) Subject: DG Justice, Freedom and Security In order to ascertain the cost-effectiveness of campaigns run by the DG, can the Commissioner provide further details on how particular aspects of the budget were spent for the last two years for which figures are available? This relates to the following lines (coded as per the 2006 budget): — 18 04 01 02 — Which NGOs are funded in the UK under Daphne II, particularly for policy development and public information campaigns? — 18 04 03 — Can the Commissioner list the UK experts on fundamental rights and the organisations from which they hail? — 18 04 05 02 — Can the Commissioner list the policy documents and public information material in English that have been published in English by the Monitoring Centre on Racism and Xenophobia? — 18 05 01 03 — How many UK judges have participated, and at what level of seniority; what does the exchange programme consist of from an EU level input; what has been spent on the development of common learning and teaching materials, with what end result? — 18 05 04 — What has been provided to UK nationals? — 18 05 05 — Does any training in the European Police College involve material related to the broader ideology of European integration? — 18 06 01 02 — What information campaigns were run to improve public awareness?
