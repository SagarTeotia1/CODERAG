Answer given by Mrs Fischer Boel on behalf of the Commission (3 April 2008) The Commission can confirm that operators - rather than Member States - will be allowed the freedom to decide where and how the EU logo should be placed on the organic products, if they respect the specific criteria as regards presentation, composition, size and design that will be described in the implementing rules of Commission Regulation (EC) No 834/2007 Council Regulation (EC) No 834/2007 of 28 June 2007 on organic production and labelling of organic products and repealing Regulation (EEC) No 2092/91, OJ L 189, 20.7.2007 . , currently in discussion between the Member States and the Commission at the Standing Committee of Organic Farming. The Member States and the private bodies will be free to continue to use their marking systems for organic food as mentioned in Article 25 paragraph 2 of Council Regulation (EC) No 834/2007 on organic production and labelling of organic products.
