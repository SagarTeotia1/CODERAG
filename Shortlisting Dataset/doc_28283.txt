Question for written answer E-004371/2011 to the Commission Rule 117 Mara Bizzotto (EFD) (4 May 2011) Subject: Nuclear power plants in the EU In the wake of the Fukushima disaster and in view of the energy supply problems that might arise from the political upheavals in the Maghreb, nuclear technology has returned to the fore and is once again at the centre of impassioned debates throughout the EU. The arguments for and against nuclear power, and the risks and benefits entailed, are being weighed up at both national and EU level. To give a complete picture of the current situation, can the Commission supply the following information: — number and location of the Member States’ nuclear power plants now in operation; — number and location of the Member States’ nuclear power plants that have been decommissioned; — number and location of the Member States’ nuclear power plants in the process of decommissioning; — number and location of nuclear power plants under construction in Member States?
