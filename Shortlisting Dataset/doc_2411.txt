9999/C Q999 E/028 WRITTEN QUESTION E-0477/04 by Christopher Heaton-Harris (PPE-DE) to the Commission (09 February 2004) Subject: Banana trade In 1998 the EU launched a three-year 'New Banana Recovery programme'. Please could the Commission confirm if the productivity of bananas has been increased or decreased since this date? Has an inquiry taken place following the three-year programme to ascertain if it was a success? If so, please give details. Following the end of the recovery programme was a new Banana Recovery programme launched? If so, please give details. Who approved this programme? Were any MEPs substantially involved in the Banana Recovery programme? If so, please give details.
