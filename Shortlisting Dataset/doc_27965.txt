ORAL QUESTION H-1056/06 for Question Time at the part-session in February 2007 pursuant to Rule 109 of the Rules of Procedure by Marie Panayotopoulos-Cassiotou to the Council Subject: Demographic developments in Europe In the light of the demographic changes in Europe and the growing interest expressed by the European Union in resolving the demographic problem, will the German Presidency say whether it intends to draw up a European Pact on the quality of family life, along the lines of the European Youth Pact (22-23 March 2005) and the European Gender Equality Pact (23-24 March 2006)?
