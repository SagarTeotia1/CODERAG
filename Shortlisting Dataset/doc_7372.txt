DEVE Hearing on environmental degradation The Committee on Development will hold a hearing on "Environmental degradation and its impact on poverty", on 29 February 2012 (ASP 3G2) from 10 to 12.30. The hearing will examine the environment-poverty-development nexus as well as the role of the European Union in addressing the impact of environmental degradation on poverty through its development aid. The hearing will also examine this issue with reference to two specific cases presented by two experts: the social and environmental effects of oil spills and toxic substances generated from electronic waste.
