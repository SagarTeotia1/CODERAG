ORAL QUESTION H-0256/03 for Question Time at the part-session in May 2003 pursuant to Rule 43 of the Rules of Procedure by Proinsias De Rossa to the Commission Subject: Sellafied nuclear plant Is the Commission aware of the proceedings taken by Ireland against Britain before at least one international tribunal arising from the operation of nuclear processes at the BNFL plant at Sellafield? Could it indicate whether the EU or any of its institutions is a party or has sought to intervene in these proceedings; whether any provision of EC or EU law is or might be made applicable to this case; and whether it has adopted a position on the merits of the proceedings and if so, could it set out that position?
