Question for written answer P-5161/2010 to the Commission Rule 117 Dan Jørgensen (S&D) (1 July 2010) Subject: Transport of hazardous waste between Denmark and Australia An EU Commission spokesman has been quoted in the Danish press as saying that a shipment of hazardous waste from Australia to Denmark should be permitted under the Basel Convention, cf. http://www.dr.dk/Regioner/Fyn/Nyheder/Fynogoer/2010/06/19/104358. Could the Commission confirm or deny that it is giving the ‘green light’ to this shipment?
