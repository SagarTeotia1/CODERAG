ORAL QUESTION WITH DEBATE O-0087/09 pursuant to Rule 115 of the Rules of Procedure by Gabriele Albertini , on behalf of the Committee on Foreign Affairs to the Commission Subject: Development of the Nabucco gas pipeline On 13 July, four Member States and Turkey signed an agreement in Ankara paving the way for the Nabucco pipeline in order to reduce Europe's dependence on Russian gas. A month later (6 August), Turkish Prime Minister Erdogan signed a protocol with Russian Prime Minister Putin to start exploration work on the South Stream pipeline, which also concerns the Southern Corridor. How does the Commission assess the parallel launch of these two projects? What concrete steps is the Commission taking in order to support Nabucco? How does the Commission plan to address this issue with Turkey, which plays a crucial and strategic role as regards the development of the South Corridor?
