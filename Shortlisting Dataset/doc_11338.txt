Answer given by Mrs Reding on behalf of the Commission (9 November 2011) The Commission would refer the Honourable Member to its answer to Written Question E‑1463/2011 http://www.europarl.europa.eu/QP-WEB . Moreover, as already stated in its answer to Written Question E‑5271/2011 http://www.europarl.europa.eu/QP-WEB , the Commission rejects all forms of violent radicalisation or extremism but also strongly warns against stigmatising any religious or ethnic communities.
