Question for written answer E-006317/2011 to the Commission Rule 117 Marc Tarabella (S&D) (30 June 2011) Subject: Principle of pre-listing 1. Is the Commission legally obliged to apply the principle of pre-listing for establishments in third countries authorised to export foodstuffs, animal feed or animal by-products into the EU? 2. Can it say which third countries, out of the 15 main exporters of these products to the EU, benefit from the pre-listing system and why it is not used for establishments from the EU? 3. With which of the countries referred to above has the EU signed a free-trade agreement regarding sanitary and phytosanitary matters?
