Answer given by Ms GrybauskaitÄ— on behalf of the Commission (10 March 2006) The new accounting framework and rules apply to all financial transactions of the Commission, including agricultural and structural fund spending, so that they are properly recorded in the general accounts of the Commission. The budgetary accounts are prepared in accordance with the Financial Regulation Council Regulation (EC, Euratom) No 1605/2002 of 25 June 2002 on the Financial Regulation applicable to the general budget of the European Communities, OJ L 248, 16.9.2002. and so were not impacted by the change in the general accounting rules.
