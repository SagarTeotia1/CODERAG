P5_TA(2001)0127 Expulsions of third-country nationals * A5-0065/2001 Draft Council directive on mutual recognition of decisions on the expulsion of third country nationals (10130/2000 - C5-0398/2000 + 13968/2000 - C5-0004/2001 - 2000/0819(CNS) ) The draft was rejected. European Parliament legislative resolution on the Draft Council directive on mutual recognition of decisions on the expulsion of third country nationals (10130/2000 - C5-0398/2000 + 13968/2000 - C5-0004/2001 - 2000/0819(CNS) ) (Consultation procedure) The European Parliament, - having regard to the initiative of the French Republic (10130/2000 OJ C 243, 24.8.2000, p. 1. ), - having regard to the Council Draft (13968/2000), - having regard to Article 63(3) of the EC Treaty, - having been consulted by the Council pursuant to Article 67 of the EC Treaty ( C5-0004/2001 ), - having regard to Rule 67 of its Rules of Procedure, - having regard to the report of the Committee on Citizens' Freedoms and Rights, Justice and Home Affairs and the opinion of the Committee on Petitions ( A5-0065/2001 ), 1. Rejects the Council Draft; 2. Calls on the French Republic to withdraw its initiative; 3. Instructs its President to forward its position to the Council, the Commission and Government of the French Republic.
