2004/C 783 E/134 WRITTEN QUESTION E-3475/03 by Mihail Papayannakis (GUE/NGL) to the Commission (24 November 2003) Subject: Protection of the Caretta caretta turtle Despite repeated expressions of concern regarding the Caretta caretta turtle in Zakynthos, and the decision regarding the operation of the Zakynthos marine park, the situation, according to reliable information, appears to be worsening rather than improving. Following the Commission's recent contacts, visits and inspections can it indicate what results were obtained and what action it intends to take to ensure progress regarding protection for the turtle and the practical functioning of the marine park?
