Answer given by Mrs Hübner on behalf of the Commission (6 July 2005) 1. The works mentioned in the question raised by the Honourable Member are financed under measure 3.15 of the operational programme ‘Operação Norte’. 2. The total eligible investment is of EUR 29079983 .00 of which EUR 15993990 .65 are co‑financed by the European Regional Development Fund (corresponding to a ERDF co‑financing rate of 55 %) and the remaining EUR 13085992 .35 are co-financed by national public expenditure. The building of these sea walls at the mouth of Douro River must be completed before the end of 2008. No particular payment conditions are attached to this action.
