In relation to occupational exposure, it is not intended to extend its scope to issues of occupational chemical safety. Rather, occupational chemical safety of workers in the sense of Article 3 of the framework Directive 89/391/EEC OJ L 183, 29.6.1989, as amended. is ensured by several Directives such as Directive 89/654/EEC ‘Working place’ OJ L 393, 30.12.1989. , Directive 89/656/EEC ‘Personal protective equipment’ OJ L 393, 30.12.1989. and Directive 98/24/EC ‘Chemical agents’ OJ L 131, 5.5.1998. . On the basis of the existing legislation, the Commission does not consider it appropriate to propose any specific measures for professional users of hair dye products. The existing regulatory framework provides for the necessary tools to ensure the safety of the consumers and the professional users, which have to be fully applied and enforced.
