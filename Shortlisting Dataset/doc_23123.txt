Question for written answer E-007890/2011 to the Commission Rule 117 Mara Bizzotto (EFD) (5 September 2011) Subject: ‘Spazio-Europa’ in Rome The Commission has funded an initiative called ‘Spazio-Europa’, which is a public space in central Rome run by Parliament’s information office in Italy and the Commission representation in Italy in order to promote awareness and discussion of European issues, in particular those relevant to young people. Can the Commission say: 1. Who is running ‘Spazio-Europa’? 2. How many people are involved in running it? 3. How many young people actually make use of it? 4. How many other such initiatives there are in the EU? 5. How much such initiatives cost the Commission? 6. Whether this initiative has been launched in other Member States?
