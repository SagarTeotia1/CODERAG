WRITTEN QUESTION E-0362/10 by Ilda Figueiredo (GUE/NGL) to the Commission (8 February 2010) Subject: Stage reached on the ‘European economic recovery plan’ At the end of November 2008, the Commission launched what it called the ‘European economic recovery plan’ — COM(2008)0800. In particular, this plan envisaged speeding up investment aimed at modernising European infrastructure, improving energy efficiency in buildings, promoting the swift adoption of ‘environmentally-friendly products’, boosting investment in R & D, innovation and education, developing clean technologies in the automobile and construction sectors and expanding high-speed Internet for all. Can the Commission provide the following information: 1. What stage has been reached on this plan? 2. What projects have been approved and what funding has the Commission provided in each of the European Union Member States? 3. What appropriations have already been transferred to each Member State? 4. What other projects, actions and Community funding have been guaranteed on an exceptional basis to combat the crisis?
