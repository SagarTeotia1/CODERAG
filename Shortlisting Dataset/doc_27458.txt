WRITTEN QUESTION E-3613/05 by Claude Moraes (PSE) to the Commission (12 October 2005) Subject: Fuel poverty Does the Commission have any current proposals, or does it hold a view on the issue of fuel poverty, that is the phenomenon of certain sections of EU citizens being unable to afford basic heating and lighting? Is the Commission aware of multinational European campaigns to ensure that electricity and gas suppliers are also socially responsible in their pricing and services to vulnerable EU citizens?
