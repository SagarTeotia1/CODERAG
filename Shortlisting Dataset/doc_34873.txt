Question for written answer E-001208/2011 to the Commission Rule 117 Vilija Blinkevičiūtė (S&D) (15 February 2011) Subject: Regulation of the number of third country seasonal workers On 13 July 2010 the Commission submitted a proposal for a directive on the conditions of entry and residence for third country seasonal workers. The directive should apply to unskilled workers. Bearing in mind the subsidiarity principle, what does the Commission think about the fact that Member States will be able to lay down the number of migrant seasonal workers?
