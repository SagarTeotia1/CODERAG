Answer given by High Representative / Vice President Ashton on behalf of the Commission (1 July 2010) The issue referred to by the Honourable Member has not been discussed in the context of the Common European Security and Defence Policy. The Commission is aware of the statements made by the Russian authorities. The Commission wishes to recall that military issues lie outside its sphere of competence. It is not aware of other aspects of this possible sale which would call for an intervention by the Commission.
