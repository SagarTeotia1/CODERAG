Answer given by Mrs de Palacio on behalf of the Commission (15 January 2004) 1. The minimal vision requirements for obtaining a driving licence can be found in Article 6 of Annex III of Council Directive 91/439/EEC of 29 July 1991 OJ L 237, 24.8.1991 . . The Commission does not anticipate proposing any revisions to this Directive on the question of minimum vision standards. 2. All Member States have introduced visual requirements that correspond with the minimum requirements of the above mentioned Directive. As the Honourable Member has been informed of in the Commission's reply to his previous questions E-1231/03 OJ C 58 E, 6.3.2004, p. 52 . , E-2568/03 OJ C 58 E, 6.3.2004, p. 181 . and E-2569/03 See page 129. , a special working group for vision has been established in 2003 which plans to screen the legislation/actual situation in the different Member States during the coming year 2004. The results of this examination will be made public by the Commission as soon as they are available.
