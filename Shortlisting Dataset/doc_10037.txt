The opinion poll contained fifteen or so questions on the reconstruction of Iraq (administration, financing, political transition, security, military aspects, humanitarian aid), on the Union's role on the international stage (impact of the war in Iraq, peace process in the Middle East, development of cultural relations with the Arab world, closeness to the foreign policy of the United States) and on the perception of certain risks (terrorism, countries regarded as posing a threat to peace). This opinion poll captures the attitudes of individuals in the Union to these various aspects. The findings, broken down by country and by socio-demographic variable, have been made available to decision-makers, journalists, the scientific community and any interested individual.
