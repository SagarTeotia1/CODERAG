DROI Joint AFET / DEVE / DROI meeting with 2009 Sakharov Prize Laureates At an extraordinary joint meeting in Strasbourg, the Committees on Foreign Affairs, on Development and the Subcommittee on Human Rights will hold an exchange of views with Mr Oleg Orlov, Mr Sergei Kovalev and Mrs Lyudmila Alexeyeva on behalf of MEMORIAL and all other human rights defenders in Russia , laureate of the Sakharov Prize for the Freedom of Thought in 2009. The meeting will take place on Thursday, 17.12.2009 from 09h00 - 10h30 in room LOW N1.4. and will be webstreamed. 20091209CAN66245
