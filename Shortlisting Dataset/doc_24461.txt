Answer given by Mrs Reding on behalf of the Commission (1 July 2011) The Commission is currently assessing information provided by the Maltese authorities with respect to maternity leave and the transposition of Council Directive 92/85/EEC Council Directive 92/85/EEC of 19 October 1992 on the introduction of measures to encourage improvements in the safety and health at work of pregnant workers and workers who have recently given birth or are breastfeeding (tenth individual Directive within the meaning of Article 16 (1) of Directive 89/391/EEC), OJ L 348, 28.11.1992. . If, once this information is assessed, Malta is found not to be in compliance with EC law and the decision of the Court of Justice of the EU, then the Commission will proceed to take all necessary steps to ensure the full compliance of the Maltese authorities.
