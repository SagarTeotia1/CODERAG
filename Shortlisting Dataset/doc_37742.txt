WRITTEN QUESTION E-6123/08 by Georgios Georgiou (IND/DEM) to the Council (12 November 2008) Subject: A European Coast Guard Greece has received between 1.5 and 2 million immigrants who have arrived by many different means. For years citizens have been hearing about the creation of a European Coast Guard, but it is still at the planning stage. Will the Commission say when the European Coast Guard will become operational?
