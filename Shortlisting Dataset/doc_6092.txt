Recently, however, a court in the capital, Harare, rejected the Tribunal's ruling, allowing the Zimbabwe Government simply to continue with its land seizures. Is the Commission aware of the ruling by the SADC Tribunal and if so, what view does it take of it? Must Zimbabwe implement the ruling? If not, why not?
