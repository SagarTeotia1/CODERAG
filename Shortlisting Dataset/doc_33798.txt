2004/C 78 E/009 WRITTEN QUESTION E-3652/02 by Hanja Maij-Weggen (PPE-DE) to the Commission (18 December 2002) Subject: El Kosheh retrial Is the Commission aware that the final hearing of the retrial concerning the violence in El Kosheh which took place in January 2000 is due to be heard in January 2003? What efforts has the Commission made since this violent episode in its relations with the Egyptian authorities to encourage increased religious tolerance and communal peace? Will the Commission urge the Egyptian authorities to ensure that all the available evidence is thoroughly examined and that a just verdict is delivered?
