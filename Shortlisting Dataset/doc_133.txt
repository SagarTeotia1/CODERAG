WRITTEN QUESTION E-3144/06 by Alyn Smith (Verts/ALE) to the Commission (11 July 2006) Subject: Publishing industry Following the Commission's public consultation on future policy orientations for the publishing sector in 2005, can the Commission outline what considerations it has given to the stakeholder's responses and what future actions it plans to take in this area? Can the Commission also list all present means employed by (a) the Commission, (b) each of the 25 Member States of the European Union and (c) any regional and/or local body/authority, which have been used across the European Union to promote or support the publishing sector?
