Answer given by Mrs Fischer Boel on behalf of the Commission (26 January 2005) The Commission would like to inform the Honourable Member that LEADER+, the Community Initiative for rural development, encourages the implementation of integrated, high-quality and original strategies for sustainable development. In that context actions and projects that accommodate the selected development strategy can be co-financed under LEADER+. According to information provided by the Member States, under the Greek Operational Programme Community Initiative (O.P.C.I.) LEADER+ one contract has been signed for this type of activity with a total European Agriculture Guidance and Guarantee Fund (EAGGF) contribution of € 129,500. Of this amount, € 65,879.184 has been already paid to the beneficiary. As regards other Member States, this type of activity may be supported in Belgium, Poland and France.
