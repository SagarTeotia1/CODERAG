14 February 2001 MOTION FOR A RESOLUTION pursuant to Rule 48 of the Rules of Procedure by Salvador Garriga Polledo on protection of the Spanish language as a part of the Community's cultural heritage Motion for a European Parliament resolution on protection of the Spanish language as a part of the Community's cultural heritage The European Parliament, A. whereas in 2001 the European Year of Languages will be celebrated throughout the Community, B. whereas Spanish, spoken by almost 400 million people, is the third most widelyâ€‘spoken language in the world after Mandarin Chinese and English, C. whereas over 46 million people worldwide are learning Spanish, and 20 million Spanish speakers regularly use the Internet, D. whereas such an important part of the Community's cultural heritage deserves special attention in order to promote its growth worldwide, and must be one of the media the EU uses to communicate its message to the world, 1. Calls for the EU to assist in improving the quality of Spanish teaching throughout the world; 2. Calls for the Community to assist in a general coordination of Spanish language policy; 3. Recommends recognising the importance, in economic and strategic terms, of a mastery of language engineering with regard to Spanish so that it may serve as one of the media the European Union uses to communicate its message throughout the world.
