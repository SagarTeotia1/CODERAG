DEVE The 15th session of the ACP-EU JPA will meet in Ljubljana, Slovenia, from 17-20 March The three Standing Committees will meet on Saturday 15 March and the Plenary session will start from Monday 17 March. On the plenary agenda the following main items will feature: Question Time to Council and Commission, a major debate on the EPA negotiations and urgent debates with resolution on the Situation in Chad and in Kenya.
