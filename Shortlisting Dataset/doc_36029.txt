WRITTEN QUESTION E-6366/07 by Francisco José Millán Mon (PPE‑DE) to the Council (18 December 2007) Subject: Remarks by Cuban deputy minister According to media reports, Cuba's Deputy Minister for Foreign Affairs, Mr Eumelio Caballero, said in Havana on 28 November 2007 that the EU does not have an independent policy on Cuba, thanks to US pressure, and called on the Union to remove the measures implemented against Cuba in 2003 in order to pave the way for a ‘respectful dialogue between the parties’. Can the Council state its position on these remarks?
