At the Community level, the Commission — as represented by OLAF — plays a central lead role in the organisation and coordination of the anti-fraud strategy. By utilising specific Community legislation Council Regulation (EC) No 515/97 of 13 March 1997 on mutual assistance between the administrative authorities of the Members States and cooperation between the latter and the Commission to ensure the correct application of the law on customs and agricultural matters, OJ L 82, 22.3.1997. in this sector as well as the administrative cooperation possibilities opened up by the conclusion of administrative cooperation arrangements with the Community’s external trading partner countries, a range of anti-fraud measures at both national and international levels is achieved.
