The Commission has put forward a proposal on food information to consumers COM(2008)40 final. which proposes rules for nutrition labelling. It is proposing that the information on trans fatty acids may be provided on a voluntary basis. Health education is within the competence of Member States. The Commission is supporting national efforts. In particular, the Public Health Programme provides support to the sharing of know-how on community based approaches so that promising initiatives can be faster developed in each Member State. Information on projects is available from the projects database of the Executive Agency for Health and Consumers (EAHC) at: http://ec.europa.eu/eahc/projects/database.html Within the EU Platform for Action on Diet, Physical Activity and Health a number of commitments focus on education campaigns in relation to diet and health. They are described in the Platform database at: http://ec.europa.eu/health/ph_determinants/life_style/nutrition/platform
