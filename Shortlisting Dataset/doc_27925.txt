WRITTEN QUESTION P-3179/06 by Daniel Varela Suanzes-Carpegna (PPE‑DE) to the Commission (7 July 2006) Subject: EU‑Morocco fisheries agreement The European Parliament adopted the report on the EU‑Morocco fisheries agreement at its plenary sitting of 16 May 2006. 1. Can the Commission say when the agreement was ratified by the Moroccan Parliament? If it has not yet been ratified, can it explain the reasons for the delay? 2. When did the EU‑Morocco fisheries agreement enter into force, or when is it scheduled to enter into force? 3. Can the Commission say whether the technical aspects that remained outstanding after the agreement was signed (seine fishing with lights, definition of longline fishing, landings at Moroccan ports, etc.) have now been resolved, and can it report to the European Parliament on the outcome? 4. Article 2(4) of the protocol to the agreement stipulated that the first EU payment would be made by 30 June 2006. Has this payment been made? If not, when will it be made and when will Parliament be notified? 5. When will the Commission notify Parliament of technical changes to the text of the agreement as requested by plenary in its report?
