Amendment 63 Proposal for a regulation Article 45 a (new) Article 45a Other accompanying sanctions The sanctions provided for in this Chapter shall be accompanied by other sanctions or measures, in particular: a) a temporary ban for at least the duration of the programming period, or a permanent ban on access to public aid or subsidies; b) the repayment of public aid or subsidies received by IUU vessels during the relevant financial period.
