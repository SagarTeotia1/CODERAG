Question for written answer E-008681/2011 to the Commission Rule 117 Franz Obermayr (NI) (4 October 2011) Subject: EU‑funded mosque building Further to Question No E‑008177/2011 — on ‘EU‑funded mosque building: a spur to integration?’ — can the Commission indicate: 1. how much money from the European Regional Development Fund has gone into the construction of mosques in Europe during the current funding period? 2. what EU monies have been spent during the current funding period on co-financing for building mosques or Islamic facilities centres (meeting places, youth and women’s centres, places of worship etc.)? 3. what is the EU’s total expenditure to date on the construction of mosques? 4. what EU monies are spent annually on the construction of mosques or Islamic facilities?
