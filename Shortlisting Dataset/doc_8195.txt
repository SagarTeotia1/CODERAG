Finally, the Commission considers that Member States should give priority to enforcement and controls in view of preventing illegal power-train modifications of such vehicles. Directive 2000/30/EC on roadside technical checks OJ L 203, 10.8.2000. allows Member States to carry out inspections and to target enforcement activities on these modified minicars. Besides, the Commission is currently evaluating the impact of making roadworthiness testing mandatory for such vehicles and of including them in the scope of roadside technical checks.
