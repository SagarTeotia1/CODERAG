Answer given by Mr Dalli on behalf of the Commission (22 March 2012) While the Environment and Health Strategy COM(2003) 338 final. and its related Action Plan COM(2004) 416 final. (EHAP) are the Commission's main instrument for improving awareness of consequences of respiratory diseases, the Commission is carrying out a reflection process on chronic diseases together with Member States and will consider what further actions are appropriate in the light of its findings. Other relevant Commission actions include EU policies such as on air quality http://ec.europa.eu/environment/air/quality/index.htm , spread of allergic invasive species COM(2006) 216 final. and reduction of tobacco use and environmental tobacco smoke http://ec.europa.eu/health/tobacco/law/free_environments/index_en.htm .
