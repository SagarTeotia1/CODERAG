9999/C Q999 E/038 WRITTEN QUESTION E-1455/04 by Catherine Stihler (PSE) to the Commission (30 April 2004) Subject: Transportation and sale of puppies in Member States Will the European Commission ensure that the welfare of puppies during transport is addressed in the forthcoming Transport Directive? What will the Commission do to protect young puppies being transported between Member States and imported from outwith the European Union for sale in the pet trade?
