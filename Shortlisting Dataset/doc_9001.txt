Question for written answer E-004438/2011 to the Commission Rule 117 Simon Busuttil (PPE) (5 May 2011) Subject: EU terrorist financing tracking programme The Commission's report on the joint review of the implementation of the agreement between the EU and the US on the processing and transfer of financial messaging data for the purposes of the Terrorist Finance Tracking Programme (TFTP) outlined the action taken to date with a view to the possibility of setting up an EU TFTP system as envisaged in Article 11 of the agreement. Further to the action discussed in the report, what are the next steps the Commission will be taking on this issue, and what timeframe does it envisage?
