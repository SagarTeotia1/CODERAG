WRITTEN QUESTION E-1653/07 by Robert Kilroy-Silk (NI) to the Commission (29 March 2007) Subject: Protection of the the rights of children in Romania 1. Will the Commission make a statement on the progress or lack of progress in dealing with the problem of abandonment of children in maternity wards in Romania? 2. Will the Commission make a statement on progress made — or lack of progress made — in dealing with the problems of children with severe disabilities living in residential institutions? 3. Will the Commission give its opinion on Children Protected only on Paper await Real Protection by Pierre Poupard, the Unicef representative in Romania?
