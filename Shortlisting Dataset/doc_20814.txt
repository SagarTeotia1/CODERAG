8 June 2000 MOTION FOR A RESOLUTION further to the Council and Commission statements pursuant to Rule 37(2) of the Rules of Procedure by Enrique Barón Crespo, Klaus Hänsch and Antonio Seguro on behalf of the PSE Group on the preparation of the European Council meeting in Feira on 19 and 20 June 2000 European Parliament resolution on the preparation of the European Council meeting in Feira on 19 and 20 June 2000 The European Parliament, - having heard the statements by the President of the Council and the President of the Commission on the preparation of the Feira European Council, - having regard to the conclusions of the Luxembourg, Berlin, Cardiff, Cologne, Helsinki and Lisbon European Councils, - having regard to its resolutions of 16 December 1999 on the Helsinki European Council and of 15 March 2000 on the Special European Council in Lisbon, - having regard to its resolutions of 18 November 1999 on the preparation of the reform of the Treaties and the next Intergovernmental Conference, of 3 February 2000 on the convening of the Intergovernmental Conference and of 13 April 2000 setting out its proposals for the Intergovernmental Conference, - having regard to the Commission's Recommendation for the Broad Guidelines of the Economic Policies of the Member States and the Community for the year 2000, drawn up in accordance with Article 99(2) of the Treaty establishing the European Community, ( COM(2000) 214 ), - having regard in particular to the European Commission's contribution to the Special European Council held in Lisbon on 23-24 March 2000, entitled an Agenda of Economic and Social Renewal for Europe ( COM(2000) 7 ), - having regard to its resolution of 18 May 2000 on the Broad Economic Guidelines 2000, A. recalling that its resolution of 13 April 2000 makes a plea in favour of a comprehensive reform of the Treaties in order to achieve two essential objectives intrinsically linked to each other: enlargement and deepening, IGC 1. Welcomes the attempt by the Portuguese Presidency to enlarge the IGC agenda and, in accordance with its resolution of 13 April 2000, urges the Heads of State and Government gathering at Feira to be ambitious in dealing with the Conference agenda under the French Presidency; 2. Asks the European Council to give a mandate to the French Presidency to enlarge the IGC agenda to include, in particular: - enhanced cooperation, in case the Union’s future diversity makes differentiated integration a political necessity, - the incorporation into the Treaty of the Charter of Fundamental Rights with a view to giving it binding legal force, - the inclusion in the Treaty of the new institutional aspects of European Security and Defence Policy, - the strengthening of both the coordination and management of economic policies and of the role of Community institutions within Economic and Monetary Union, safeguarding the independence of the European Central Bank; 3.
