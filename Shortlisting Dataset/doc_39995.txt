WRITTEN QUESTION E-0223/06 by Georgios Karatzaferis (IND/DEM) to the Commission (27 January 2006) Subject: Referendums in Greece The last time a democratic referendum was held in Greece was in 1974, i.e. 32 years ago. No other question has been put before the Greek people for ratification since that time. When have referendums been held in the remaining 24 EU countries in the past 25 years, and on what occasions?
