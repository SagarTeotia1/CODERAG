However, there are tax exceptions in place in many countries for other pay packages such as shares, stock options and severance packages for managers. 4. The issue of staff acquiring shares at preferential prices deserves special attention, as it raises possible conflict of interest issues. In particular, directors may be tempted to overstate performance of the company to obtain short terms profits. This is the reason why the Commission has recommended prior shareholder approval of share-based remuneration schemes. 5. The Commission will review further current practices of Member States and companies, seeking input, where appropriate, from the European Corporate Governance Forum. Many of the shortcomings raised by the Honourable Member could already be dealt with if Member States improved their corporate governance rules in line with the abovementioned Commission 2004 recommendation.
