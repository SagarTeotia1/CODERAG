Question for written answer E-4119/2010 to the Commission Rule 117 Kay Swinburne (ECR) (11 June 2010) Subject: Recognition of Mexican architects' qualifications in the EU EU citizens who have qualified as architects in Mexico are not able to have their qualification recognised in EU Member States. The Union, the Architects’ Council of Europe and the Mexican authorities have been negotiating the possibility of recognition but little progress has been made. Can the Commission indicate: — What is the status of the current negotiations with the Mexican authorities and the Architects’ Council of Europe? — Whether it anticipates a favourable outcome in the near future?
