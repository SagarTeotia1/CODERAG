ORAL QUESTION H-0174/07 for Question Time at the part-session in March 2007 pursuant to Rule 109 of the Rules of Procedure by Laima Liucija Andrikienė to the Council Subject: Further ratification of the EU Constitution One of German EU Presidency priorities is the continuation of the EU Constitution’s ratification process. Does the German Presidency already have a road map for the continuation of the constitutional process, in order to adopt a Constitution before the next European elections in 2009? What are the concrete steps which the German Presidency is going to make in order to achieve its goal concerning timely ratification of the Constitution?
