WRITTEN QUESTION E-3571/07 by Bill Newton Dunn (ALDE) to the Commission (12 July 2007) Subject: A new ‘Sangatte’ refugee camp outside Calais? What is the truth behind the report of the Daily Mail newspaper in the UK last week alleging that the Communist mayor of Calais is authorising a new refugee camp, to facilitate more illegal immigrants into the UK, in defiance of the new French President who had reportedly pledged ‘there will be no new Sangatte while I am President’.
