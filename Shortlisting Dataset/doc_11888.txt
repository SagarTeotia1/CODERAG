Question for written answer E-003761/2011 to the Commission Rule 117 Diane Dodds (NI) (15 April 2011) Subject: Nuclear incident in Japan Given the detection of radioactivity in the sea around Japan and the level of fish imports from that region into the EU, what precautions has the Commission taken to ensure that this food is safe for the consumer? Has the Commission undertaken any work to measure the increasing levels of radioactivity in the seas and the areas affected? What steps will the Commission take in order to ensure the safety of fish imports, should the problem escalate?
