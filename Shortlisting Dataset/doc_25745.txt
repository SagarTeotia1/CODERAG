Points out that national State aid is an important financial instrument for economic, social and territorial cohesion, and must therefore be incorporated into the national reference framework and be approved as part of the multiannual national programme; 52. Appreciates the Commission's intention to improve the transparency of regional State aid in an enlarged EU and supports easy access for all interested parties to the full text of all applicable regional aid schemes in the EU; 53. Calls for the fundamental rules on State aid associated with the Structural Funds to be incorporated into the Council regulation laying down general provisions on the European Regional Development Fund, the European Social Fund and the Cohesion Fund; 54. Thanks the Commission for having taken account of Parliament 's considerations during the consultation period leading to the drafting of the draft Regional Aid Guidelines; 55. Instructs its President to forward this resolution to the Council, the Commission, and the Committee of the Regions.
