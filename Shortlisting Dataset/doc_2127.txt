CONTENTS PROCEDURAL PAGE LEGISLATIVE PROPOSAL DRAFT LEGISLATIVE RESOLUTION EXPLANATORY STATEMENT PROCEDURAL PAGE By letter of 4 February 2002 the Council consulted Parliament, pursuant to Article 254(2) of the EC Treaty, on the draft Council decision amending Article 20 of the Protocol on the Statute of the Court of Justice of the European Communities (12991/2001 - 2001/0827 (CNS)). At the sitting of 7 February 2002 the President of Parliament announced that he had referred this draft to the Committee on Legal Affairs and the Internal Market as the committee responsible and the Committee on Citizens' Freedoms and Rights, Justice and Home Affairs and the Committee on Constitutional Affairs for their opinions ( C5-0050/2002 ). The Committee on Legal Affairs and the Internal Market appointed Giuseppe Gargani rapporteur at its meeting of 26 February 2002. It considered the Council draft and draft report at its meetings of 27 March and 23 April 2002. At the latter meeting it decided to apply the procedure without debate pursuant to Rule 114(1) of the Rules of Procedure.
