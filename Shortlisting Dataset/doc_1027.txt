However, a number of policyholders confirmed the statement made by Mr SCAWEN in (H3) that, " in many cases, the FOS ruling takes so much time that by the time a decision has been reached, the policyholder has become statute barred ". He suggests that " it is not a coincidence that the FOS took almost five years before attempting to resolve my claim " (Mr WEIR, H2).
