Answer given by High Representative/Vice-President Ashton on behalf of the Commission (19 October 2010) According to the annex of the Council Decision of 26 July 2010 establishing the European External Action Service (EEAS), the staff in the EU Delegations that will be transferred ‘en bloc’ to the EEAS is as follows: — all Heads of Delegations and Deputy Heads of Delegations and support staff directly attached to them; — all Political sections or cells and staff; — all information and public diplomacy sections and staff; — all Administration sections. All officials in EU delegations covered by the Staff regulations, including Commission staff will continue to be covered by Annex X, including Chapter 2, Article 5.
