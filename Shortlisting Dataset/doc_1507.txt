Rule 20 Duties of the Vice-Presidents 1. Should the President be absent or unable to discharge his duties, or should he wish to take part in a debate pursuant to Rule 19(3), he shall be replaced by one of the Vice-Presidents pursuant to Rule 15(2). 2. The Vice-Presidents shall also carry out the duties conferred upon them under Rules 22, 24 (3) and 82 (3). 3. The President may delegate to the Vice-Presidents any duties such as representing Parliament at specific ceremonies or acts. In particular, the President may designate a Vice-President to take charge of the responsibilities conferred on the President in Rules 43 (3) and 44 (2).
