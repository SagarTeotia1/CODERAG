ORAL QUESTION H-0053/10 for Question Time at the part-session in March 2010 pursuant to Rule 116 of the Rules of Procedure by Marian Harkin to the Council Subject: Domestic violence In the Spanish Presidency's statement at the plenary session in January 2010, the Presidency highlighted its firm intention to combat violence against women, to propose draft legislation to fight violence against women and to establish a European Observatory of Domestic Violence. Can the Presidency elaborate on what exactly its plans are in this area and when we can expect such initiatives to be put in place?
