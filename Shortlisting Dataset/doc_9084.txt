Reply (19 April 2010) The questions presented by the Honourable Member concerning retail prices in an individual Member State do not fall within the sphere of competence of the Council. More generally, however, food commodity prices are currently the subject of extensive political discussion at European level. On 28 October 2009, the Commission published a communication to the European Parliament, the Council, the European Economic and Social Committee and the Committee of the Regions entitled ‘A better functioning food supply chain in Europe’ COM(2009)591 final. . At the Spanish Presidency's initiative, the Council held an extensive exchange of views on this communication on 18 January 2010. On 29 March 2010, Presidency conclusions 8124/10. on this communication were adopted, supported by a vast majority of delegations.
