P5_TA(2000)0499 Protocol amending Europol Convention * A5-0312/2000 Initiative from the Portuguese Republic with a view to the adoption of a Council Act on the drawing up on the basis of Article 43(1) of the Convention on the establishment of a European Police Office (Europol Convention) of a Protocol amending Article 2 and the Annex to that Convention (9426/2000 - C5-0359/2000 - 2000/0809(CNS) ) The initiative was amended as follows: Text proposed by the Portuguese Republic OJ C 200, 13.7.2000, p. 1. Amendments by Parliament (Amendment 1) Initiative, Title Initiative from the Portuguese Republic with a view to the adoption of a Council Act on the drawing up on the basis of Article 43(1) of the Convention on the establishment of a European Police Office (Europol Convention) of a Protocol amending Article 2 and the Annex to that Convention Initiative from the Portuguese Republic with a view to the adoption of a Council Act on the drawing up on the basis of Article 43(1) of the Convention on the establishment of a European Police Office (Europol Convention) of a Protocol amending Articles 2, 28, 29, 34 and 40 and the Annex to that Convention (Amendment 9) PROTOCOL, RECITAL -1(new) (-1) The entry into force of the Europol Convention has provided the European Union with an important tool in the field of law enforcement and of organised crime by developing and strengthening the operative exchange of information and intelligence between law enforcement parties in Member States.
