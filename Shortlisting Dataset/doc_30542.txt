PECH PECH highlights role of women for the development of fisheries areas The afternoon of the PECH meeting of 1st December was devoted to a public hearing on "Women and the sustainable development of fisheries areas", with an introduction by Commissioner Maria Damanaki and a number of presentations by local, regional, national and transnational women networks and organisations active in the area of fisheries and aquaculture. The debate, conducted by the Chair Carmen Fraga Estévez (EPP, ES) and Josefa Andres Barea (S&D, ES), the Committee's standing rapporteur for women issues and gender mainstreaming, focused inter alia on the problem of the “invisible” work performed by women in fisheries, as well as on best practices for self-organisation and networking and on successful projects for the diversification of activities initiated by women. 20101202CAN05288
