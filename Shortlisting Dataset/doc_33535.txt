9999/C Q999 E/052 WRITTEN QUESTION E-0597/04 by Christopher Huhne (ELDR) to the Commission (18 February 2004) Subject: Freedom of movement 1. Will the Commission tabulate for each Member State the provisions that exist whereby nationals of other Member States may avail themselves of their rights to free movement of labour? For example, which Member States insist on registration with the police or on carrying a special identity card? 2. At what point is the national of another Member State entitled in each case to claim benefit, and what is the level of that benefit and what are the qualifications for it? 3. Will the Commission list in the case of each Member State any other provisions that might be regarded as treating nationals of another Member State differently to nationals of the home Member State?
