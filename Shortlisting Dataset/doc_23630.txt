DEVE Humanitarian Aid in the Hemicycle - and on the Web Around 150 external guests came to the Hearing on Humanitarian Aid, organised by the Development Committee on 29 January in the Hemicycle. The role of the military in humanitarian crises, the participation of NGOs and adequate financing for the upcoming humanitarian challenges were among the issues. MEPs discussed with the nine experts, including UN Under-Secretary-General John Holmes and ECHO Director-General Antonio Cavaco.
