ORAL QUESTION H-0634/06 for Question Time at the part-session in September I 2006 pursuant to Rule 109 of the Rules of Procedure by Liam Aylward to the Commission Subject: Brazilian beef imports into the European Union Can the European Commission make a comprehensive statement as to the state of play of Brazilian beef imports into the European Union in light of the fact that the EU has found that Brazilian beef is fundamentally unsafe, and could the latest figures accounting for the amount of Brazilian beef imports into the European Union be given?
