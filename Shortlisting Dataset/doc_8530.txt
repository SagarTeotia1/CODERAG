INTA The workshop on the Anti-Counterfeiting Trade Agreement The workshop will provide a public forum to discuss various aspects of the Anti-Counterfeiting Trade Agreement such as civil liberties, impact on access to medicines or on employment. A comprehensive assessment of the agreement and the EU intellectual property rights protection and enforcement trade policies will be given. The workshop will take place on 1 March 2012.
