WRITTEN QUESTION P-4832/09 by Georgios Stavrakakis (S-D) to the Commission (5 October 2009) Subject: Cohesion policy Can the Commission say: — What advance payments have been made to Greece every year since 2007 from the total allocation for the 2007‑13 programming period? — What interim payments the Commission has made to Greece, and under which operational programmes? — What is the outstanding balance of advances paid to Greece for which the Commission has not received an interim payment request? — Where advances to Member States have not been transferred in full to the relevant projects, how the relevant amounts are classified in the budgets of the individual Member States? — What is the level of cohesion policy funding available to Greece from the 2007‑13 programming period allocation in respect of the years 2007, 2008 and 2009? — Whether it has conducted any studies on the investments Greece is undertaking with cohesion policy funding and what the major shortcomings are with respect to achieving the goal of a knowledge-based economy?
