ORAL QUESTION H-0092/08 for Question Time at the part-session in March 2008 pursuant to Rule 109 of the Rules of Procedure by Colm Burke to the Commission Subject: Intercultural dialogue in EU In this Year of Intercultural Dialogue in the EU the Commission has planned many events. An emphasis should be placed on involving young people in the events of this year so that they may benefit from the rich cultural diversity in the EU. What specific measures does the Commission envisage in order to involve young people in the European Year of Intercultural Dialogue?
