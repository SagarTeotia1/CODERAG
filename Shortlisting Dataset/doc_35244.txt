Question for written answer E-010634/2010 to the Commission Rule 117 Ilda Figueiredo (GUE/NGL) (20 December 2010) Subject: Use of the Jaspers initiative (preparation of projects) In relation to the European Union's adoption of the Jaspers initiative (preparation of projects), can the Commission provide the following information: 1. What is the current situation regarding the use of this initiative? What use has been made of it by each individual Member State? 2. Are there any plans to speed up the process and publicise it more widely in order to encourage micro, small and medium-sized enterprises and other small-scale social organisations to participate?
