WRITTEN QUESTION P-4768/06 by Roger Helmer (NI) to the Commission (30 October 2006) Subject: Working Time Directive 2002/15/EC Does the Commission acknowledge that nine EU Members have failed to implement the Working Time Directive 2002/15/EC OJ L 80, 23.3.2002, p. 35. ? Will Commissioner Barrot make a public statement on the nine countries who have failed to implement the WTD which the UK made law on 4 April 2005? Can you confirm whether Holland has obtained a five-year dispensation on implementing the WTD?
