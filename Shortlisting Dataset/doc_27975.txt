WRITTEN QUESTION E-0693/06 by Silvana Koch-Mehrin (ALDE) to the Commission (22 February 2006) Subject: Financial Perspective: general, net contributors 1. In 2005, how high were the annual German gross payments to the EU budget, how high will they be in 2006 and how high will they be from 2007‑13, that is during the course of the coming financial perspective (in absolute figures and percentages based on the total EU budget)? 2. If the target were 1 % of the EU gross national income, how high would Germany's annual gross payments to the EU budget be? 3. What are the total amounts arising from the 17 December 2005 agreement on the financial planning period from 2007‑13 (in absolute amounts according to commitments and payments and/or in percentages of EU GNI)? 4. How may the total annual expenditure for the 2007‑13 be categorised into the following headings: sustainable growth; cohesion for growth and employment (until now: Structural and Cohesion Funds); sustainable management and protection of natural resources (including agriculture); EU citizenship, freedom, security and justice, as well as sub-categories such as competitiveness for growth and employment (in absolute amounts according to commitments and payments)? 5. How high are the annual returns to Germany in absolute figures and percentages of the total EU budget for the financial planning period 2007‑13? 6. How will the returns in the financial planning period 2007‑13 develop, by comparison with the current financial perspective and based on the total EU budget? Will there be any increases in the returns to Germany? 7. How will Germany's position as net contributor develop? 8. How will Germany's position as net contributor develop in relation to other net contributors?
