WRITTEN QUESTION E-4577/06 by Robert Kilroy-Silk (NI) to the Commission (26 October 2006) Subject: Denial of freedom of expression in Italy Following the Commission’s claim to support freedom of expression anywhere in the world, will it investigate the allegation that a television programme, Le Iene , (‘The Hyenas’), that claims that four Italian MPs had snorted cocaine and 12 had smoked cannabis, was prevented from being broadcast on Berlusconi’s Mediaset network by MPs pressurising the privacy authority?
