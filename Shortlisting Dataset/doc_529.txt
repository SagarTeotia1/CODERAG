P5_TA(2000)0397 Sustainable management of forests in developing countries ***III A5-0216/2000 European Parliament legislative resolution on the joint text approved by the Conciliation Committee for a European Parliament and Council regulation on measures to promote the conservation and sustainable management of tropical forests and other forests in developing countries ( C5-0284/2000 - 1999/0015(COD) ) (Codecision procedure: third reading) The European Parliament, - having regard to the joint text approved by the Conciliation Committee ( C5-0284/2000 ), - having regard to its position at first reading OJ C 279, 1.10.1999, p. 184. on the Commission proposal to Parliament and the Council ( COM(1999) 41 ) OJ C 87, 29.3.1999, p. 97. , - having regard to the amended Commission proposal ( COM(2000) 54 ) OJ C 248 E, 29.8.2000, p. 97. , - having regard to its position at second reading on the Council common position Texts Adopted of 14.3.2000, Item 1. , - having regard to the Commission's opinion on Parliament's amendments to the common position ( COM(2000) 308 - C5-0261/2000 ), - having regard to Article 251(5) of the EC Treaty, - having regard to Rule 83 of its Rules of Procedure, - having regard to the report of its delegation to the Conciliation Committee ( A5-0216/2000 ), 1. Approves the joint text; 2. Instructs its President to sign the act with the President of the Council pursuant to Article 254(1) of the EC Treaty; 3. Instructs its Secretary-General duly to sign the act and, in agreement with the Secretary-General of the Council, to have it published in the Official Journal of the European Communities; 4. Instructs its President to forward this legislative resolution to the Council and Commission.
