15.12.2010 B7-0734/2010 MOTION FOR A RESOLUTION pursuant to Rule 120 of the Rules of Procedure on nutraceuticals as food supplements Cristiana Muscardini PE455.840v01-00 B7‑0734/2010 Motion for a European Parliament resolution on nutraceuticals as food supplements The European Parliament , – having regard to Rule 120 of its Rules of Procedure, A. whereas there is nowadays a certain degree of confusion and laxity concerning food supplements, B. whereas the ranks of food supplements also include nutraceutricals used to supplement the diet, C. whereas standard food supplements are not normally intended to prevent, treat or cure any specific disease, while nutraceuticals on the other hand are also used to prevent or stabilise degenerative illnesses affecting the body’s structure or functioning, D. whereas consumers are not given appropriate information about the use of various food supplements, 1. Requests that the Commission launch studies into those food supplements currently on the market that also have healing or preventive functions; 2. Calls on the Commission to furnish consumers with appropriate information on the use of different food supplements, including nutraceuticals; 3. Asks the Commission to launch monitoring in accordance with standards of good manufacturing practice and compliance with ISO certification for nutraceutical producers in Europe.
