Joint answer given by Mr Potočnik on behalf of the Commission Written questions : E-003013/11 , E-003014/11 (11 May 2011) The Commission would point out that issues raised in Question E‑003013/2011 are the same as the issues raised in previous Written Question E‑002326/2011 by Mr Meyer http://www.europarl.europa.eu/QP-WEB/application/home.do?language=EN . Therefore the Commission would refer the Honourable Member to its answer to previous Written Question E‑002326/2011 http://www.europarl.europa.eu/sidesSearch/sipadeMapUrl.do?L=ES&PROG=QP&SORT_ORDER=DAA&REF_QP=2011-2326&F_REF_QP=2326/2011&S_RANK=%&LEG_ID=7 . With regard to the issues raised in Question E‑3014/2011 related to the compensatory measures concerning the extension of the airport of Barajas, the Commission would like to point out that the information made available by the Spanish authorities does not allow to provide an answer to the specific questions raised by the Honourable Member in the framework of this written question. The Commission has asked Spain for detailed information regarding the points raised and will inform the Honourable Member of its findings.
