ECON Exchange of views with Thomas Steffen, Chairman of CEIOPS, on 22 September Chairman of the Committee of European Insurance and Occupational Pensions Supervisors Thomas Steffen will present the outcome of the CEIOPS fourth quantitative impact study (QIS 4) for Solvency II, proposal for a directive for solvency requirements of insurance and reinsurance undertakings. QIS 4 has among others examined the method of calculation of Minimum Capital Requirements for insurance and reinsurance undertakings. CEIOPS Chair's presentation will be followed by the exchange of views on QIS 4 and on more than 800 ECON amendments to the Solvency II proposal. 20080918CAN37569
