P6_TA(2007)0046 EC-Russia Short-stay Visa Agreement * A6-0029/2007 European Parliament legislative resolution on the proposal for a Council decision on the conclusion of the Agreement between the European Community and the Russian Federation on the facilitation of issuance of short-stay visas (8780/2006 - COM(2006)0188 – C6-0169/2006 – 2006/0062(CNS) ) (Consultation procedure) The European Parliament , – having regard to the proposal for a Council decision ( COM(2006)0188 ) Not yet published in OJ. , – having regard to Article 62(2)(b)(i) and (ii) and Article 300(2), first subparagraph, first sentence, of the EC Treaty, – having regard to Article 300(3), first subparagraph, of the EC Treaty, pursuant to which the Council consulted Parliament ( C6-0169/2006 ), – having regard to Rules 51 and 83(7) of its Rules of Procedure, – having regard to the report of the Committee on Civil Liberties, Justice and Home Affairs and the opinion of the Committee on Foreign Affairs ( A6-0029/2007 ), 1. Approves the conclusion of the Agreement; 2. Instructs its President to forward its position to the Council and the Commission, and the governments and parliaments of the Member States and of the Russian Federation.
