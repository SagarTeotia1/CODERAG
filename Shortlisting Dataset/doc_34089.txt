WRITTEN QUESTION P-4666/08 by Marie Panayotopoulos-Cassiotou (PPE‑DE) to the Commission (28 August 2008) Subject: Participation by young people in European programmes What is the Commission’s assessment of participation by young Europeans in European youth programmes? How are the organisations responsible for implementing the programmes evaluated and how is the experience obtained by participants of value in terms of their future lives and careers? What is the level of participation in these programmes in each Member State?
