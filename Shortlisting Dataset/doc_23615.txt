Answer given by Mr Barnier on behalf of the Commission (25 January 2011) EC law, notably the Professional Qualifications Directive Directive 2005/36/EC of the European Parliament and of the Council of 7 September 2005 on the recognition of professional qualifications, OJ L 225, 30.9.2005. , seeks to facilitate the mobility of professionals in the single market, including by laying down a framework for comparing qualifications acquired in different Member States. However, it is up to Member States to decide if (and how) they wish to regulate a particular profession, such as ‘interior designer’.
