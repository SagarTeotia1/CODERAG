at 16.10.2003 Giorgio CALÒ (ELDR, I) as at 03.09.2003 Enlargement President of Lithuania's address to the House - East and West coming closer together Formal sitting - Address by Mr Rolandas Paksas, President of the Republic of Lithuania 05 .11.2003 President Pat Cox welcomed President Rolandas Paksas of Lithuania to the European Parliament. President Cox stated that it was " a happy coincidence " that President Paksas was addressing the House on the day when the Parliament would be debating the progress made by the candidate countries towards accession. He welcomed the remarkable progress that Lithuania had made towards accession and recalled that at one point Lithuania was not part of the " first-wave " of candidate countries for enlargement.
