At the sitting of 2 September 2002 the President of Parliament announced that he had referred this proposal to the Committee on Industry, External Trade, Research and Energy as the committee responsible and the Committee on Budgets, the Committee on the Environment, Public Health and Consumer Policy, the Committee on Development and Cooperation and the Committee on Women’s Rights and Equal Opportunities for their opinions ( C5-0392/2002 ).
