ORAL QUESTION H-0368/06 for Question Time at the part-session in May 2006 pursuant to Rule 109 of the Rules of Procedure by Leopold Rutowicz to the Council Subject: Military operations in the Pakistani province of Baluchistan In November 2005 there was a spate of reports of cases involving killings or violence against women and children as a result of operations by the Pakistani army in the province of Baluchistan. According to reports, more than 200 people have died. The highly-respected Human Rights Commission of Pakistan has confirmed and documented countless cases of violence against the civilian population by the Pakistani armed forces. Will the Council examine the situation as regards human rights violations in the province of Baluchistan and explain its position before Parliament?
