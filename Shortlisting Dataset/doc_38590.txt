WRITTEN QUESTION E-2584/05 by Proinsias De Rossa (PSE) to the Commission (14 July 2005) Subject: Irish reports on the use of ozone-depleting substances As a follow-up to its answer of 4 February 2005 to my written question E‑3495/04, what response, if any, has the Commission received from the Irish authorities with regard to the final warning (‘reasoned opinion’) it announced it was sending to Ireland on 13 January 2005 in IP/05/37 with regard to the failure to submit reports on the use of certain ozone-depleting substances under the Ozone Regulation (2037/2000 OJ L 244, 29.9.2000, p. 1 . )? What action has the Commission taken or is it proposing to take on this matter?
