WRITTEN QUESTION E-6969/08 by Jim Allister (NI) to the Council (6 January 2009) Subject: Christians in the Gaza Strip What is the Council doing to ensure the safety of Palestinian Christians in the Gaza Strip, given such proclamations as that by the Popular Resistance Committees terror group on eradicating the Christian presence in Gaza and in view of the lack of action by the Hamas authorities to protect this group against daily threats of kidnapping and assault?
