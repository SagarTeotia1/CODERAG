ORAL QUESTION H-0085/03 for Question Time at the part-session in March 2003 pursuant to Rule 43 of the Rules of Procedure by Sylviane Ainardi to the Commission Subject: Flags of convenience In its resolution of 19 December 2002 on the sinking of the Prestige and its consequences, the European Parliament called in particular for ‘flags of convenience to be prohibited in EU territorial waters’. What measures will the Commission propose for this EP recommendation to be implemented as quickly as possible?
