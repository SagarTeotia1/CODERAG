Answer given by Mr Borg on behalf of the Commission (17 March 2009) According to information obtained from the Portuguese authorities, the project in question is currently being evaluated for possible financing under the Portuguese Operational Programme in the framework of the European Fisheries Fund (EFF). The Commission would like to take this occasion to recall that, according to the regulations in force, it does not have systematic access to information concerning individual applications or projects which Member States are processing under their respective operational programmes.
