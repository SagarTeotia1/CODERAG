Amendment 19 Annex, article 11, paragraph 6 6. The Administrative Board shall propose to the Commission the level and the cost-base of the levy on the air navigation charges referred to in paragraph 1. deleted Justification An additional levy on air navigation charges must be avoided. User charges will already contribute to the programme via Eurocontrol. A successful definition phase should deliver a clear business case triggering the necessary private investment (and public funding).
