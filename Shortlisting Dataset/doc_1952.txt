Reply (16 March 2009) As it provides the secretariat for the Interinstitutional Data-processing Committee, it is the Commission's responsibility in the first instance to implement the procedures for forwarding the study requested by the Honourable Member. In its reply to Question E‑6416/08 , the Commission did in fact undertake to ask the Chairman of the Interinstitutional Data‑processing Committee to forward that study. The Council gave its agreement. According to the information available to the Council, the secretariat of the Interinstitutionnal Data‑processing Committee has just forwarded the study to the Honourable Member.
