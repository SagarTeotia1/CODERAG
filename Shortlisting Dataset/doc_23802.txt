Calls on Mr Lukashenko to immediately stop persecution of peaceful protesters in Belarus; 4. Calls on the authorities of Belarus to immediately release the demonstrators and opposition leaders; 5. Strongly condemns the Belarussian regime's indiscriminate attacks on the media, journalists, members of the opposition, human rights activists and any person who attempts freely to voice criticism of the President and the regime, as manifested through arbitrary arrests, ill-treatment of detainees, disappearances, politically motivated persecution and other acts of repression that flout the basic principles of democracy and the rule of law; 6. Calls on the Council and the Commission to prepare a strategy to support democratic forces and independent media in Belarus and to consider founding an independent radio station with the aim of providing the Belarussian people with independent information; 7. Instructs its President to forward this resolution to the Council, the Commission, the Parliaments and Governments of the Member States and the President, Government and Parliament of Belarus.
