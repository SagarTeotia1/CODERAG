WRITTEN QUESTION E-3449/09 by Robert Kilroy-Silk (NI) to the Commission (12 May 2009) Subject: Demolition of Palestinian homes in Jerusalem Further to the Commission’s answer to my Parliamentary Question E‑1742/09, will it say whether the Government of Israel has taken any notice of the Commission’s representations and the Council’s ‘deep concern’ about its intention of demolishing approximately 90 houses in the Al‑Bustan/Silwan area?
