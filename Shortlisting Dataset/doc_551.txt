Mr Gray agreed that the Commission's role on the CEPOL board is limited, but disagreed with the criticism that the Commission not being effective. "We decided to only transfer the necessary financial instalments, based on forecasts and obligations", he explained. He also acknowledged the difficulties generally encountered by the growing number of small agencies. "We have set-up a Working Group to help these agencies reach their objectives", he said.
