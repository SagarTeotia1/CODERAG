The political group leaders and President Pöttering will have meetings with the Slovenian Prime Minister, Janez Jansa, and his government. President Pöttering will also meet the Speaker of the Slovenian National Assembly, France Cukjati, and the group leaders will take part in a Youth Forum at the National Assembly. There will be a press conference with President Pöttering and Prime Minister Jansa at 17.40 on Wednesday. At this first plenary session of the assembly, which was set up in November 2006, parliamentarians will discuss and vote on three reports on the following subjects: EU-Latin America relations with a view to the fifth Lima summit in 2008 (summit of presidents and heads of government of the countries of the EU, Latin America and the Caribbean); economic and trade relations; sustainable development and global warming. The votes on these reports will take place on Thursday 20 December (morning).
