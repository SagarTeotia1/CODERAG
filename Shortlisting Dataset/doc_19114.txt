Answer given by Mr McCreevy on behalf of the Commission (25 February 2005) The Honourable Member will find herewith a list of the 126 Internal Market Directives having to be transposed in 2004, sorted by transposition date. For each Directive the Member States which did not carry out any complete transposition as at 6 February 2005 are indicated. At the end of the document, a summary table shows the number of Directives not transposed by Member State, and the national transposition deficit. The requested information will be sent to the Honourable Member directly. It contains a list of 126 Internal Market Directives that had to be transposed in 2004, giving details as to whether Member States have or have not fully transposed the Directives.
