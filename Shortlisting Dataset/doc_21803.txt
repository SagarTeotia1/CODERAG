WRITTEN QUESTION E-6160/08 by Pedro Guerreiro (GUE/NGL) to the Commission (14 November 2008) Subject: Improving the fishing port and working conditions for fishermen in Gâmbia, Setubal At a meeting with artisanal fishermen in Gâmbia, Setubal, attention was drawn to the inadequate conditions in the fishing port in terms of the safety of vessels and working conditions for fishermen, including the need to improve the services provided in the market, specifically with regard to refrigeration. Can the Commission answer the following questions: 1. What possibilities currently exist for Community funding to contribute to improvements in the fishing port and working conditions for fishermen in Gâmbia, Setubal? 2. Is the Commission aware of any project financed at Community level for this purpose?
