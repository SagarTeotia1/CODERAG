ORAL QUESTION H-0308/02 for Question Time at the part-session in May 2002 pursuant to Rule 43 of the Rules of Procedure by Astrid Thors to the Commission Subject: Children's rights in the applicant countries To what extent will the Commission's progress report on enlargement take account of how the applicant countries have honoured their commitments under the UN Convention on the Rights of the Child and of the reports received by the Committee on the Rights of the Child pursuant to that Convention? In reply to question H-0915/97 tabled in 1997, the Commission pledged that the annual reports on the Phare programme would show the amounts planned for national programmes to improve the situation of children in the applicant countries. To what extent is that the case? Written answer of 16.12.1997
