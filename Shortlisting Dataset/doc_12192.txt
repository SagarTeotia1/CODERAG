On 10 January this year, the Commission allocated €1.1 million to support a United Nations High Commission for Refugees (UNHCR) protection, care and maintenance programme for around 60,000 Angolan refugees living in the DRC During Angola's 27-year long civil war, there was a regular flow of refugees across the border, particularly to the provinces of Bas Congo and Bandundu in southern DRC. Following the Angolan peace agreement that was signed in April 2002, most of the refugees have indicated their desire to return home.
