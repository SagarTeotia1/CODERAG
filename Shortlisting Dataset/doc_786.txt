WRITTEN QUESTION E-1434/10 by Zbigniew Ziobro (ECR) to the Commission (11 March 2010) Subject: Execution of European arrest warrants 1. How many requests to execute European arrest warrants (EAW) were made to the individual Member States each year between 2005 and 2009 (please provide a breakdown of the number of EAWs by requesting Member State), and in how many cases were the warrants actually executed? 2. During that period, what was the average time taken to execute the EAWs from the time of issue until the actual handover of the persons being sought? 3. During the same period, what were the reasons given for refusing to execute EAWs?
