Question for Question Time H-0504/2010 to the Commission Part-session: October 2010 Rule 116 Richard Howitt (S&D) Subject: Possible relocation of Heathrow Airport to the Thames Estuary What initial considerations does the Commission have in relation to reports that renewed feasibility studies are taking place on relocating Heathrow Airport to an artificial island to be sited in the Thames Estuary, with the consequent environmental, congestion and dislocation impact this will have on my constituents in South Essex? Which further studies and consultations will the Commission undertake in regard to this question?
