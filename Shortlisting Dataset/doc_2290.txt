European Sustainable Nuclear Energy Technology Platform www.snetp.eu groups the main stakeholders from the technical nuclear community with the aim of defining and implementing a Strategic Research Agenda in the field of nuclear systems and safety. Since 1976 Cooperation Agreement between the European Atomic Energy Community and the International Atomic Energy Agency, OJ L 329, 23.12.1975. , the Euratom Community has cooperated with the International Atomic Energy Agency in Vienna on the establishment of high level safety standards to protect people and the environment, as well as in the effective and efficient implementation of safeguards. In May 2008, a Joint Statement http://www.iaea.org/NewsCenter/News/PDF/iaea_euratom070508.pdf was signed between the Commission and the IAEA The International Atomic Energy Agency. services in order to reinforce mutual cooperation on nuclear energy. The Commission also pursues an active policy at international level with regard to nuclear safety and security COM(2008)312. .
