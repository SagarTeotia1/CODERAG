ECON "European Social Entrepreneurship Funds and European Venture Capital Funds" On 20 March 2012, ECON is holding a public hearing on the European Social Entrepreneurship Funds and European Venture Capital Funds, in the context of the Commission proposals on both subjects of 7 December 2011. The hearing will have two sessions: the first one, on European Social Entrepreneurship Funds, will examine whether the proposed regime establishes an appropriate framework to favour the market for social enterprises by improving the effectiveness of fundraising . The second session, on Venture Capital Funds, will examine whether the Commission's proposal is appropriate and sufficient to promote the European Venture Capital industry and thus to enhance SMEs' access to equity finance.
