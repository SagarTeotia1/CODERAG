Reply (17 December 2010) The Council has prioritised negotiations on the proposal amending the regulation of the European Parliament and of the Council establishing the criteria and mechanisms for determining the Member State responsible for examining an application for international protection lodged in one of the Member States by a third-country national or a stateless person (the Dublin II Regulation) 16934/08. . Intensive discussions on the proposal are ongoing in the Council. The Presidency reported on the progress being made towards establishment of the Common European Asylum System, including the Dublin II Regulation, to the Council meeting held on 8 and 9 November 2010 15561/10. , and members of the Council held an exchange of views on issues arising, including the issue of solidarity.
