All information pertaining to current, recent or cancelled tender opportunities as well as a list of entities selected to carry out these tenders can be found on the following website: http://europa.eu.int/comm/europeaid/tender/index_en.htm For general information about grants and loans awarded by the different directorates-general (DGs) of the Commission, please consult the following website: http://europa.eu.int/comm/secretariat_general/sgc/aides/index_en.htm EuropeAid grants are awarded for projects proposed to the EU by non-state actors. The intention is that projects are selected for grants on the basis of calls for proposals. The publication of a call for proposal indicates the relevant funding priorities of the EU, eligibility criteria and any other conditions for the award of a grant. The search facility on the website allows to see current and expected calls for proposals (as well as those deadlines which have expired and information about grants which have been awarded as a result): http://europa.eu.int/comm/europeaid/cgi/frame12.pl EuropeAid has also grants available in the context of NGO co-financing and decentralised cooperation. To find out more about these grants, go to: http://europa.eu.int/comm/europeaid/projects/ong_cd/index_en.htm 3. General situation of the EC financing. EuropeAid manages funds which form part of the Commission’s external assistance. External assistance is central to the EU's role. The Commission strives to reinforce the impact of such assistance, by increasing the coherence between the EU’s external assistance and its other policies. To find out more, please refer to: http://europa.eu.int/comm/europeaid/general/mission_en.htm
