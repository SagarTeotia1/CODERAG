CULT Media Mundus: adoption of draft report The new programme, Media Mundus, aims to increase the competitiveness of the EU audiovisual industry in international markets, consumer choice and cultural diversity, as well as to reinforce the cultural and political role of the EU in the world. The Committee will vote on amendments which have been tabled to the draft report .
