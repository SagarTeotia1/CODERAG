To the extent that a state aid measures meets the conditions specified in this decision, Member States are allowed to carry out the measure and enjoy a block exemption from the notification requirement. Moreover, the Commission may declare compatible with the common market other types of state aid measures in favour of housing under Article 87(3) of the EC Treaty, based on a case-by-case assessment. In its assessment the Commission takes into account, among others, whether the aid would adversely affect trading conditions to an extent contrary to the common interest. In its communication issued last year on social services of general interest COM(2006)177 final. , the Commission provided for an extensive overview inter alia on the conditions under which EU competition law applies to these services, including social housing. As a follow-up to that communication, the Commission will issue next autumn a first bi-annual report on the situation of various social services of general interest across the EU, including social housing.
