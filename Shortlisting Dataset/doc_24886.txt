Question for written answer E-003765/2011 to the Commission Rule 117 William (The Earl of) Dartmouth (EFD) (15 April 2011) Subject: ESFS II Does the Commission consider that the absence of any real limits to the power of the ESFS (European System of Financial Supervisors) and its component institutions to demand data from any organisation, whether private, commercial or governmental, operating in the European Union is contrary to the right to personal privacy, which is often characterised as a fundamental human right?
