European Parliament legislative resolution on the proposal for a Council Decision providing further macro-financial assistance to Romania ( COM(1999) 405 - C5-0097/1999 - 1999/0167(CNS) ) (Consultation procedure) The European Parliament, - having regard to the Commission proposal to the Council, COM(1999) 405 - 1999/0167(CNS) , - having been consulted by the Council pursuant to Article 308 of the EC Treaty ( C5-0098/1999 ), - having regard to Rule 67 of its Rules of Procedure, - having regard to the report of the Committee on Industry, External Trade, Research and Energy and the opinion of the Committee on Budgets ( A5-0017/1999 ), 1. Approves the Commission proposal as amended; 2. Calls on the Commission to alter its proposal accordingly, pursuant to Article 250(2) of the EC Treaty; 3. Calls on the Council to notify Parliament should it intend to depart from the text approved by Parliament; 4. Asks to be consulted again if the Council intends to amend the Commission proposal substantially; 5. Instructs its President to forward its position to the Council and Commission.
