Answer given by Mrs Ferrero-Waldner on behalf of the Commission (27 February 2008) Following the organisation of the first congress of the ‘Declaration of Damascus’ on 1 December 2007, arrests of a considerable group of human rights activists was reported. Of this group nine persons remained in custody. Some more arrests were reported since. Together with Member States’ embassies, the Delegation of the Commission in Damascus monitors closely the government’s actions against civil society activists, maintains regular contacts with human rights organisations and the lawyers, and observes and reports about court cases on a systematic basis.
