IMCO Consumer Rights Directive: Hearing with National Parliaments 23.02.2010 - 10.00 to 12.30 - ASP 3G2 The Internal Market and Consumer Affairs Committee will hold a hearing with National Parliaments on the Consumer Rights Directive. Key questions in the debate are the appropriate level of consumer protection and the Commission's full harmonisation approach.
