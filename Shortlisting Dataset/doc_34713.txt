WRITTEN QUESTION E-3025/07 by Kyriacos Triantaphyllides (GUE/NGL) to the Commission (19 June 2007) Subject: Imports of dangerous products into the EU There is a whole range of products in circulation in the European Union, such as toys which can cause injury, exploding hair-dryers and others, which originate in the People's Republic of China, the country which is the main source of problematic products. In addition, in 2006, 48 % of the products classified as ‘dangerous’ by the European Union were of Chinese origin. What measures will the Commission take to combat the unrestrained import of dangerous products into the European Union?
