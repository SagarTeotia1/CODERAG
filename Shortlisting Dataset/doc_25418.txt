CULT European capitals of culture The European City of Culture was first launched by the Council of Ministers in 1985. In 2006, with the arrival of 12 new Member States, a new method for designating the "European Capitals of Culture" was needed. This report set out the Parliament's position on the Commission's plans for updating the selection procedure.
