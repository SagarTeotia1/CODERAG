Article 2, 3rd indent - evaluate the need to amend the scope of the Directive according to the last developments in the railway transport and interoperability legislative framework in order to cover, in the most effective way, all railway applications. - evaluate the need to amend the scope of the Directive according to the last developments in the railway transport and interoperability legislative framework in order to cover, in the most effective way, all railway applications; the Commission shall scrutinise these possibilities as soon as possible and shall also consider the possibility of finally covering all railway applications in one single Directive . Justification It is desirable to address the emissions of engines for railway applications that are more powerful than 560 kW without unnecessary delay. As soon as that is done, a splitting of provisions for railway applications into two Directives should be avoided.
