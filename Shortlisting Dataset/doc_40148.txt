P5_TA(2002)0488 Amendments to the constituent acts of the European Centre for the Development of Vocational Training following the adoption of the new Financial Regulation * A5-0336/2002 Proposal for a Council regulation amending Regulation (EEC) No 337/75 as regards the budgetary and financial rules applicable to the European Centre for the Development of Vocational Training and access to the Centre's documents and repealing Regulation (EEC) No 1416/76 ( COM(2002) 406 - C5-0439/2002 - 2002/0180(CNS)) The proposal was amended as follows The vote on the draft legislative resolution was postponed pursuant to Rule 69. ( A5-0336/2002 ) : Text proposed by the Commission Amendments by Parliament Amendment 102 RECITAL 5 a (new) (5a) The constituent act relating to the Centre mentioned in this Regulation should provide scope for the Member State in which the Centre has its seat to make a direct or indirect financial contribution. Amendment 103 ARTICLE 1, POINT 2 Article 10, paragraph 1, subparagraph 1 a (new) (Regulation (EEC) No 337/75 The revenue shall include any financial contribution from the Member State in which the Centre has its seat. Amendment 104 ARTICLE 1, POINT 2 Article 10, paragraph 1, subparagraph 1 b (new) (Regulation (EEC) No 337/75) The statement of expenditure may be presented using a nomenclature based on the nature and/or objective of the expenditure, provided that a distinction is made between administrative appropriations and operating appropriations. This nomenclature shall be defined by the Centre. Amendment 105 ARTICLE 1, POINT 3 Article 11, paragraph 2 a (new) (Regulation (EEC) No 337/75) 2a. Before taking any decision which has financial implications and which may have an impact on the amount of the Community subsidy for the current year or for subsequent years, the management board shall notify the Commission and the budgetary authority. If neither arm of the budgetary authority has raised any objection within six weeks, the management board shall adopt the final decision. Amendment 106 ARTICLE 1, POINT 3 Article 11, paragraph 3 a (new) (Regulation (EEC) No 337/75) 3a. The final budget shall be adopted by the management board of the Centre following the final adoption of the EU general budget establishing the amount of the subsidy and the establishment plan. Amendment 107 ARTICLE 1, POINT 4 Article 12, paragraph 1 a (new) (Regulation (EEC) No 337/75) 1a. In the event of revision of the framework Financial Regulation for the bodies referred to in Article 185 of the Financial Regulation, the Commission shall consult the European Parliament, the Council and the Court of Auditors. Amendment 108 ARTICLE 1, POINT 4 Article 12, paragraph 2 a (new) (Regulation (EEC) No 337/75) 2a. The Executive Director may delegate his powers of budget implementation to staff of the Centre covered by the Staff Regulations, in accordance with the conditions laid down by the financial regulation of the Centre or by the financial rules, adopted by the management board, within the meaning of Article 185 of the Financial Regulation. Those so empowered may act only within the limits of the powers expressly conferred upon them. Amendment 109 ARTICLE 1, POINT 4 Article
