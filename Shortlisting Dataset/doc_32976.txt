FEMM Exchange of views with the Director of the European Institute for Gender Equality The European Institute for Gender Equality (EIGE) opened its doors in Vilnius, on 16 December 2009. The agency's main activity will be the collection, analysis and dissemination of information on gender equality on a European level.
