WRITTEN QUESTION P-3100/09 by Jeanine Hennis-Plasschaert (ALDE) to the Commission (27 April 2009) Subject: Cross-border crime against shops — study into attacks on shops In its answer to my Written Question E‑1237/08 the Commission wrote that, within the framework of its action plan COM(2006)437. for developing a comprehensive and coherent EU strategy to measure crime and criminal justice, the Commission intended to conduct a study into attacks on shops in order to supplement statistical data available at European level. The answer was given on 21 April 2008. We are now a year further on. Could the Commission indicate whether the study into attacks on shops in the EU has in the meantime being conducted? If so, could it inform me of the results of the study and could it indicate how it intends to follow up these results? If not, could it indicate when we can expect the study and the results?
