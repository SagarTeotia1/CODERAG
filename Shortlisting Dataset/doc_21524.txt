23.2.2011 B7-0153/2011 MOTION FOR A RESOLUTION pursuant to Rule 120 of the Rules of Procedure on the European Youth Employment Fund Salvador Garriga Polledo PE459.685v01-00 B7‑0153/2011 Motion for a European Parliament resolution on the European Youth Employment Fund The European Parliament , – having regard to Rule 120 of its Rules of Procedure, A. whereas employment prospects for a large number of young people in the European Union are dire, B. whereas it is totally unacceptable that an entire generation of young people may see their opportunity to contribute to progress in the European Union, of which they are a part, through their employment, pass them by, C. whereas the European Union has been very active in regard to vocational training for these unemployed young people, D. whereas an effort commensurate with the need must be made at all costs to integrate unemployed young people in the EU into the labour market, in a way that gives them self-respect, 1. Calls for the establishment of a European Youth Employment Fund; 2. Calls for the size of the fund to be commensurate with its task of bringing young people in the European Union who are currently unemployed onto the labour market; 3. Calls for the Union’s institutions to be as generous as possible so that this fund becomes the cornerstone underpinning a change in the negative trend experienced today by a whole generation of young people in the European Union.
