WRITTEN QUESTION E-3019/07 by Gian Paolo Gobbo (UEN) to the Commission (19 June 2007) Subject: Liberalisation of railway services and safeguarding rail transport In the context of the projects for the liberalisation of rail services proposed by the Commission has account been taken of the need to ensure the efficiency of rail transport throughout the Member States? Does not the Commission consider that a wide and dense network of rail services is the best way of ensuring a reduction in the air pollution caused by the exponential increase in road traffic in the European Union?
