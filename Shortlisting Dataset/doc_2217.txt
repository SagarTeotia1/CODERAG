Question for written answer E-8027/2010 to the Commission Rule 117 Georgios Papanikolaou (PPE) (6 October 2010) Subject: Patient mobility in the EU Many patients from Member States whose national health systems are not well organised prefer to travel abroad in the hope of obtaining a higher standard of care in more efficiently run hospitals at the hands of more highly trained specialists. In view of this: 1. Does the Commission have information regarding patient mobility within the EU? 2. Have studies been made regarding which countries appear to offer better guarantees regarding healthcare and which countries seem less promising?
