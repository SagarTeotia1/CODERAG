Parliamentary Session 14-17 September Parliamentary Session 14-17 September This dossier will allow you to follow the main subjects of the September Parliamentary session (14-17) in Strasbourg, eastern France, which are the inaugural speech of President Buzek, the decision on whether Jose Manuel Barroso will be reconfirmed as Commission President, milk subsidies and preparations for the G20 summit. It will be updated daily. Members gather in Strasbourg from 14-17 September for their monthly session of debates and votes on proposed European legislation. This focus will be updated daily with all the latest news from Strasbourg. To guide you round, here is where you will find the information you need - it will be put there and updated following debates and votes from the Chamber.
