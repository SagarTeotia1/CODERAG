Notes that the budgetary procedure 2007 is the first seven-year Financial Framework following the adoption of the new Interinstitutional Agreement on budgetary discipline and sound financial management (Interinstitutional Agreement) OJ C 139, 14.6.2006, p. 1. ; 2. Is, however, alarmed at the fact that, despite the increase in the number of Member States of the European Union, the rapid restructuring of the world economy, the alarming scale of unemployment and the challenges faced by the international community owing to the technological revolution, the Community budget continues to be limited; 3. Believes that it is important to allow the Commission the possibility to prepare properly for the lifecycle of new and renewed programmes and activities; notes, however, that, extra-preparatory actions or pilot projects, if launched in 2007, should be carefully evaluated as to their necessity in 2007 and that priority should be given to employment , social and technological sectors; 4. Notes that the vast majority of our budget lines is fixed by a binding multi-annual financial envelope, adopted by codecision, and that only shifts within specific programmes, or from year to year, are possible; takes note that the "free-to-decide" budget lines are those covering social dialogue (040302; 04030301; 04030302; and 04030303), mobility (040305 and 040304), the agencies (Bilbao; Dublin; Cedefop; and Turin), and studies on the social situation and demographic change (040307); 5.
