FEMM Report delegation to the European Institute For Gender Equality, Vilnius - 15 June A delegation of committee Members visited the European Institute for Gender Equality in Vilnius, Lithuania on 19-20 May. During the visit they discussed the cooperation between the Institute and the committee, as well as issues such as the reconciliation of professional and private life, violence against women and the gender aspects of the Europe 2020 strategy.
