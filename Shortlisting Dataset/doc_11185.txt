WRITTEN QUESTION E-2513/04 by Chris Davies (ALDE/ADLE) to the Commission (18 October 2004) Subject: Nuclear power and fair competition In order to ensure fair and transparent competition between electricity producers, what requirements are placed upon Member State governments to ensure that civil nuclear power generators take full financial responsibility: — for all insurance requirements; — for the costs of decommissioning civil nuclear facilities; — for the storage and disposal of nuclear waste produced in these facilities? What requirements are made to ensure that civil nuclear power generators do not receive subsidies for the production of military material that may enable them to compete unfairly with other electricity producers?
