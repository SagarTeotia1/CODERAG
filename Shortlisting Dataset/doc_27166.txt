However, if six countries outside the EU still ratify it then the agreement will enter into force there. How will I be able to find out the latest developments on this in the European Parliament? On Friday 24 February a feature page dedicated to ACTA will be created, which will then be regularly updated. Live broadcast of meetings will be announced and there will be links to documents that have been released.
