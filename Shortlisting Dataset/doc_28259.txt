P5_TA(1999)0128 Legislative procedures in the field of development and cooperation ***I (procedure without debate) A5-0088/1999 European Parliament legislative resolution on current legislative procedures in the field of development and cooperation (Commission communication to the European Parliament and the Council - Effects of the entry into force of the Amsterdam Treaty) ( SEC(1999) 581 - C4-0219/1999 ) (Codecision procedure: confirmation of first reading) The European Parliament, - having regard to the list of Commission proposals pending as at 1 May 1999 for which the entry into force of the Treaty of Amsterdam requires a change of legal basis and/or procedure ( SEC(1999) 581 - C4-0219/1999 ), - having regard to its resolution of 4 May 1999 OJ C 279, 1.10.1999, p. 51. on the implications of the entry into force of the Treaty of Amsterdam (list of legislative proposals pending as at 1 May 1999, indicating new legal bases and any changes in legislative procedure following the entry into force of the Treaty of Amsterdam) ( C4-0134/1999 - SEC(1999) 581 - C4-0219/1999 ), - having regard to Article 251(2) of the EC Treaty, - having regard to the report of the Committee on Development and Cooperation ( A5-0088/1999 ), 1. Confirms as first reading under the codecision procedure its text adopted on 28 October 1994 OJ C 323, 21.11.1994, p. 492. on the proposal for a Council regulation in the field of employment creation and support to small firms and micro-enterprises in the Maghreb countries ( COM(1994) 289 - C4-0090/1994 - 1994/0167(COD) - former 1994/0167(SYN) ); 2. Instructs its President to forward this legislative resolution to the Council and Commission.
