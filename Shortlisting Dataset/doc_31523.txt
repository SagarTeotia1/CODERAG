CONT Handbook 2009 for new Members of the Committee on Budgetary Control The handbook 2009 for new members of the Committee on Budgetary Control is now available in all languages : The first part of the handbook deals with general issues; the second part of the handbook sets out some of the highlights of the work of CONT during the sixth parliamentary term 2004-2009; the third part of the handbook (annexes) lists some of the other activities of the Commitee over the last parliamentary term. This handbook includes links to important documents referred to in the text .
