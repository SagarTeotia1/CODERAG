Answer given by Mr Tajani on behalf of the Commission (18 February 2009) The Intelligent Transport Systems (ITS) Action Plan issued by the Commission on 16 December 2008 Communication from the Commission — Action Plan for the deployment of the Intelligent Transport Systems in Europe — COM(2008)886 final. will run until 2014. The Commission will report on the progress in the implementation of this Action Plan in 2012. This report will also review and, if necessary, extend the priority areas as well as the scope of the actions. However, the present Action Plan already contains several actions related to ITS in urban areas, public transport and vulnerable road users as mentioned by the Honourable Member.
