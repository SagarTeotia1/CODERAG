WRITTEN QUESTION E-3517/09 by Robert Kilroy-Silk (NI) to the Commission (13 May 2009) Subject: Torture in the UAE What representations has the Commission made to the Government of the UAE following the allegation that Issa bin Zayed al Nahyar tortured businessman Mohammed Shat Pow, who allegedly had sand thrust down his throat, bullets fired near his feet, was beaten with a plank of wood with nails in it, had salt rubbed into his wounds, had an electric cattle prod used on his genitals and was set alight?
