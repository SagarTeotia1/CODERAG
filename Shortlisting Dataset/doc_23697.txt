At this first plenary session of the assembly, which was set up in November 2006, parliamentarians will discuss and vote on three reports on the following subjects: EU-Latin America relations with a view to the fifth Lima summit in 2008 (summit of presidents and heads of government of the countries of the EU, Latin America and the Caribbean); economic and trade relations; sustainable development and global warming. The votes on these reports will take place on Thursday 20 December (morning).
