Commission also suggested a new partnership between industry, trade unions and Member States in connection with the CARS 21 process to accompany the common crisis response. The Initiative for Investment and Employment (‘Iniciativa para o Investimento e Emprego’ (IIE)) approved by the Portuguese Government on 19 January 2009 includes several measures to address the current situation, including measures to increase labour demand and income support policies. The IEFP (Institute for Employment and Vocational Training) is the main deliverer of these measures, which it does through its local branches (employment centres and vocational training centres).
