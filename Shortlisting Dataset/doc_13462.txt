AFCO Lobby report adopted The report by Alexander Stubb on the framework for the activities of interest representatives (lobbyists) was adopted on 1st of April by 18 positive votes, 1 vote against and 3 abstentions. The report stresses that lobbying must be transparent and takes a positive standpoint towards a common lobbyists' register of all the European institutions.
