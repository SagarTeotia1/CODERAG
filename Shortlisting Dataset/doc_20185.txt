ORAL QUESTION H-0186/07 for Question Time at the part-session in April 2007 pursuant to Rule 109 of the Rules of Procedure by Philip Bushill-Matthews to the Council Subject: Provision of child care Following the Barcelona European Council in 2002, when Member States agreed that by 2010 child care should be available to at least 90% of children between 3 and 6 years old, and at least 33% of children under 3 years of age, and in light of the new Commission communication on demographic changes within Europe, can the Council report on how close different Member States are to achieving these targets?
