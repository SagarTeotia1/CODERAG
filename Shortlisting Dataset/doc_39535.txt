Answer given by Mr Å pidla on behalf of the Commission (11 June 2007) The Commission wishes to inform the Honourable Member that it is actively examining the conformity of the national legislation adopted in Ireland which transposes the two anti-discrimination Directives (2000/43/EC Council Directive 2000/43/EC of 29 June 2000 implementing the principle of equal treatment between persons irrespective of racial or ethnic origin, OJ L 180, 19.7.2000. and 2000/78/EC Council Directive 2000/78/EC of 27 November 2000 establishing a general framework for equal treatment in employment and occupation, OJ L 303, 2.12.2000. ).
