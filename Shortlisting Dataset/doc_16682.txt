whereas the purpose of Draft amending budget No 7/2009 is to formally enter this budgetary adjustment into the 2009 budget, 1. Takes note of Preliminary draft amending budget No 7/2009, which is the fourth amending budget solely dedicated to the EU Solidarity Fund; 2. Takes note that the funding of the EU Solidarity Fund will be provided by Chapter 1 4 of the Revenue of the General Budget for the European Communities, concerning the Own Resources Based on Gross National Income pursuant to Article 2(1)(c) of Decision 2007/436/EC, EURATOM to finance Chapter 13 06 of the 2009 Budget concerning the Solidarity Fund; 3. Approves Draft amending budget No 7/2009 unamended; 4. Instructs its President to forward this resolution to the Council and the Commission.
