ITRE Workshop on Energy efficiency - Presentations The event, organised by Policy Department A, looked at best practice examples in Members States and the cost of implementation of various measures to improve energy efficiency, focussing mainly on th ree main topics: energy efficiency obligations schemes, the potential, costs and difficulties with the proposed 3% renovation rate for public buildings and the promotion of combined heat and power (CHP).
