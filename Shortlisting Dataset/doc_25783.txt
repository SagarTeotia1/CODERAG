Which Member States have availed themselves of the possibility provided for in Articles 68 and 69 of Regulation (EC) No 73/2009, and to which programmes are they allocating the specific aid? What percentage of the national ceiling is being allocated to programmes under Articles 68 and 69 in each Member State? Which mechanisms have been chosen to gather the financial resources needed for the specific support (use of unused funds, linear reduction in value of payment entitlements)?
