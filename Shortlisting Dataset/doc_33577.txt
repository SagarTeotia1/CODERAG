Quarries and open-cast mining where the surface of the site exceeds 25 hectares, or peat extraction, where the surface of the site exceeds 150 hectares, are listed in Annex I. Other projects of quarries are listed in Annex II (2)(a). Change to or extension of quarries listed in Annex I, where such a change to or extension in itself meets the thresholds set out in Annex I, are set out in Annex I. For such projects, an EIA is carried out. Change or extension of projects of quarries listed in Annex II already authorised, executed or in the process of being executed, which may have significant adverse effects on the environment, are listed in Annex II. For such projects, the screening procedure will determine whether an EIA must be carried out. Council Directive 96/61/EC of 24 September 1996 concerning integrated pollution prevention and control OJ L 257, 10.10.1996 . (IPPC Directive) covers installations for the production of cement clinker in rotary kilns with a production capacity exceeding 500 tonnes per day. According to this directive, operators have to submit an application including a description of the nature and quantities of foreseeable emissions for the installations as well as the identification of significant effects of the emissions on the environment. Competent authorities shall then issue a permit based on best available techniques, taking into account local conditions. Member States shall also ensure that the public concerned are given early and effective opportunities to participate in the procedure for issuing a permit as required in Annex V of the IPPC Directive.
