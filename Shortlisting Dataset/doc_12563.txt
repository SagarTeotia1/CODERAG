Answer given by Mr Almunia on behalf of the Commission (21 December 2007) The Honourable Member will find in the annex sent directly to her and to Parliament's Secretariat the requested data in terms of trade values and quantities. These data cover the year 2006 as well as the first eight months for 2007 (until August). The data cover EU cheese imports from non-EU countries as well as the total of EU cheese import from these countries.
