WRITTEN QUESTION E-6779/08 by Proinsias De Rossa (PSE) to the Commission (19 December 2008) Subject: INTAS programme and FP7 Could the Commission outline how EU research and technological development activities regarding the countries of eastern Europe and central Asia, which were carried out by the International Association for the Promotion of Cooperation with scientists from the New Independent States of the former Soviet Union through the INTAS programme, have been carried over into FP7?
