WRITTEN QUESTION E-2937/06 by Glyn Ford (PSE) to the Commission (30 June 2006) Subject: Free movement of people and the commune of Woluwe Saint Lambert In view of the fact that the above commune is still demanding to see Belgian ID cards from EU citizens which is contrary to the EU law that entered into force on 1 May 2006, will the Commission contact the above commune to ask them to desist?
