Answer given by Mr Potočnik on behalf of the Commission (11 November 2011) The Commission has developed and will continue to develop a number of actions related to marine litter. Reference is made to the joint answer to Questions E‑002302/11 and E‑002368/11 http://www.europarl.europa.eu/QP-WEB which gives an overview of these actions. The Commission has no plans for the collection of litter from the Pacific. As regards to the sorting of wastes, the Commission has been carrying out awareness raising campaigns in all Member States on waste management, in particular landfill of waste and waste shipment, including the aspects of recycling and separate waste collection. The present campaign concentrates on the implementation of Directive 2008/98/EC on waste OJ L 312, 22.11.2008 . and its environmental objectives, to be followed by a public discussion on all aspects of plastic waste in the environment, including options for improving the management of plastic waste.
