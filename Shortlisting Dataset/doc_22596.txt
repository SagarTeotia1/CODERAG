WRITTEN QUESTION E-1781/06 by Proinsias De Rossa (PSE) to the Council (28 April 2006) Subject: Forced prostitution What action is the Council Presidency taking in response to the European Parliament's resolution on forced prostitution in the context of world sports events ( P6_TA-PROV(2006)0086 ) of 15 March, most notably paragraph 4, which urged the Commission and the Member States to launch a Europe-wide campaign during international sports events to inform and educate about the scale of the problem of forced prostitution and trafficking in human beings and to seek to curb demand by raising awareness among potential clients, and paragraph 5, which called on the Commission and the Member States to launch a prevention campaign targeting potential victims and informing them of the risks and dangers of becoming caught up in human trafficking networks and also providing them with information as to their rights and where they can obtain assistance in countries of destination?
