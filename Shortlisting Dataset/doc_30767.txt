WRITTEN QUESTION E-0509/05 by Geoffrey Van Orden (PPE-DE) and Nirj Deva (PPE-DE) to the Commission (15 February 2005) Subject: Assistance to the Maldive Islands What assessment has the Commission made of the damage and casualties sustained by the Maldive Islands during the recent tsunami, and what efforts are being made to assist the Maldives in its subsequent relief and reconstruction programme, given the serious damage to key infrastructure? What efforts are being made to link such assistance to the encouragement of early democratic reform?
