Question for written answer E-7229/2010 to the Commission Rule 117 Mara Bizzotto (EFD) (15 September 2010) Subject: Crisis and medium-sized enterprises in Portugal In the light of the economic and financial crisis that has deeply affected Europe since late 2007, and bearing in mind the role of medium-sized enterprises — as defined in the recommendation of 6 May 2003 OJ L 124, 20.5.2003, p. 36 . — in the economic fabric of the EU, can the Commission say how many bankruptcy proceedings have been initiated in respect of medium-sized enterprises in Portugal since 2007? Over the same period, what percentage of medium-sized enterprise failures can be attributed to globalisation, and what percentage to structural problems in the country’s economy and production system? What is the total number of jobs lost, and what impact has the failure of medium-sized enterprises had on GDP growth?
