IMCO Toy Safety Directive: around 500 amendments to be voted on On 6 November 2008, IMCO Members will vote around 500 amendments tabled to the draft Toy Safety Directive. The most controversial issues include: requirements for chemical substances used in toys, allergenic fragrances , choking hazards and the rules on warnings to be put on toys and their packaging. 20081103CAN41196 Read the Draft Report (Marianne Thyssen) and Amendments document
