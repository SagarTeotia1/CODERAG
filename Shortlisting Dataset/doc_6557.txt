European Parliament and Council decision modifying Decision No 163/2001/EC of the European Parliament and of the Council of 19 January 2001 on the implementation of a training programme for professionals in the European audiovisual programme industry (MEDIA-Training) (2001-2005) ( COM(2003) 188 – C5-0176/2003 – 2003/0064(COD)) Doc.: A5-0027/2004 Procedure : Codecision (1st reading) & Valter VELTRONI (PES, I) Report on the proposal for a European Parliament and Council decision modifying Council Decision 2000/821/EC of 20 December 2000 on the implementation of a programme to encourage the development, distribution and promotion of European audiovisual works (MEDIA Plus - Development, Distribution and Promotion) ( COM(2003) 191 – C5-0177/2003 – 2003/0067(COD)) Doc.: A5-0028/2004 Procedure : Codecision (1st reading) (without debate) Vote : 12.02.2004 Vote By adopting two reports, from Valter VELTRONI (PES, I) and Gianni VATTIMO (PES, I), (the latter by 409 votes in favour to 14 against with 16 abstentions) Parliament approved the Commission's proposal to extend in their current form the MEDIA Plus and MEDIA Training programmes by one year to 31 December 2006. They are currently run from 2001 to 2005. As well as the budgetary changes proposed by the Commission, MEPs voted to increase the budget for MEDIA Plus by an additional €18 million and that for MEDIA Training by €2 million, reflecting the revision of the financial perspectives in the light of enlargement.
