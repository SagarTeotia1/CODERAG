WRITTEN QUESTION E-5597/07 by Jules Maaten (ALDE) to the Council (14 November 2007) Subject: Death sentence handed down by Iranian court to Dutch human rights activist Abdullah al‑Mansouri With reference to Written Question E‑3983/06 of 21 September 2006 (extradition of Dutch human rights activist to Iran), I should like to put some further questions to the Council: 1. Is the Council aware of the death sentence passed on the Dutch human rights activist Abdullah al‑Mansouri by an Iranian court? 2. What will the Council do about this?
