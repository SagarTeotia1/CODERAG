Reply (9 March 2007) On 15 November, the European Commission presented to the Council and the European Parliament its ‘White Paper on enhancing the Single Market Framework for Investment Funds’ (COM(2006)686 final + Annexes — SEC(2006)1451 and SEC(2006)1452). This document, which clarifies the current views and intentions of the Commission on harmonised investment funds (UCITS) as well as non-harmonised investment funds (including hedge funds), is currently under consideration in the Council, with a view to the adoption of Council conclusions at the May Ecofin meeting, as provisionally scheduled under the Council Presidency's Ecofin programme See Document 17082/06 Ecofin 484 of 22 December 2006. .
