Answer given by Mr Almunia on behalf of the Commission (17 April 2008) Concerning the Honourable Member's first two questions, on the appropriate accounting treatment of accumulated interest and corresponding expenditure which occur with a delay, such flows should be recorded according to the accrual principle, as set out in the European system of accounts (ESA 95). Concerning the Honourable Member's last two questions, interest earnings and interest payments are covered both through regular dialogue visits and in the context of transmissions of Excessive Deficit Procedure (EDP) notifications, in the framework of Council Regulation (EC) No 3605/93 as amended, via the EDP notification tables and requests for clarifications from Eurostat to Member States. The EDP notification tables are published on the website of Eurostat.
