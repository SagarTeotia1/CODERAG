Reply (6 December 2010) The Honourable Member is hereby informed that the Council has not discussed the possibility of amending Regulation No 422/67/EEC Regulation No 422/67/EEC, 5/67/Euratom of the Council of 25 July 1967 determining the emoluments of the President and members of the Commission and of the President, Judges, Advocates–General and Registrar of the Court of Justice, OJ 187, 8.8.1967 , pp. 1–5. . As for the Honourable Member's last question, the Council would point out that it is not its role to interpret Article 7(2) of the above regulation.
