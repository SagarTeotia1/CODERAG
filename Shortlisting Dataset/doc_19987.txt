Question for written answer E-5382/2010 to the Commission Rule 117 Zbigniew Ziobro (ECR) (13 July 2010) Subject: The oil spill in the Gulf of Mexico and European safety measures The oil spill from the BP-run Deepwater Horizon platform in the Gulf of Mexico has exposed quality control deficiencies in the safety systems and procedures used on such installations as well as the inadequacy of environmental liability legislation. Has the Commission conducted an analysis of this incident and of the relevant regulations in force in Member States and at Community level? In the Commission's opinion, are these regulations adequate to deal with any incidents involving oil spilling into the environment?
