P5_TA(2001)0483 Substances that deplete the ozone layer (4th amendment to Montreal Protocol) * (procedure without debate) A5-0294/2001 European Parliament legislative resolution on the proposal for a Council decision concerning the conclusion of the fourth amendment to the Montreal Protocol on substances that deplete the ozone layer ( COM(2001) 249 - C5-0251/2001 - 2001/0101(CNS) ) (Consultation procedure) The European Parliament, - having regard to the proposal for a Council decision ( COM(2001) 249 ) OJ C 213 E, 31.7.2001, p. 251. , - having regard to the fourth amendment to the Montreal Protocol on substances that deplete the ozone layer, - having regard to Articles 174(4), 133 and 300(2), first subparagraph of the EC Treaty, - having been consulted by the Council pursuant to Article 300(3), first subparagraph of the EC Treaty ( C5-0251/2001 ), - having regard to Rules 67 and 97(7) of its Rules of Procedure, - having regard to the report of the Committee on the Environment, Public Health and Consumer Policy ( A5-0294/2001 ), 1. Approves the conclusion of the fourth amendment to the Montreal Protocol; 2. Instructs its President to forward its position to the Council and Commission.
