WRITTEN QUESTION E-2341/08 by Georgios Papastamkos (PPE‑DE) to the Commission (23 April 2008) Subject: Repayment by the Member States of unjustifiably disbursed appropriations under the CAP 1. Where does Greece rank among the countries from which the Commission is requesting the repayment of appropriations unjustifiably disbursed under the common agricultural policy? 2. Can the Commission specify the reasons why it considered the amounts to be unjustifiably disbursed? What are the reasons in the case of Greece? 3. Does the Commission have comparative data on the ‘performances’ of the Member States over the last five-year period?
