Question for written answer E-8548/2010 to the Commission Rule 117 Liam Aylward (ALDE) (20 October 2010) Subject: EU funding programmes Could the Commission supply a detailed list of all funding that is available to local and regional authorities through programmes and classifications? Is there an information centre where local and regional authorities as well as organisations can access information about the funding they can receive, the amount they are entitled to and the programmes and the criteria that apply to them?
