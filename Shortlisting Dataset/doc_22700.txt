P5_TA(2002)0515 Trans-European energy networks ***I A5-0324/2002 European Parliament legislative resolution on the proposal for a European Parliament and Council decision amending Decision No 1254/96/EC laying down a series of guidelines for trans-European energy networks ( COM(2001) 775 – C5-0111/2002 – 2001/0311(COD)) (Codecision procedure: first reading) The European Parliament , – having regard to the Commission proposal to the European Parliament and the Council ( COM(2001) 775 OJ C 151 E, 25.6.2002, p. 207. ), – having regard to Article 251(2) and Article 156 of the EC Treaty, pursuant to which the Commission submitted the proposal to Parliament ( C5-0111/2002 ), – having regard to Rule 67 of its Rules of Procedure, – having regard to the report of the Committee on Industry, External Trade, Research and Energy and the opinion of the Committee on Economic and Monetary Affairs ( A5-0324/2002 ), 1. Approves the Commission proposal as amended; 2. Asks to be consulted again should the Commission intend to amend the proposal substantially or replace it with another text; 3. Instructs its President to forward its position to the Council and Commission.
