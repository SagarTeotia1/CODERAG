2004/C 782 E/026 WRITTEN QUESTION E-1232/03 by Nicholas Clegg (ELDR) to the Commission (2 April 2003) Subject: Member States' policies on airport expansion Could the Commission provide a summary of the principal airport expansion plans in Member States of which it is aware, and give its opinion on whether it considers that the plans are: 1. sufficiently co-ordinated across the EU; 2. adequate to deal with the projected increase in air passenger demand over the next 40 years?
