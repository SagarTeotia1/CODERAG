WRITTEN QUESTION E-5872/06 by Emine Bozkurt (PSE) and Jean Lambert (Verts/ALE) to the Commission (12 January 2007) Subject: Age discrimination in goods and services Age discrimination in goods, facilities and services is a topic not yet attended to by the EU. 1. What concrete actions is the European Commission going to take in the year of equal opportunities 2007 to further stimulate the debate on how to combat age discrimination in goods, facilities and services? 2. Would the Commission consider proposing a directive on age discrimination in goods and services, given that many elderly people as well as young people suffer from age discrimination?
