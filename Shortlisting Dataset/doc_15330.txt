WRITTEN QUESTION E-1149/10 by Iva Zanicchi (PPE) to the Council (4 March 2010) Subject: Protection of children who have disappeared in Haiti Amnesty International and other humanitarian organisations have recently reported the disappearance of several dozens of children who had survived the tragic earthquake in Haiti. An appeal has been launched to the international community to pay due heed to the serious risks of child abuse and trafficking. What measures have been taken, or does the Council intend to take, to monitor and protect the children who survived the earthquake in Haiti?
