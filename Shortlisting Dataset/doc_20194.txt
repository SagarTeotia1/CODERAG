Question for written answer E-003657/2011 to the Commission Rule 117 Daniël van der Stoep (NI) and Barry Madlener (NI) (13 April 2011) Subject: The bankruptcy of multiculturalism (follow-up question) On 30 March 2011, Mrs Vassiliou, on behalf of the Commission, answered Question E‑001386/2011 concerning the bankruptcy of multiculturalism. 1. Is the Commission aware of Question E‑001386/2011 and its answer to it? 2. Is the Commission aware that the form taken by multiculturalism in the EU is now determined, inter alia , by the current European rules on immigration and asylum? If not, why not? 3. Will the Commission shoulder its shared responsibility for encouraging or limiting multiculturalism in the EU, vested in it by the Lisbon Treaty and European legislation on immigration and asylum? If not, why not? 4. Can the Commission indicate exactly when it believes that multiculturalism has succeeded and when it has failed, and, from this point of view, state its assessment of the current state of multiculturalism in the EU?
