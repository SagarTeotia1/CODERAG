Securing supply of rare earth elements MEPs demanded Commission to ensure that EU is not hurt by imminent shortage of imported rare earth elements (REE), essential for green, medical and military technologies. Responsibility and solidarity There were they key concepts during Wednesday's address of the president of Hungarian republic to the Plenary. The former MEP summed up the priorities of HU presidency and highlights the EP as 'soul of the EU.' 2 in 1 MEPs adopted on Wednesday the schedule of Parliament's plenary sessions for 2012 and 2013. By secret ballot, MEPs adopted amendments to the calendars so that two sessions in Strasbourg will be held during a single calendar week in October. 11th March: European Day on Remembrance of Victims of Terrorism On Thursday, the Parliament observed a minute of silence in memory of the victims of terrorism. President Buzek said: "no terrorist or any criminal organization is able to weaken our faith in the values which built our united Europe". Volcanic Ash crisis MEPs asked the Commission about the measures foreseen to avoid another crisis like the one caused by the eruption of an Icelandic volcano on May last year. The economic consequences of the crisis, passengers'' safety, better coordination and forecasting mechanisms were some the issues mentioned during the debate.
