Question for written answer E-5457/2010 to the Commission Rule 117 Martin Ehrenhauser (NI) (13 July 2010) Subject: Number of officials in the various grades according to nationality All of the rights and obligations of a European Union official are laid down in the Staff Regulations of Officials of the European Communities. Council Regulation (EU, Euratom) No 1296/2009 OJ L 348, 29.12.2009, p. 10 . of 23 December 2009 lays down, based on the Staff Regulations of Officials of the European Communities, the current basic monthly salaries for each grade and step with effect from 1 July 2009. Officials in the administrators' function group (AD) may be graded during their career in grades 5 to 16. 1. How many Commission officials, according to nationality by EU Member State, are to be found in which grade of the administrators' function group (AD 5 to 16) (for example: 15 Austrians in AD 12, etc)?
