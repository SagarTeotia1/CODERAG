ATTENDANCE REGISTER EUROPEAN PARLIAMENT 2004 - 2005 SESSION Sittings of 10 to 13 January 2005 STRASBOURG MINUTES IN THE CHAIR: Josep BORRELL FONTELLES President 1 Resumption of session The sitting opened at 17.05. 2 Statement by the President The President made a statement on the tsunami in Asia. The House observed a minute's silence in memory of the victims of the tsunami. The President announced that, on behalf of Parliament, he had sent a message to the Jakarta Summit, guaranteeing that funds would be swiftly made available: the Committee on Budgets would make a decision the following day on a transfer of EUR 100 million for aid. He pointed out that José Manuel Barroso (President of the Commission) had agreed that two Members of the European Parliament should take part in the donors' conference in Geneva. 3 Approval of Minutes of previous sitting Corrections to votes: sitting of 15.12.2004 Giusto Catania report - A6-0067/2004 paragraph 1,
