WRITTEN QUESTION E-1563/08 by Marios Matsakis (ALDE) to the Commission (18 March 2008) Subject: Infections acquired from using contaminated public swimming pools or baths Every year thousands of EU citizens suffer from infections acquired from using contaminated public swimming pools or baths. With summer approaching, will the Commission issue stern advice to EU Member State authorities to follow strictly EU directives and thus prevent many such infections from happening in 2008?
