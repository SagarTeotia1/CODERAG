As regards the free phone, OLAF has informed the Commission that 247 case records have been created as a result of messages received between January 2005 and July 2010 and that the majority of calls do not lead to the creation of case records. The current trend in 2010 is for between 50 and 100 free phone calls to be received per month. Since the Fraud Notification System was set up on 1 March 2010, 235 messages were received. An open invitation to tender was held for the Fraud Notification System.
