CONTENTS Procedural page PARLIAMENT'S RULES OF PROCEDURE PROPOSAL FOR A DECISION EXPLANATORY STATEMENT OPINION OF THE COMMITTEE ON BUDGETARY CONTROL Procedural page At the sitting of 15 March 2001 the President of Parliament announced that she had referred a letter on matters relating to the provisions on the discharge procedure in the Rules of Procedure to the Committee on Constitutional Affairs as the committee responsible and to the Committee on Budgetary Control for its opinion (2001/2060(REG)). At its meeting of 12 November 2001 the Committee on Constitutional Affairs decided to draw up a report and appointed Hans-Peter Martin rapporteur. It considered the draft report at its meetings of 18 June 2002, 15 July 2002 and 12 September 2002. At the last meeting it adopted the proposal for a decision by 25 votes to 1 with 0 abstentions. The following were present for the vote: Giorgio Napolitano, chairman; Jo Leinen and Ursula Schleicher, vice-chairmen; Hans-Peter Martin, rapporteur; Teresa Almeida Garrett, Margrietus J. van den Berg, (for Enrique Barón Crespo), Georges Berthu, Jean-Louis Bourlanges, Richard Corbett, Jean-Maurice Dehousse, Gianfranco Dell'Alba (for Olivier Dupuis), Giorgos Dimitrakopoulos, Andrew Nicholas Duff, Lone Dybkjær, José María Gil-Robles Gil-Delgado, Sylvia-Yvonne Kaufmann, Neil MacCormick (for Johannes Voggenhuber), Hanja Maij-Weggen, Iñigo Méndez de Vigo, Gérard Onesta, Jacques F.
