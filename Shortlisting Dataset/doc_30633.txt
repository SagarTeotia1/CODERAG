The confiscation and recovery of assets acquired through criminal activities is an essential element of successfully fighting organised crime. Council Decision 2007/845/JHA OJ L 332, 18.12.2007 . provides that Member States should set up or designate, by 18 December 2008 a national Asset Recovery Office in their country. An update of this information will be provided to the Honourable Member in early 2009. The Commission confirms that it will prepare an implementation report by December 2010 and that for the time being there are no plans to put forward proposals to regulate the administration of assets confiscated from criminal organisations.
