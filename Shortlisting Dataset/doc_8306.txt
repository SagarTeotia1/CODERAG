The situation in delegations is more problematic: despite a slight increase over the past few years, only 8.3 % of women currently occupy management posts. The fourth Action Programme explicitly provides for measures for the Relex family of Directorates-General (DGs) DG External Relations (RELEX), DG Development (DEV), EuropeAid Coperation Office (AIDCO), the European Community Humanitarian Office (ECHO), DG Enlargement (ELARG) and DG Trade (TRADE). with the objective of improving the gender balance. The special situation of these DGs will need to be considered carefully in the fifth Action Programme.
