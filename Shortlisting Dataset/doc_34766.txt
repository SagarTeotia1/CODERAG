DRAFT LEGISLATIVE RESOLUTION European Parliament legislative resolution on the proposal for a Council decision on providing further supplementary-financial assistance to Moldova ( COM(2002) 538 – C5‑0484/2002 – 2002/0236(CNS)) (Consultation procedure) The European Parliament , – having regard to the Commission proposal to the Council ( COM(2002) 538 OJ C not yet published. ), – having been consulted by the Council pursuant to Article 308 of the EC Treaty ( C5‑0484/2002 ), – having regard to Rule 67 and Rule 158 (1) of its Rules of Procedure, – having regard to the report of the Committee on Industry, External Trade, Research and Energy ( A5‑0411/2002 ), 1. Approves the Commission proposal; 2. Calls on the Council to notify Parliament should it intend to depart from the text approved by Parliament; 3. Asks to be consulted again should the Council intend to amend the Commission proposal substantially; 4. Instructs its President to forward its position to the Council and Commission.
