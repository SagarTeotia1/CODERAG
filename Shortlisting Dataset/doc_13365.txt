AFET Ban Ki-moon in AFET On 19 October 2010, the Committee on Foreign Affairs in association with the Committee on Development, the Subcommittee on Human Rights and the Subcommittee on Security and Defence and in the presence of Members of the Committee on the Environment, Public Health and Food Safety and the Committee on Women's Rights and Gender Equality will hold an exchange of views with Ban Ki-moon, UN Secretary General. The exchange of views will take place from 017.00 to 18.00 and in LOW N 1.4 in Strasbourg. 20101018CAN87790
