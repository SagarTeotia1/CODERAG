Answer given by Mr Potočnik on behalf of the Commission (22 December 2011) The Honourable Member refers to the compliance of the municipal council of Benicàssim with the Aarhus convention Convention on access to information, public participation in decision-making and access to justice in environmental matters was adopted on 25 June 1998 in Aarhus. . Concerning the question on access to environmental information, Directive 2003/4/EC Directive 2003/4/EC of the Parliament and the Council of 28 January 2003 on public access to environmental information and repealing Council Directive 90/313/EEC, OJ L 41, 14.2.2003. implements the access to the information ‘pillar’ of the Aarhus Convention. Under the directive public authorities are required to provide environmental information upon request, subject to certain conditions. In cases where the provisions concerning access to information were not respected, the public is entitled to seek administrative and judicial review of the decision following national procedures. From the indications provided by the Honourable Member the Commission is not in a position to comment on a possible breach of the requirements under Directive 2003/4/EC. In order to allow the Commission to look into this matter further, it would welcome receiving additional information.
