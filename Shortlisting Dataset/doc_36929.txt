WRITTEN QUESTION E-2528/08 by Marios Matsakis (ALDE) to the Commission (29 April 2008) Subject: Illegal tiger hunting in India Despite the outlawing of tiger hunting in India, home to nearly half of the world's remaining wild tigers, these animals are seriously threatened by poaching and the destruction of their habitat. What is the Commission doing to help India maintain its biodiversity, tackle the illegal trade in wildlife in particular and combat the trade in tiger skin, bones and other body parts?
