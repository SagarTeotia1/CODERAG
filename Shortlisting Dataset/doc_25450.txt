27.9.2010 B7-00534/2010 MOTION FOR A RESOLUTION pursuant to Rule 120 of the Rules of Procedure on a European Union publicity campaign Salvador Garriga Polledo PE446.625v01-00 B7‑00534/2010 Motion for a European Parliament resolution on a European Union publicity campaign The European Parliament , – having regard to Rule 120 of its Rules of Procedure, A. whereas the European Union is in danger of being overtaken by its international competitors, B. whereas in the eyes of the world, Europe suffers from significant weaknesses, C. whereas from outside we are seen as an old continent which finds it very difficult to innovate, D. whereas the Lisbon Agenda, which sought to make Europe the world’s most competitive continent, has failed in this endeavour, 1. Calls for the European Union to present a new image to the world, which at present considers it outmoded and obsolete; 2. Calls for it to do so through a massive publicity campaign which will show the world its successes and achievements; 3. Calls for Community economic operators to conduct this campaign, which will aim to restore Europe to its rightful position.
