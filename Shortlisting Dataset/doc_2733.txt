Answer given by Mrs Kroes on behalf of the Commission (7 July 2005) The Commission has not received specific notifications concerning state aid to Ferguson Shipbuilders in Port Glasgow, Scotland. However, the United Kingdom operated several schemes for state aid to shipbuilding until the expiry of operating aid by 31 December 2000 See Council Regulation (EC) No 1540/98 of 29 June 1998 establishing new rules on aid to shipbuilding, ‘Shipbuilding Regulation’, OJ L 202, 18.7.1998. : the Shipbuilding Intervention Fund, Shipbuilders’ Relief, Export Credit Guarantees and Regional Selective Assistance. Furthermore, the United Kingdom continued to operate the Home Shipbuilding Credit Guarantee Scheme (HSCGS) until the end of the financial year of 2004/2005. Shipyards in the United Kingdom were eligible to apply for aid under this scheme before the United Kingdom authorities.
