LIBE Joint LIBE/JURI meeting on Justice Programme for 2014-2020 In the joint meeting of LIBE and JURI Committees on 28.02., Paraskevi MICHOU, Director for Civil Justice of the Commission gave a presentation on Justice Programme for 2014-2020 (2011/0369(COD), Rapporteurs Philip CLAEYS (NI, BE) and Luigi BERLINGUER (S&D, IT)). In the LIBE meeting that followed, she continued with a presentation on Establishing the Rights and Citizenship Programme 2014-2020 (Rapporteur Kinga GÖNCZ (S&D, HU)).
