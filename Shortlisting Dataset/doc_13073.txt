PECH New Fisheries Partnership Agreement with Guinea-Bissau During the meeting of 23 January, Luis Manual Capoulas Santos presented his draft report on the conclusion of a new fisheries agreement with Guinea-Bissau for duration of four years. This agreement repeals and replaces the agreement, which entered into force on 29 August 1980 and was one of the oldest agreements signed by the Community. The vote will take place during the meeting of 27-28 February 2008. 20080129CAN19860 OEIL: dossier CNS/2007/0209 Draft report
