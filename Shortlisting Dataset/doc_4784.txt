He received 450 votes in favour (65.31 per cent of the valid votes). The 61-year-old German Christian-Democrat will lead the European Parliament for two and a half years until the next European elections in June 2009. Mr Poettering said: "The dignity of man, respect of law, and solidarity of the European peoples will be the principles of my engagement. Mr Poettering will deliver his programme for his presidency in Strasbourg on 13 February at 10.00 am.
