Question for written answer E-002458/2011 to the Council Rule 117 Martin Ehrenhauser (NI) (14 March 2011) Subject: Council positions on press articles In its answer to Question E‑011249/2010, the Council stated ‘it is Council policy not to comment on press articles.’ 1. Since when has the Council been adhering to this information policy? 2. Has the Council had any adverse experiences involving comment on press articles? If so, what? 3. Has the Council had any positive experiences involving comment on press articles? If so, what? 4. Why is it Council policy not to comment on press articles? 5. What disadvantages does the Council see this policy as having? 6. Does the Council intend to review its information policy?
