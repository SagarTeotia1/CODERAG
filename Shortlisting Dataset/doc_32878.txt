P5_TA(2002)0259 Community Environment Action Programme (2001-2010) ***III A5-0170/2002 European Parliament legislative resolution on the joint text approved by the Conciliation Committee for a European Parliament and Council decision on the Sixth Community Environment Action Programme (PE-CONS 3618/1/2002 – C5-0171/2002 – 2001/0029(COD)) (Codecision procedure: third reading) The European Parliament , – having regard to the joint text approved by the Conciliation Committee (PE-CONS 3618/1/2002 – C5-0171/2002 ), – having regard to its position at first reading OJ C 47 E, 21.2.2002, p. 113. on the Commission proposal to Parliament and the Council ( COM(2001) 31 OJ C 154 E, 29.5.2001, p. 218. ), – having regard to its position at second reading P5_TA(2002)0007. on the Council common position OJ C 4, 7.1.2002, p. 52. , – having regard to the Commission's opinion on Parliament's amendments to the common position ( COM(2002) 84 - C5-0057/2002 ), – having regard to Article 251(5) of the EC Treaty, – having regard to Rule 83 of its Rules of Procedure, – having regard to the report of its delegation to the Conciliation Committee ( A5-0170/2002 ), 1. Approves the joint text; 2. Instructs its President to sign the act with the President of the Council pursuant to Article 254(1) of the EC Treaty; 3. Instructs its Secretary-General duly to sign the act and, in agreement with the Secretary-General of the Council, to have it published in the Official Journal of the European Communities; 4. Instructs its President to forward this legislative resolution to the Council and Commission.
