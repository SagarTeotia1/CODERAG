The Council has not been seized with proposals to extend the management of the Agency to other large-scale IT systems in addition to SIS II, the VIS and Eurodac. Although from a strictly technical point of view the data held in each of these systems could be linked, legally this has not been regulated. As regards the costs, the Honourable Member is invited to address the Commission with this particular aspect of his question. The question of staffing is still under discussion, so no further concrete figures can be given than those contained in the initial Commission's proposal 11722/09. . As regards the Management Board, the Commission proposal provides that it is to be composed of one representative of each Member State and two representatives of the Commission. Associated countries will also each have one representative.
