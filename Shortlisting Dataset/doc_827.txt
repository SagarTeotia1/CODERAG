FEMM The face of female poverty in the European Union - 29 November The draft report is a succession of the public hearing held on 28 September on how poverty affects women in the EU. The Rapporteur examines the issue from the angles of feminization of poverty; labour policies and social protection; reconciliation of work and family life; poverty among elderly women; and the link between poverty and gender violence. The draft report emphasises the importance of social dialogue and the role of civil society in tackling the problem and calls for proper funding targeted to combat poverty. 20101123CAN98908 Draft report
