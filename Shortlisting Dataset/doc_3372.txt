WRITTEN QUESTION E-4087/07 by Konstantinos Hatzidakis (PPE-DE) to the Commission (3 August 2007) Subject: Unfair commercial practices on electronic markets To what extent do enterprises engage in unfair commercial practices towards consumers and other businesses on electronic markets in each Member State? What are the most widespread unfair commercial practices? What steps will the Commission take to prevent such practices, thereby ensuring greater protection for the consumers and businesses affected by them?
