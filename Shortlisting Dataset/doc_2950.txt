In addition to the abovementioned legislative amendments, the Commission works closely with the Member States to adapt the operational programmes, where justified, to the new challenges and assists the Member States in simplifying the systems for management and implementation. For example, a number of seminars and trainings are organised with the programme authorities in order to help Member States setting up systems for a simplified reimbursement of eligible expenditure on the basis of the indirect costs, lump sums and flat rates.
