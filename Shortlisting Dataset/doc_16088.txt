AFET Exchange of views with Gérard Longuet, Minister of Defence of France 24 October 2011 (Strasbourg): French Defence Minister Gérard Longuet will meet the Committee on Foreign Affairs and the Subcommittee on Security and Defence to discuss current developments in European defence policy, such as the impact of budgetary austerity, or the NATO operation in Libya.
