Considers that the notion of sustainable development must not be overburdened with a series of demands which would render it impracticable and that in order for sustainable development to be achieved, the idea of economic development must be accepted; 16. Considers that the drive towards sustainable development necessitates strong coordination mechanisms in order to ensure the inclusion of local decision making but also in order to establish effective points of contact between local and global decision making; Economic Policy 17. Considers that the economic and social policies of the Member States need to be strengthened so that the European Union can be in a position to fight the tendency towards recession effectively; 18. Underlines nevertheless that, while this obviously means greater cooperation between the Member States, the definition and putting in place of economic policies, particularly as regards budgetary matters, must not be transferred to Community level; Foreign Affairs 19. Supports the proposal to set up a Euro-Mediterranean Development Bank which can support investment in the southern Mediterranean area and hopes that the Council in Seville will be able to take decisions in this regard; 20. Supports the efforts of the Palestinian Authority, the State of Israel and the international community in finding a solution to the hostilities in the Middle East, and the proposal for an international peace conference aimed at achieving the establishment of a viable Palestinian State and security for the State of Israel, as recognised by all the Arab countries; Kashmir 21. Calls on both India and Pakistan to exercise maximum restraint along the Line of Control and to consider seriously the various and genuine ongoing international efforts aimed at finding a peace formula acceptable to both sides, and calls for new confidence-building and security measures to be brought forward aimed at promoting dialogue between the parties concerned, including an enhanced role for the UN; 22. Instructs its President to forward this resolution to the Council, the Commission and the Parliaments of the Member States.
