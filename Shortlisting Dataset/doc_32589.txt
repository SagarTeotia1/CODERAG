4.12.2006 PROPOSAL FOR AMENDMENT OF PARLIAMENT'S RULES OF PROCEDURE pursuant to Rule 202 of the Rules of Procedure by Hans-Gert Poettering and Martin Schulz Amendment of Rules 15 and 182(1) Election of Quaestors and committee bureaux PARLIAMENT'S RULES OF PROCEDURE Present text Amendments Amendment 1 Rule 15 After the election of the Vice-Presidents, Parliament shall elect five Quaestors. The Quaestors shall be elected by the same procedure as the Vice-Presidents. After the election of the Vice-Presidents, Parliament shall elect six Quaestors. The Quaestors shall be elected by the same procedure as the Vice-Presidents. Or. de Amendment 2 Rule 182, paragraph 1 1. At the first committee meeting after the election of committee members pursuant to Rule 177, the committee shall elect a bureau consisting of a chairman and one, two or three vice-chairmen who shall be elected in separate ballots. 1. At the first committee meeting after the election of committee members pursuant to Rule 177, the committee shall elect a bureau consisting of a chairman and one, two , three or four vice-chairmen who shall be elected in separate ballots. Or. de Justification With a view to ensuring that parliamentary business can be conducted effectively, the number of office-holders should be brought into line with the substantial increase in the number of Members following the accession of Romania and Bulgaria on 1 January 2007.
