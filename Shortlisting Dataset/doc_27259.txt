ORAL QUESTION H-0310/08 for Question Time at the part-session in May 2008 pursuant to Rule 109 of the Rules of Procedure by Eoin Ryan to the Commission Subject: New EU measures to combat illegal drug importations into the EU Can the European Commission make a statement outlining the new measures it is supporting at present so as to help combat the illegal importation of drugs into the European Union, and can the European Commission outline any new initiatives that it is supporting to help confiscate the assets of criminals within the EU27 zone?
