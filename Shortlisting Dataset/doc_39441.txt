WRITTEN QUESTION E-6420/08 by Ilda Figueiredo (GUE/NGL) to the Commission (28 November 2008) Subject: Community support for Sunviauto Sunviauto — Industria de componentes automóveis, SA, a car component plant based in Pedroso, Vila Nova de Gaia that employs around 750 people, is threatening to make a further 100 workers redundant in a municipality that already has one of the highest rates of unemployment in the country. Can the Commission provide the following information: 1. What Community support has Sunviauto received, and on what conditions? 2. What support is planned to help the car and car components industry, with the aim of protecting jobs with rights?
