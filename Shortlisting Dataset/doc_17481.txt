WRITTEN QUESTION E-2833/05 by Geoffrey Van Orden (PPE-DE) to the Commission (27 July 2005) Subject: Northern Cyprus Written Question E‑1876/05, submitted on 17 May and answered on 4 July, has not been properly answered. What substantive progress has been made in fulfilling Council's commitment set out on 26 April 2004 that it ‘is determined to put an end to the isolation of the Turkish Cypriot community and to facilitate the reunification of Cyprus by encouraging the economic development of the Turkish Cypriot community’? What are the obstacles to further progress, and what steps are being taken to overcome those obstacles?
