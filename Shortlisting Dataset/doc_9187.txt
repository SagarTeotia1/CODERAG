Calls on the EU and Russia to intensify their negotiations on a new partnership and cooperation agreement, and reiterates its strong support for a broad, wide-ranging and legally binding agreement that goes beyond economic co-operation alone and also includes, as integral components, the areas of democracy, the rule of law and respect for fundamental human rights; notes the agreement on the Partnership for Modernisation which should encompass both the economy and society; and supports diversifying the Russian economy and EU-Russia trade relations; calls on the Commission and the Russian Government to develop the Partnership for Modernisation in more detail; underlines the need for swift preparation of a concrete work plan in line with the results achieved so far in the context of the four EU-Russia common spaces; underlines the importance of ensuring the effective functioning of the judiciary and stepping up the fight against corruption; 3.
