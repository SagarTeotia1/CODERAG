WRITTEN QUESTION E-5678/07 by Marco Cappato (ALDE) and Marco Pannella (ALDE) to the Commission (15 November 2007) Subject: Inquiry by the financial police into fraud against the Community in Augusta (Siracusa) amounting to EUR 750 000 According to a report by the press agency AFI on 6 May this year a group of Sicilian businessmen received EUR 750 000 worth of European funding to build a four-star hotel on the seafront at Augusta in the province of Siracusa, but used the grant for other purposes. The total amount of the funding was EUR 2.5 million. Can the Commission say: — whether OLAF is aware of these circumstances; — whether the Italian authorities have requested its intervention; — whether it has launched or intends to launch an inquiry?
