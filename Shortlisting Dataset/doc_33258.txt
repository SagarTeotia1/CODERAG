Four specific programmes are set up as part of the framework programme: The fight against violence and drugs prevention programme, the fundamental rights and citizenship programme, the criminal justice programme and the civil justice programme. The objectives of the programme are derived from the Charter of Fundamental Rights and from EU citizenship and seek to support civil society associations and the fight against racism and xenophobia through a dialogue and by complementing action by Member States, respecting the principle of subsidiarity (Art. 5 of the Treaty).
