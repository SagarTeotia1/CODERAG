Has decided to make appropriations available for the following measures: Establishment plan/upgradings: - 1 A and 3 B posts in the Information Technology and Telecommunications Directorate; - 1 A and 1 B posts for risk prevention and protection at work; however, these appropriations will remain in the reserve pending the submission of a precise job description; points out that this new service will also cover the needs of the Committee of the Regions and the Economic and Social Committee and that further possibilities for interinstitutional cooperation are to be explored; - 2 A and 1 C post in the Legal Service; - 1 C post for the Conciliation Service in the Directorate-General for Committees and Delegations; - 1 A post in the Directorate-General for Press and Information; - 2 LA posts in the Directorate-General for Committees and Delegations for linguistic checking of texts before submission to plenary; - 1 A, 2 B and 2 C posts for the Security Service; however, these appropriations will remain in the reserve pending the submission of a security plan for Parliament; - the upgrading of 135 permanent posts and 11 temporary posts in the Secretariat of Parliament, 12 temporary posts in the political group secretariats, one LA4 post to LA3 in the Distribution Service and one upgrading for the non-attached Members' Secretariat; - the transfer of one temporary A post in the political group secretariats to the Secretariat of Parliament, as the Secretary-General is the Appointing Authority for staff working for the non-attached Members; 16. Has reduced the appropriations in Chapter 11 ("Staff in active employment”) by EUR 2.1 million following the Commission's new parameters for the adjustment of staff salaries for 2001; 17. Believes that there is room for improving the efficiency of the interpretation service with modern information technology; has decided, therefore, that the appropriations against Item 2101 ("Distributed data-processing systems”) should also be used for providing more IT support to facilitate the access of interpreters to information during parliamentary meetings, for the reallocation of interpreters following last-minute programme changes and to contribute to higher-quality work by interpreters; 18.
