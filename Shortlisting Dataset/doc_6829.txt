PECH Vote on new Protocol to the Cape Verde FPA On 31 August, Members of the PECH Committee will vote on the draft report by Pat the Cope GALLAGHER (ALDE, IE) on a new Protocol to the Fisheries Partnership Agreement with the Republic of Cape Verde, due to enter into force on 1st September for a 3-year period. The EU financial contribution over the whole period amounts to 1.3 m€, including 110.000 €/year earmarked as aid to the development of the local fishery sector.
