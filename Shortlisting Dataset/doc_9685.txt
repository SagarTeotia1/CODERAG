ORAL QUESTION H-0847/06 for Question Time at the part-session in October 2006 pursuant to Rule 109 of the Rules of Procedure by Marian Harkin to the Commission Subject: Gender pay gap Will the Commission give its opinion on the recent ruling by the European Court of Justice, C17/05, on 3 October 2006 in B.F. Cadman vs the Health & Safety Executive? In the context of this judgment and the wider context of the gender pay gap, what practical steps is the Commission taking to ensure the reduction of this pay gap?
