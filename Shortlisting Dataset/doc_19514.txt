Question for written answer E-012215/2011 to the Council Rule 117 Niki Tzavela (EFD) (4 January 2012) Subject: Financial discipline in the eurozone According to the decisions taken at the summit of 9 December, any country found to be in breach of financial discipline will automatically face penalties. In view of the above, will the Council say: Does any provision exist for automatic penalties for members of the eurozone which impose policies on other Member States, which, when implemented, prove harmful to those Member States?
