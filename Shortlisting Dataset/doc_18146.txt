What legislative measures in the area of asylum and refugee policy at EU level are currently being discussed or negotiated? 2. Are there/have there been discussions or negotiations on a ‘Dublin III Regulation’? 3. If so, with whom? 4. If so, with what content? 5. What changes will the ‘Dublin III Regulation’ contain? 6. What time frame is envisaged for the ‘Dublin III Regulation’?
