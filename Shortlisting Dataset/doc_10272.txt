ORAL QUESTION H-0592/07 for Question Time at the part-session in September II 2007 pursuant to Rule 109 of the Rules of Procedure by Manuel Medina Ortega to the Council Subject: The Western African neighbourhood On the basis of the commitments undertaken by the European Council of 21 and 22 June on neighbourhood policies and migration control, does the Council believe that it would be feasible to develop a far-reaching neighbourhood policy with the countries of Western Africa which would make it feasible to tackle successfully the problems caused in this region by migratory movements?
