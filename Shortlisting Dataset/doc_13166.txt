WRITTEN QUESTION E-2443/05 by Mario Borghezio (IND/DEM) to the Commission (6 July 2005) Subject: The turban to be brought back in Turkey In view of the fact that the Turkish Prime Minister Mr Erdogan is committed to bringing back the ‘turban’, long since abolished by the Kemalists, can the Commission say whether it does not see this decision as an obstacle to Turkey’s accession to the European Union?
