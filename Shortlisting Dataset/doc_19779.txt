WRITTEN QUESTION E-0426/06 by Paulo Casaca (PSE) to the Commission (7 February 2006) Subject: CITES controlled bird species Will the Commission provide information, broken down by Member State, detailing the numbers of CITES controlled bird species imported into the EU in 2003, 2004 and 2005 under the EU's CITES Regulations (Council Regulation (EC) No 338/97 OJ L 61, 3.3.1997, p. 1. , Commission Regulation (EC) No 1808/2001 OJ L 250, 19.9.2001, p. 1. )? Can the Commission estimate what proportion of these might have been caught in the wild?
