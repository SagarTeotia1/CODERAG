LIBE 30 June: Round table with National Parliaments on the EU borders policy On Monday 30 June 2008, Members of National Parliaments have been invited to hold a joint debate on "Freedom and Security in the integrated management of EU borders - An exchange of views on the new generation Schengen Information System (SISII), Frontex, Eurosur, entry-exit system, and data profiling". This public Round table will take place from 15.00 to 18.30 in the room PHS 3C50 with interpretation in all official languages of the EU (except maltese).
