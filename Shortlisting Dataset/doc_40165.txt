Question for written answer E-012504/2011 to the Commission Rule 117 Sergio Paolo Frances Silvestris (PPE) (9 January 2012) Subject: Disappearance of the Pakistani actress Veena Malik The Pakistani actress Veena Malik, who appeared nude on the cover of the Indian edition of FHM magazine bearing a Pakistani secret service tattoo, and who disappeared on Friday, is said to have returned to her home country hiding under a burqa, according to the Pakistani channel Geo TV. After the photograph of her was published in the December issue of the magazine, Veena Malik received death threats from Pakistani Fundamentalists, accusations from the military and criticism from her own father. Her disappearance was reported last Friday, by the director of the film in which she is acting. In the light of the above, can the Commission: 1. indicate whether it is aware of the events surrounding the Pakistani actress Veena Malik? 2. give a general outline of the situation of women in Pakistan? 3. state what steps it will take with regard to this matter?
