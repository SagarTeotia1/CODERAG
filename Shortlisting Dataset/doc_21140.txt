WRITTEN QUESTION E-3637/08 by Graham Watson (ALDE) to the Commission (30 June 2008) Subject: Global maternal mortality The European Union has rightly committed to the achievement of the Millennium Development Goals. The Community and its Member States are making a substantial contribution to the efforts of the international community. The Commission will be aware that one of the Millennium Development Goals provides specific targets for improving maternal health. What steps is the Commission taking to ensure that all pregnant women and newborn infants have access to a skilled birth attendant and emergency care, when in labour?
