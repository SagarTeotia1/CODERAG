Emphasises and reiterates its commitment to the conclusions of the Helsinki Summit and to strict application of the Copenhagen criteria on respect for human rights in the applicant countries; 4. Calls on the Turkish Government urgently to restore the momentum of reform and democratisation that seemed to emerge just before Turkey was officially recognised as an EU candidate; 5. Calls on the government and political parties of Turkey to take advantage of the current constitutional revision procedure in order to implement, in the spirit of the Helsinki agreements, urgent reforms which would enable the Turkish State to guarantee the essential democratic rights of freedom of opinion and free speech, in keeping with Turkey's undertakings as a country applying for membership of the European Union, and calls furthermore for reforms to make the Turkish judicial system truly independent; 6. Instructs its President to forward this resolution to the Council, the Commission, the Turkish Government and the Turkish Grand National Assembly.
