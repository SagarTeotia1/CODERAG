Question for written answer P-010335/2011 to the Commission Rule 117 Francesco Enrico Speroni (EFD) (9 November 2011) Subject: Legal profession and EU languages Can the Commission say whether lawyers who, under the provisions of Directive 77/249/EEC, intend to participate orally in legal proceedings in a Member State other than that in which they are professionally registered, are required to converse in the language of the place of the proceedings, or may they express themselves in another EU language, using simultaneous interpretation?
