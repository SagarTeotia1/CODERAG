The MIC also established, with the support of the EC Joint Research Centre (JRC), a monitoring and alert tool (MIC Daily) in August 2005, which covers available early warning and alert information in a comprehensive way. It is updated on a daily basis and disseminated to civil protection authorities in the 30 countries participating in the Community civil protection mechanism. The Commission continues refining its internal strategy to further improve its early warning capacity. It is currently preparing a call for tender on civil protection simulation exercises, which will focus on early warning elements for tsunamis and similar disasters in the Mediterranean and Atlantic as one priority for the exercise scenarios.
