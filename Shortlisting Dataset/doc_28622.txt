Reply (8 March 2005) The Council takes note of the points raised by the Honourable Member in his question, and in reply can only confirm the tenor and general thrust of the reply it gave at the October 2004 part-session further to his earlier question on the subject, H-0365/04 . The Council would also remind the Honourable Member that the Presidency has confirmed that, together with the Parliament, it is determined to negotiate the terms of an agreement on the regulations and general conditions governing the performance of the duties of its Members on the basis of Article 190(5) TEC.
