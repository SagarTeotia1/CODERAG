WRITTEN QUESTION E-3544/07 by Robert Kilroy-Silk (NI) to the Commission (11 July 2007) Subject: Member States not helping refugees Following his claim on 6 June 2007 that the Member States were showing a ‘lack of solidarity and of burden sharing’ on the issue of asylum‑seekers and refugees, will the Commissioner for Justice, Freedom and Security: (a) indicate which countries he was referring to, and, (b) what he proposes to do about it?
