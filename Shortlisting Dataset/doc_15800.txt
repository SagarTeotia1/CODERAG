Answer given by Mr Dimas on behalf of the Commission (21 September 2006) The Commission can confirm that it has received four complaints regarding wind farm developments in the United Kingdom. These relate to proposed developments in the following locations: the Isle of Lewis; the Isle of Skye; the vicinity of the River Teith Special Area of Conservation; and offshore at Scarweather Sands, Swansea Bay. With regard to each complaint, the Commission has assessed the information provided and will raise the relevant issues with the national authorities.
