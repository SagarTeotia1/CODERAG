IMCO Hearings in the Internal Market: online gambling and customs cooperation The IMCO Committee Members exchanged views and held a hearing on online gambling in the Internal Market (Rapporteur: Jürgen CREUTZMANN (ALDE, DE)) on 15 June from 15:00 till 18:30. A hearing on modernised customs and the Internal Market (Rapporteur: Matteo SALVINI (EFD, IT)) took place on 16 June from 10:00 till 12:30.
