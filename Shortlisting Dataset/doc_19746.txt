WRITTEN QUESTION E-5328/09 by Proinsias De Rossa (S&D) to the Commission (27 October 2009) Subject: Eels recovery regulation Could the Commission outline the current situation with regard to the communication by the Member States by 31 December 2008 of the national plans required under the regulation, adopted unanimously by the Council in 2007, establishing measures for the recovery of eels (Regulation (EC) No 1100/2007 OJ L 248, 22.9.2007, p. 17. ) and their implementation at the latest by 1 July 2009? To the Commission's knowledge, which Member States are implementing a complete, temporary ban on eel fishing under their national plans?
