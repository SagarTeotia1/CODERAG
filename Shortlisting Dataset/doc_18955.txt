ORAL QUESTION H-0551/03 for Question Time at the part-session in October 2003 pursuant to Rule 43 of the Rules of Procedure by Bill Newton Dunn to the Commission Subject: Subsidies to airlines Why was the French Government allowed by the Commission to give Air France an enormous financial subsidy when its fares remain uncompetitively high - whereas Ryanair, which provides cheap competitive fares which please the public, is investigated by the Commission for receiving alleged unfair subsidies from local airports?
