2004/C 84 E/240 WRITTEN QUESTION E-0315/04 by Michl Ebner (PPE-DE) to the Commission (6 February 2004) Subject: Language programme for minority languages In July 2003 the Commission published a call for proposals (DG EAC/45/03) to prepare the ground for the implementation of the Action Plan on language learning and linguistic diversity OJ C 180, 31.7.2003, p. 34 . . One of the goals explicitly referred to in the call for proposals was to prepare information and to raise awareness on the potential of existing programmes and activities to promote regional and minority languages. Following the closing date for the submission of project proposals at the end of September, seven of the 33 project proposals submitted were approved. Does the Commission believe that the selection of projects corresponds to the goals of the Action Plan?
