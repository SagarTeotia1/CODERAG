Rule 123 Order in the Chamber 1. The President shall call to order any Member who creates a disturbance during the proceedings. 2. Should the offence be repeated, the President shall again call the Member to order, and the fact shall be recorded in the minutes of proceedings. 3. If a further offence is committed, the President may exclude the offender from the Chamber for the remainder of the sitting. The Secretary-General shall see to it that this disciplinary measure is carried out immediately, with the assistance of the ushers and, if necessary, of Parliament's Security Service.
