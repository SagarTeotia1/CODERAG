WRITTEN QUESTION E-1430/06 by Georgios Karatzaferis (IND/DEM) to the Commission (5 April 2006) Subject: Modernisation of the port of Thessaloniki The port of Thessaloniki is the gateway to Macedonia and the whole of northern Greece. Why has a modern goods terminal not been created in that port? Has the Municipality of Thessaloniki requested that the Commission provide co-funding for such a project and, if so, when?
