24 March 2003 FINAL A5-0085/2003 REPORT including a proposal for a recommendation of the European Parliament to the Council on the reform of the Conventions on drugs ( 2003/2015(INI) ) Committee on Citizens' Freedoms and Rights, Justice and Home Affairs Rapporteur: Kathalijne Maria Buitenweg 319.267 CONTENTS PROCEDURAL PAGE PROPOSAL FOR A EUROPEAN PARLIAMENT RECOMMENDATION EXPLANATORY STATEMENT PROPOSAL FOR RECOMMENDATION B5‑0541/2002 PROCEDURAL PAGE At the sitting of 13 January 2003 the President of Parliament announced that he had referred the proposal for a recommendation on the reform of the Conventions on drugs, tabled pursuant to Rule 49 of the Rules of Procedure, to the Committee on Citizens' Freedoms and Rights, Justice and Home Affairs as the committee responsible. At its meeting of 21 January 2003, Committee on Citizens' Freedoms and Rights, Justice and Home Affairs decided to draw up a report on this topic and appointed Kathalijne Maria Buitenweg rapporteur (2003/2015(INI)). The committee considered the draft report at its meetings of 11 February 2003, 18 February 2003 and 19 March 2003 . At the last meeting it adopted the motion for a resolution by 24 votes to 20, with 0 abstentions. The following were present for the vote: Jorge Salvador Hernández Mollar (chairman); Robert J.E.
