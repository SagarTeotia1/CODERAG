Answer given by Mrs Fischer Boel on behalf of the Commission (19 September 2007) Council Regulation (EC) No 2826/2000 Council Regulation (EC) No 2826/2000 of 19 December 2000 on information and promotion actions for agricultural products on the internal market, OJ L 328, 23.12.2000. and Commission Regulation (EC) No 1071/2005 Commission Regulation (EC) No 1071/2005 of 1 July 2005 laying down detailed rules for applying Council Regulation (EC) No 2826/2000 on information and promotion actions for agricultural products on the internal market, OJ L 179, 11.7.2005. lay down the rules on the promotion and information programmes for agricultural products on the internal market. These Regulations foresee EU co-financing by 50 % of the total budget of promotion and information campaigns for agricultural products. The rest of the financing is shared between the Member State and the applicant organisation which must contribute at least 20 % of the total budget of the promotion programme. Member States are responsible for monitoring the programmes and for making the payments in respect thereof (Article 10 of Commission Regulation (EC) No 1071/2005).
