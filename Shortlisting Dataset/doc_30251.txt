Answer given by Ms Hübner on behalf of the Commission (17 August 2006) The super-prefecture Drama-Kavala-Xanthi is eligible for participation in the following Interreg Community initiative for the promotion of cross-border and inter-regional cooperation. Community initiative programmes: — Strand A (cross-border cooperation): Interreg IIIA/Phare cross‑border cooperation Greece- Bulgaria and Greece-Turkey programmes; — Strand B (transnational cooperation): Cadses Central, Adriatic, Danubian and south-east European space. , Archimed Special planning integration in the south-eastern Mediterranean area. and Medocc Programme supporting projects aimed at transnational co-operation in the field of territorial development of western Mediterranean countries. ; — Strand C (interregional cooperation): EAST. The Interreg programmes, as well as the Community support framework (CSF) programmes, are managed in a decentralised way according to Article 8(3) of Council Regulation (EC) No 1260/1999 of 21 June 1999 laying down general provisions on Structural Funds OJ L 161, 26.6.1999 . . Therefore, this kind of question should be addressed to the managing authority of each programme. In the framework of the Community initiative URBAN II (programming period 2000‑06), there are no programmes approved by the Commission in the super-prefecture of Drama-Kavala-Xanthi. As in the case of the other Community Initiative programmes, the EQUAL programmes are also managed in a decentralised way (Article 8 (3) of Council Regulation (EC) No 1260/1999). According to research made in the EQUAL common database (ECDB), around 10 EQUAL projects (development partnerships) involved partners from the region of the super- prefecture of Drama-Kavala-Xanthi. The super prefecture of Drama-Kavala-Xanthi was partner in one 2002 round Development Partnership (Project No 2002 GR-200764) and the prefectures of Drama and Xanthi are partners in two 2005 development partnerships (Projects 2005 GR‑232149 and 2005 GR‑232323 respectively). The responsible managing authority could provide information on the funding received by the super-prefecture of Drama-Kavala-Xanthi and the prefectures of Drama and Xanthi in the specific EQUAL development partnerships.
